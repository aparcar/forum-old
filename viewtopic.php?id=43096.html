I replaced my old router with a (very cheap) Netgear WNR854T - it had everything I needed - an 802.11n to Wired Ethernet to support a small private LAN of wired servers and wireless clients.

I went with Netgear's stock firmware, at first, but its refusal to port-forward, to a different class-C subnet, prompted me to re-configure my DHCP and DNS on my Linux server. This went badly - with thousands of incomprehensible error messages and a >95% failure rate to resolve DNS names... but only from Linux.

After much cursing at utterly opaque documentation, and pursuing many blind alleys, I decided that I most suspected the Netgear firmware was corrupting my DNS traffic.

After installing OpenWRT on the WNR854T, to my joy, my LAN-side DNS worked flawlessly... but I am now faced with another problem: the OpenWRT'd WNR854T does not recognise the WiFi card in my WNR854T... though wi-fi worked flawlessly before flashing with OpenWRT.

I followed the installation instructions here:

http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/netgear/wnr854t

And I have read the section about "Wifi Card" - but am finding its advice difficult to interpret. The linked mwl8k git repository:

http://git.marvell.com/?p=mwl8k.git;a=summary

Seems to have versions for Linux 2.6.32 (same as the kernel on the WNR854T) and 3 rc versions for 2.6.33... but I can't find any install instructions.

I can see recommendations for other "they sound wrong for my hardware" drivers too - though it's not clear if these should work with my standard hardware - or if I'd need to change the Wi-fi component to use them. As I bought the WNR854T for peanuts, I'm reluctant to get involved with upgrading hardware or putting up with flaky drivers... especially as they don't seem to have been touched for 3 years (Is that a bad or good sign?)

So... in short, my questions are:

1. Can I get Wi-Fi working with OpenWRT on my WNR854T using existing available software - without altering hardware?
2. If I can't do this easily, what's the "best" (i.e. will actually work) inexpensive Wi-Fi/Ethernet router that I could buy, reasonably cheaply, to replace my ill advised WNR854T choice?

Thanks in advance...
