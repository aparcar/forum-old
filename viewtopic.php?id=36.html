You will need the tftp2.rar utility to TFTP the new Firmware to yor Linksys

Execute the TFTP utility and set the IP to 192.168.1.1

Unplug the Linksys and by the time you plug it in again, you have 5 seconds or less to press the UPGRADE button in the utility.
