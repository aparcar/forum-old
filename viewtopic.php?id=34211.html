Hi

Stealch wrote:

Device based on broadcom BCM6368 V3.1 chip.

BCM6368 is not supported yet in OpenWrt, but will be soon. But ...

Stealch wrote:

But this device have many good functions, such as 802,1q, L2 management switch and VDSL2 support up to 100Mbit/s downstream to 60Mbit/s Upstream.

The internal ADSL and VDSL modem is currently not supported, and likely will not be for a very long time, or maybe never. There are no open source drivers, and the binary drivers do not work on OpenWrt.


Regards
KM
