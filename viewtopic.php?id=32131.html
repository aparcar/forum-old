Hi humanblackstorm ,dont take my words as an expert ,Im just amature , I had same ur problem couple of times and by trial and error I restored to original firmware,
try what I did.
connect thru wired wit any IP,but lan gateway 192.168.1.1. open cmd window with     ping -t 192.168.1.1  ,connect power but as soon as first LED light appears start multiple pressing on QSS hardware button(this important) for about 10-15 seconds and leave it,in secods it will start to make a very fast flashing (SYS  LED),then open Putty and putt   root@192.168.1.1 thru telnet not ssh,now change password as instructions,but u have to change it to a strong pass not simple one  like admin but a longer password ,dont exit telnet window. now the nice part where u putt this command in the same command window     mtd -r erase rootfs_data  ,it will reboot by itself, then give a time of 2 mins then waaaw it is back to life,..again Im not expert at all, but I will be very happy to hear a good news from u.
