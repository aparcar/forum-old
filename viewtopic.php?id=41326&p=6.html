Hi,ppl.
Some tips about overclocking MR3420v2
AR9341 may proper functionality on 600 or higher frequency. I got the 700MHz on my device, but this need some operation.
1. Stable 600MHz may only with CPU heatsink.
2. Stable 700MHz+ - only with some CPU VRM mod.
The AR9341 may powered by external VRM (mising on board), or internal integrated VRM.
0R resistor is not jumper, its have some resistance, thats produce almost 50mV ripple AC dropout.
Need remove its and solder copper wire jamper, and
Add missing/additional capacitor.

Then need mod u-boot.
I prefer peppe2k u-boot.
https://github.com/pepe2k/u-boot_mod
For AR9341 device with 25MHz crystal (like MR3420V2)
In HEX-editor search line "3C 0C 29 AC 3C 0D 2A 80 " and replace "AC" to "E0" for 600MHz.
"+1 hex" = "+1.25MHz CPU clock".
i.e. 29ACh=535MHZ, 29E0h=600MHz, 2A30h=700MHz
And just flashed the new boot.

Result - almost 350BogoMIPs.

Stability need test in memtester and iperf-mt thru Wi-Fi interface, like iperf -c 192.168.1.1 -P32 -t300
Unstable device rebootet within half of minute.
