Hi Guys,

When I was unbricking my TP-Link WR941ND V5 (FCC ID TE7WR941NXV5) I made a mistake erasing my ART partition, so I can't enable my wireless again. I installed the openWRT for de WR841ND V8 (it's the same hardware), but I can't recovery my ART partition.

Anyone have this model and could make a dump of your partition and send me (attaching here)? With a ART partition dump I'll edit with HEX Editor and change the MAC.

If you don't know how to make it, follow this instruction to export the ART partition:
# cat /proc/mtd

Look for art or ART (Let's say for me it is mtd4) Then make a dump:
# dd if=/dev/mtd4 of=/tmp/art.backup

Copy it to your PC and attach it here.

Thank you so much!!!
