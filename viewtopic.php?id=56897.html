Yes, OpenWrt does use hostapd.
Sometimes only components like wpad, wpa-supplicant,hostapd - see http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/uci/wireless/encryption for feature differences.

Dual Band normally means one of two things in routers:
- one wireless radio that can send/receive in 2.4GHz OR 5GHz
- two wireless radios: one in 2.4GHz the other in 5GHz

You cannot use 1 radio on both frequencies at the same time.
Only with special packets can you bundle the wireless connections in 2.4 and 5GHz to one virtual connection. Since most clients only have 1 radio they do not benefit from this solution(see above).
