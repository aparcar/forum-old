Read the wiki. It contains much useful information.

A starting point may be: http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/start

Be prepared: Learning takes time, depending on your knowledge level and skills.

Regarding your initial problems:
- http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/howto/firstlogin (first telnet, set passwd, then ssh)
- some images come without luci, you have to install it after flashing the firmware. Alternatively you can build your own image with luci included, but that's no easy task with 4MB devices like the MR3040.
