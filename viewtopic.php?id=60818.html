Since you have serial I would try flashing a ubnt-loco-m-xw build either directly or with TFTP recovery.  The Nanostation build (referred to in the first post on this thread) will not work on these models since it is configured for a switch and two Ethernet ports and models like the Airgrid only have one wired direct to the SoC.  The loco build works on Nanostation loco (of course), Nanobeam, and probably also Litebeam and Airgrid.
