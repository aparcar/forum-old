After going through the list of supported hardware twice and looking up the products in google product search, I found only three (3) routers that are listed as supporting Kamikaze, are currently stocked by a supplier, and sell for under $60.

Airlink AR525W, $50

Dell TrueMobile 2300 WX5565D $55 In stock but not sure if this is still in production.

Fon 2201b $36

Buffalo: Currently unavailable in US due to legal injunction.

Unfortunately, most of the router manufacturers have switched to 2M flash.

Does anybody know of additional current production routers that support Kamikaze and sell for $60 or less?
