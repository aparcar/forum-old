Hey guys, just an idea

It takes a few minutes for the reflash to complete.

Instead of guess when it's done, why not flash the DMZ or power LED indicating that the flash is complete? That way the user doesn't have to guess when it's done, or keep checking df. It'd be a simple and reliable indicator for a completed flashing.

Anyone think this is a good idea, or is this just a waste of 3 lines of code?

- flyashi
