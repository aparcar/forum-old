I'm working on project for home control system (lights, doors, rediators etc.)
I have almost made it and decide to share some of my work in case, if someone wanna develop it.

it is all based on leds placed on TP-Link TL-WR740N v4/v5 each led control a relay, you can control easily about 8-9 relays.
Relays are arduino low level triggered relays.

I will post the hardware modification part, it work very stable for over a year.

Comments on above image are writen in Bulgarian, so let me translate it. Leds 1 and 6 use GND for control signal, so for them I use photocouper. GND (it is actualy voltage -) and voltage + are taken directly from router power plug. Screw terminals on right side is 2 for 1, they are totally 12 not 24, just drawing software have this *BUG

From there on you need just to make your own script to make that leds light when u want to have fun
