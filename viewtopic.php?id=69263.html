wave 2 is the latest generation, the next round of replace all your hardware

That the performance degrades over time is a known issue with the new wifi chip in Rango, the older one had a similar issue in the past and some people used a cron job to restart the device once in a while which worked just fine for them till a good driver became available. There is reason to believe the driver will be fixed by Marvell but there is also reason to believe that this wont be tomorrow or next week.
