Hi!

I'm a recent convert from DD-WRT, and while OpenWRT might seem confusing at first, it's far better than DD-WRT.

1) Login to LUCI - Go to Network - Interfaces - Click "Edit" on the LAN interface.

2) Change your IPV4 adress

3) Enter your IPV4 Gateway (the IP of your DHCP server)

4) Enter DNS (Use Google's 8.8.8.8 and 8.8.4.4 or use your ISP DNS)

5) To disable DHCP, go to the bottom of the page and select the box that says "Ignore this interface". You should also go to IPV6 settings and disable DHCPv6, just select disabled on those 3 selectors.

6) Go to "Physical Settings" and select the WAN and WAN6 interfaces (in the WDR4300 they should be together in the "VLAN Interface: "eth0.2". This bridges the interfaces and turns the WAN into LAN.

7) Save and apply

8) Enjoy exploring OpenWRT : )
