Your "br-lan" was bridged to eth0.1. By deleting vlan1 you dropped it out from br-lan, too. This means your routers network interface "CPU" (according to switch port naming) is no longer connected to the outside world.

http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/arcadyan/arv7510pw
Seems there is no "long press reset on boot and wait for tftp listening" like some other devices have. So I guess you don't have that option on your device.

Go grab yourself a serial cable, log into the local console and either factory reset that device or reconfigure your switch.
