DO NOT MOUNT EXISTING PARTITIONS AS SWAP!
You said that your disk has 2 partitions - one Ext4 and another FAT32 (vfat).
If you set one of them as swap then you can lose all your data on that partition.
Do not set existing partitions to swap - you need to create a new empty partition and use *that* as swap.

Let me guess... you're trying to design a device to track people over a large marketplace?

Regardless, I can give you a few hints. Personally I swear by the ALFA AWUS036H wireless card - it has excellent sensitivity, and a RP-SMA connector for an external antenna. Few cards can match up to this one, and you will definitely want an external antenna for better sensitivity. The drawback is that it only works on the 2.4GHz channels - it cannot receive on the 5GHz band (ieee802.11 a). If you want a dual-band card then go for Alfa AWUS051NH. These are both USB cards, so you will need a router with USB port. If you need a mini-PCI card then perhaps take a look at some ubiquity cards. I hear the "SR" or "XR" ones are nice.

~Frag
