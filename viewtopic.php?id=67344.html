Looking at the wiki those instruction look off. After step 7 you are done recovering the bootloader or you wouldn't land at the Marvell prompt after step 8. Sure you might still need to fix the environment or flash a firmware image if they are corrupted or broken.

So my advise, ignore step 9 till 11. Follow "Corrupt Environment Recovery" respectively "Serial Firmware Flash" if you have to or just let the device boot otherwise.
