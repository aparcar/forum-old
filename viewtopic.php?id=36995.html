what you want to accieve has nothing to do with WDS, the clients are just roaming between the APs.
Roaming (with no encryption or psk) is mostly a client-thing, but there are some standards e.g. 802.11f (IAAP) and 802.11r. this protocols are implemented in hostapd but I'm not sure if it works under openwrt. (maybe you have to run hostapd with a custom config)

If you are using only psk-authentication and no enterprise-authentication you should be fine with setting the same ssid on all APs and brigde them to the same LAN-segment. The wifi-driver of the client has to scan for other accesspoints in the background and then needs to connect to the accesspoint with the better signal. There isn't much to do on the AP, take a look if you can tweak your wifi-settings of your client.
