Just want to warn people that you will brick your WRT400N as I just did if you use this firmware:


openwrt-15.05.1-ar71xx-generic-wrt400n-squashfs-sysupgrade.bin

I found this from the page devoted to the WRT400N: https://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/hwdata/lin … wrt400n_10.

I also read through the generic debricking and failsafe pages, tried out what I could without a serial or JTAG connection, and still nothing. I tried pressing the singular front panel button repeatedly for a minute on start, and also the reset button in back too, but there was no change in blink, or was I able to ping the router at 192.168.1.1 with my laptop's IP set to 192.168.1.2.

I thought it might be "alive" because the ethernet indicator ports will light up, but I've since read that that means nothing.

Guess I have a brick?
