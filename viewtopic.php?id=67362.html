Take a look at the OpenWRT Makefile for the transmission package (feeds/packages/net/transmission/Makefile). You will see PKG_SOURCE:=$(PKG_NAME)-$(PKG_VERSION).tar.xz. Then, do a google search for the PKG_SOURCE (replace the $(PKG_NAME)-$(PKG_VERSION) with the real name and version) and you will find this transmission-2.92.tar.xz. Once downloaded, make sure you check the MD5SUM is correct.
