davidc502 wrote:

Since you are on OpenWrt Chaos Calmer 15.05.1, Yes, I recommend going to stock first and then to lede.
I tried going from OpenWrt to lede, and it was a bit of a mess. lol  So, I had to go to stock and then back to lede, and it worked fine.


I took my wrt1900ac v1 from Chaos Calmer 15.05.1 to your build directly ie  LEDE build 4.4.19.
The caveat when doing the direct flash is DO NOT SAVE SETTINGS!

I manually entered the settings again on LEDE build 4.4.19 without issue.

Then on the LEDE build I did save settings on the full flash from 4.4.19 to 4.4.23 again without issue.

Thus to recap   Chaos Calmer 15.05.1  to  LEDE 4.4.19 or 4.4.23   (DO NOT SAVE SETTINGS!)

LEDE 4.4.19 to 4.4.23  one can save settings without issue.   

I hope that is clear as mud...

Cheers
