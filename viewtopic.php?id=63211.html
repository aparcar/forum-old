I've installed Chaos Calmer (15.05, r46767) onto my router, and my IPv6 connectivity is behaving strangely:

When I'm logged into the router, IPv6 works fine: I can ping6 dev.openwrt.org and get responses. However, when I'm on my laptop, the same thing produces nothing.

My openwrt box has a /128 address from my ISP on its WAN interface, and ::1 of a /64 prefix-delegation on its LAN interface. The routing table has default from the /128 and the /64 via the same router.

My laptop has SLAAC addresses from the /64, and a default route via openwrt.

I ran tcpdump on the wan interface of openwrt, and saw the outbound echo requests from my laptop, but no replies.

I haven't seen this issue mentioned here or on google.

Does anyone have a suggestion for what to try next to isolate the issue?
