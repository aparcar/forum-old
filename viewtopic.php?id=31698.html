How did you attempt your failsafe?
For the 1043ND I do as follows

Unplug power, plug power back in.
Wait until SYS light starts flashing, then press and hold QSS until SYS light starts flashing rapidly.
I now have failsafe and telnet.
I've messed up my box more times than I can count, this always works for me.
