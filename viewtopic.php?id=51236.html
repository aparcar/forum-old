Yeah, I know....this system is old. But if I can enable some features on it then I might be able to use it for something (like wireless repeater or something).

I was able to get serial output on this model (while I wait to get a JTAG dongle to fix my e4200v2). If you are wondering what pins on the a90-9100em15-10 are for serial, they are marked on the board with "P1402", near one of the large round capacitors and just behind the "LED array" The serial settings are the typical 115200,8,N,1. The pins are 1 (TX) & 3 (RX). Ground anywhere on the board you want. I'm using my Arduino Uno to do the 3.3V serial.
