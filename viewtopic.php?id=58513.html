petiso
16 Jul 2015, 18:30

I have an Archer C7, with Chaos Chalmer RC2. The router itself is very good for the price.
I did not used it with stock firmware, just taked it out of the box and installed OpenWRT.

My experience:

- 5ghz wlan is unstable, clients drops on high traffic. I opened a ticket on OpenWRT and contacted ath10k developers trying to debug the issue.
- 2.4ghz and wired are rock solid. Very good range and throughput.

It's a good router, OpenWRT it's an excellent firmware, I'm sure it's just a little bug with 5ghz that will be soon discovered and solved: ath10k interface are very common!
