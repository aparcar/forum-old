6 Apr 2016, 22:20

Think of flashing an image to a partition as a round-robin. When you flash an image it goes to the partition that is not current;i.e.the one you are not currently running. What is on the other partition is dependent on what was there when you last ran that partition. There are ways to flash to a particular partition if that is what you need; i.e. you want to keep an image as good, while you try out another image.

Not sure about merging the partitions. I suppose a modified boot loader would be possible. Don't think you should really need ti though. Plenty of resources on this device. I have a DD build with everything I need, lots of flash remaining. The usefulness of the dual partition structure would outweigh combining them in my opinion.
