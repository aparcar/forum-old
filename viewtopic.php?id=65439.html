I'm building costumized alarm with openwrt. Everything works fine untill playing alarm file.
I have alarm.mp3 which contains 3 minutes of length. But i want alarm.mp3 only play for certain amount of time (e.g 30 seconds, 1 minutes ,etc)
I tried this following command but with no luck

madplay alarm.mp3 ; sleep 10 ; killall madplay

It looks like madplay play the whole mp3 file, which is 3 minutes instead 10 seconds.
Need help everyone
Thankyou

Post #2
mazilo
10 Jun 2016, 04:40

AFAICT, madplay plays the whole length of the music. The sleep 10 means to pause 10 seconds after madplay alarm.mp3 process has completed. Once the 10 seconds pause has elapsed, killall madplay will get executed. In this case, it probably is a waste.
