Solved the problem by myself.

Member 5t3v3 comment in
http://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=4311
mentioned shorting pins 5-6 instead of 15-16. This worked for me and I flashed this firmware:
http://downloads.openwrt.org/people/nbd/whiterussian/

I can give specs from my router (and update supported harware section), if someone tells me howto do it or can point out the "perfect" example.
