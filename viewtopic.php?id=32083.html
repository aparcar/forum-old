Could be problem with driver: kmod-usb-[O|U|E]hci
Could be problem with filesystem: do you use kmod-fs-ext4 or kmod-fs-ext3?
Could be problem with fragmentation: defrag the partition
Could be problem with hardware: the router, the harddisc or the connector PCB.

1. Have you tried turning it off and on again?
2. Have you tried different [partition|harddisc|filesystem|fs-module|usb-driver]?
