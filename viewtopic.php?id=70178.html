ok- after a few more attempts i was presented access via ssh. here are steps i used:

1 Power off the device
2 Set a static ip for the device you are connecting from on the 192.168.1.0 network
3 Open a command prompt and start a continuous “ping 192.168.1.1 -t”
4 Connect a cable to the router’s LAN PORT
5 power on device
6 ping will fail throughout the boot cycle, but there is a small window when the device will respond to ping, at that time press and hold the reset button for about 10 seconds, connect to LAN PORT, and you can connect to the device on 192.168.1.1

hitting during the first few ping responses was the key. also, i think i only hit the button once rather than a long hold down.

good luck!
j
