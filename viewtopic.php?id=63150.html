I setup a TP Link TL WDR3600 for my brother in law with stock firmware.  He had Comcast with 100Mbps.  He would use speedtest on his iPhone and would usually be able to get around 91 MBps on the speedtest scores on the 5GHZ band.

However he uses Chromecasts and the 2.4ghz band on the router would get lost or disconnect.  I decided to install OpenWRT since I setup one for my sister about 2 years ago with Attitude Adjustment and she never has any problems with speed or connections.

I guess the only option is to install Chaos Calmer on my brothers WDR3600.  I installed Chaos calmer it and hooked it back to his modem and reboot everything.  It seems that the WDR3600 takes a long time to find the modem on the WAN port.  Also his wifi on 5ghz is only giving him 50Mbps.  However hardwire he is getting 100 Mbps.  However the signal seems to be more solid than the stock firmware.

He is now wanting me to go back because he wants the full speed he is paying for on wireless. 

So my question is it a setting on OpenWRT that I have incorrectly set or is the Stock Firmware much better at giving wireless speed?  If so I will let him choose between reliability (OpenWRT) vs speed (stock).
