Post #2
PaulDotCom
26 Jan 2007, 15:41

You can *try* failsafe mode:

http://wiki.openwrt.org/OpenWrtDocs/Troubleshooting

This only works with RC5+, and I've had good success with it.

The power light blinking usually means that the CFE has found the firmware to be corrupt.  If this happens, it will wait for a new image to be TFTP'd to it. 

I do not know why all of the LAN lights are on, that is concerning.  Does anyone know what that could mean?

If all else fails, JTAG is your only way out.

Paul
