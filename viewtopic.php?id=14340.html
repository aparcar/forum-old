You may not need to reflash.  Try getting into failsafe mode:

http://wiki.openwrt.org/OpenWrtDocs/Tro … 35c155b71a

If you can't listen on UDP ports, you should be able to do the following:

Powercycle the router.  The power light will blink.  As soon as the DMZ light comes on, press the reset button on the back for about two seconds.

Then try to telnet to 192.168.1.1 port 22.  It should let you in without a password.

EDIT: I must have been half asleep when I wrote that last paragraph.  Telnet in (no need to specify the port, but it's port 23, of course) and it should let you in without a password.

Then see this for what to do next:
http://wiki.openwrt.org/OpenWrtDocs/Tro … 7c02a8f207
