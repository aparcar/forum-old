This is very simple you just route back through the main router rather than having a direct link to the Internet.

Default configuration.
Change LAN IP address to 192.168.4.1
Set up a wifi AP
Run Ethernet cable from your WAN port to the main router/modem.

You should now see on the summary status page that IPv4 WAN has obtained a 192.168.1.x address.  Connect your computer or phone to your AP and you have access to the Internet.

In this basic configuration you can still access your housemates' devices if you know their IP address, but they cannot see anything on your network.
