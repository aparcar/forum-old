wrtboy
29 Mar 2016, 20:53
gotskeet wrote:

and how can i force a 3x failed boots?

cut the power to the router while the router is booting up.  the router led shall blink rapidly and that's when you cut the power--simulating a failed boot.  my ea4500 reverts back to the linksys classic firmware after 3 failed boots.
