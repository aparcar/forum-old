Hello,

I found my mistake; The KernelPackage hwmon-bcm2835 was missing.

kmod-hwmon-core_3.18.23-1_brcm2708.ipk
kmod-hwmon-bcm2835_3.18.23-1_brcm2708.ipk

4bob:/# sensors
bcm2835_hwmon-isa-0000
Adapter: ISA adapter
temp1:  +41.2 C  (high = +85.0 C)

and Observium Sensor added: temperature lmsensors temp1 ..

Cool !!
