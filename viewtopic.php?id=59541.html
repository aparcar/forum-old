tmo26
11 Sep 2015, 19:44

Every router that has at least 1x USB2.0, where you can plug in a USB drive (with practically unlimited GB) in order to extroot your router.

http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/howto/extroot

If you don't like extroot:

http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/views/toh_standard_all?dataflt[Flash+MBs*~]=2048
http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/views/toh_standard_all?dataflt[Flash+MBs*~]=4096

However, none of them is listed as supported...
