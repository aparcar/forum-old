There're 2 cases :

1) ETH not attached to the switch. Just name the interface ethX.832
2) ETH attached to the switch. Do 1) plus configure the switch.

Then you can bridge these interfaces.
But traffic will go through CPU and its not good. Better stop using WAN and attach external connector to one of LAN ports and configure switch.
