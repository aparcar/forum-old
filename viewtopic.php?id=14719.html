this one.  You should edit /etc/ipkg.conf and put the following:

src snapshots http://wifi.ozo.com/airo/openwrt/firmware/kamikaze/2.6/6306-3.4.6-latest-madwifi-new/packages
src packages http://wifi.ozo.com/airo/openwrt/packages/mipsel
dest root /
dest ram /tmp

you can also make your own image by checking out trunk #6563.  after that revision the trunk is broken for this device.
