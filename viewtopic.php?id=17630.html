Hi,
as far as I know it's useless to delete packages that are part of the image that you flashed on your router.
The package entries will be gone but the packages themselves are still there since they are part of the image build.
No additional free space is created by using this method.
What you can do is to build your own image without the luci-components.
Maybe I'm wrong but you could do a "df" before and after uninstalling all luci-components and there should be no additional free space.
