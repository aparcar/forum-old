I'm not sure what your question is exactly, the things you mentioned are a bit too broad to explain in a simple forum post.
Anyhow, all HTML emitted by LuCI is assembled from templates and template fragments which are basically *.htm files with HTML snippets and special processing directives in it (the <% ... %> tags).

That's pretty much comparable to how programming in PHP works. I'm afraid I cannot help you much further here unless you have more specific questions.

If you like you can also find me in IRC at #luci-hackers on freenode, maybe its easier to qanswer these kind of questions in realtime.
