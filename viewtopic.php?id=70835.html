Is there DC power coming from the USB port?  Plug in a cheap unpowered hub that has a light on it. You should see the light come on, and a new hub register in dmesg.

When there is no driver for your device but the USB system is otherwise working, your device will show in lsusb, but no driver will have claimed it.
