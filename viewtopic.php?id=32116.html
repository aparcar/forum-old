See the http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/buyerguide
To learn quickly about OpenWrt, read the wiki: http://wiki.openwrt.org/

And check the wiki-page for the different devices. The buffalos are fine, but actually not quite what you want, because their bootloaders are, well, with unnecessary features.
Compare flashing procedure of this http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/tp-link/tl-wr1043nd with this: http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/buffalo/wzr-hp-ag300h

The capabilities of the wireless drivers, are here: http://www.linuxwireless.org/en/users/Drivers/

Enjoy.
