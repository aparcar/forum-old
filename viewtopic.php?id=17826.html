pktsize is the expected packet size (in bytes) for the kind of traffic you want to push into this class (very important)
pktdelay is the maximum delay which is guaranteed when there is no overallocation of bandwidth
avgrate is the guaranteed average rate (in % of the link rate)
share (also in %) affects the distribution of the remaining bandwidth after the bandwidth guarantees have been satisfied.
all class parameters are independent of the line speed, and you should probably leave them at default unless you know what you're doing.
the default parameters are tuned for most common setups.
the main thing that needs changing is the upload and download rate.
