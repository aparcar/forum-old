ART is not just model specific, it's also highly device specific, containing wireless calibration data for your specific router. Flashing ART from a different device, but identical model might technically (appear to) work (all bets about standards/ regulatory compliance or wireless stability are off though), using it from a vastly different model however can't work at all.
