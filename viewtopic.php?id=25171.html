You can still find used AR7 routers around. Here is a (partial) list of models.
Unfortunately the AR7 platform is out of production, and the now-ubiquitous Broadcom ADSL routers that replaced them don't have open source drivers for their ADSL modem (though the rest of the hardware is supported by the brcm63xx OpenWrt port).

There seems to be another interesting ADSL platform still in production, and seems to have open drivers for ADSL and VoIP: ifxmips. I haven't been able to find informations about it or even a list of possibly working routers, so I don't know the current status of this port.
