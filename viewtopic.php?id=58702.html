Post #4
alphasparc
29 Jul 2015, 14:42

Backup your art partition first (most important), uboot as well.
Copy the 8MB flash into 16MB using external flash programmer, boot the system, radio will be missing due to wrong art offset.
Sysupgrade an image with unlocked art partition and mtd -r write the backup up art partition then flash the art protected sysupgrade image and reboot.
