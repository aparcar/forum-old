Hi, i'm working on this device (ZBT-WE826) with Sierra Wireless MC7455, this only work with trunk and MBIM (QMI or PPP doesn't work).
Send these AT commands to modem (using screen or minicom to /dev/ttyUSB2) to enable MBIM:

AT!ENTERCND="A710"
AT!USBCOMP=1,1,1009
AT!RESET

And reboot.
