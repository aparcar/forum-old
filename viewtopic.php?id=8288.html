If your antenna is DC shorted like mine (horizontal polarity antennas) maybe there is the problem. I have Linksys WRT54GS v.1.1 in my network and I tried to replace it with ASUS wl-500gP (because of faster CPU and better radio specyfications) but after replacing (same antenna, cables etc) sensitivity was ~ 10dB worse than Linksys! Now I tried  even MikroTik with top grade Senao NMP-8602+ (new Atheros 5006X, top specyfications similar to Ubiquiti SR2) but even with this platform I got worse sensitivity than with Linksys! (ofcourse after backing to Linksys client signal level back to normal)
I'm not 100% sure this is DC shorted problem but I haven't other ideas...
