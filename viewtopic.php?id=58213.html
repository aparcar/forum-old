minix
28 Jun 2015, 07:27

I think you can extract them from the stock firmware for your model.

If your stock firmware size is 4063744 bytes, then I believe the first 131584 bytes should be the bootloader. Use DD to extract 

dd if=stock.bin of=bootloader.bin count=257 bs=512

Here, take a look at this page.
