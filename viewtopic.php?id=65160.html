the ea4500 may fail to reboot after a firmware flash when it gets too warm.  let it cool down first before rebooting.  i made it practice of placing a fan blowing air into it during flashing to ensure it properly reboots following a flash.  the current trunk r49377 for the ea4500/e4200v2 is rock solid.  reset button works for this router but router has to stay cool.
