Post #12
qasdfdsaq
27 Jul 2014, 04:09
jow wrote:

The internet says that the Nexus 5GHz radio only likes channels 36, 40, 44 or 48 - do you operate your 5GHz radio on one of those?

As you probably realize, the actual radio hardware is capable of using any channel from 36 to 165, it's a driver/software problem restricting it.

Whether that's Broadcom's fault, Android's fault, or LG's fault I wouldn't know. Android's one of those awkward platforms though, as it has no native support for wireless roaming, yet everybody expects it to 'just work', a lot of vendors and manufacturers have implemented their own proprietary and/or modified wireless stacks and drivers.
