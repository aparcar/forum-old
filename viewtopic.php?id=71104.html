afon222 wrote:

Have problem: dont work wifi  Can I fix it ?

By default, the wireless radio is off on OpenWRT images. You will need to turn it on manually, either from LuCI or by pressing whatever rfkill-switch your router has

afon222 wrote:

and some bugs are present: leds on ports 1-4 always off, power led is red only and no way to turn off red and turn on green. Can I fix it?

You can configure the behavior of the LEDs from LuCI, i.e. when do they lit, how do they lit and what they indicate. Look for a menu item referring to LEDs in the LuCI, and familiarize yourself with https://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/uci/system#leds

Open SSH connection to the router and type 'iw phy'. Paste results of the command here. Then type 'iw dev' and paste the results. When copy-pasting the results, use the <code> formatting element.

These two commands show the physical capabilities of your wireless radio, and the status of any wireless interfaces present.
