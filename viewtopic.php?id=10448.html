The is no VNC package for OpenWrt.  If your device is still performing it's intended task as a router/firewall then a VNC server (or anything X11-related) is considered a security risk.  Also, most of these devices are extremely limited when it comes to storage space and RAM, so the X11 libs, etc would just consume more space.  There are very small X-servers (tinyx, Xserver, etc) but they don't have much purpose on a little headless 200mhz MIPSel router.

If you just want to keep CLI apps going when you aren't connected to the router, take a look at screen, or it's lighter cousin, dtach.  They both do exactly what you want, and screen has many more features for managing multiple terms/sessions, etc.
