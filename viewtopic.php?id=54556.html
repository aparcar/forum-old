hillz wrote:

Please help how do I free up my ROM ?

You don't. You can't. ROM is "Read Only Memory", you cannot write to it, you cannot modify it, you (or rather, the system) can only read it. For whatever reason, you cherry-picked the one partition in the system you cannot write to.

Your df output shows a perfectly fine 1.6 GB of free space. Just pick another place to write to. Well, maybe not /tmp either.

But, no offense, you should really read up on why and what you write and delete on your router. If what you tried to delete had worked your router would be in a quite awful state now.
