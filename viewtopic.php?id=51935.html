@bajajhiteshster will you share your ffmpeg+libmp3lame build?
is it going to work on the Arduino Yun?

and for your question, you may want to decrease the bitrate
i've had endless problems with ffmpeg streaming until i decreased the overall bitrate to below 100kbps.
it's best if you keep it to a 32k for a compressed audio stream (mp3/2, 22050kHz, mono, s16, 32kbps)
you can also stream a 8kHz pcm_u8 at 64kbps.
