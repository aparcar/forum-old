If you put the wrong build on it thin it is bricked first check if the DHCP is working by looking at your PC IP if it is 192.168.1.XX in that range you might be ok TEL-NET into it if you can,  ALL builds I flashed looks something like this ( openwrt-15.05.1-ar71xx-generic-tl-wa830re-v1-squashfs-factory.bin )If yours is a V1 thin this is the file you should have flashed it with. If you don't get an IP from the DHCP on the router thin your PC will give it its own IP which could be anything, Thin try failsafe if that don't work Thin I'm sorry your device is bricked.Read the WIKI might help you recover from the bad flash.

OR be sure your lan is turned on on your PC lapTop and turn the wireless off whin you are configuring with a CAT-5 cable.
