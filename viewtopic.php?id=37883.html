WDS handling is in mac80211.sh. Not sure what you mean with talking WDS on the wired network. If you have a wired backbone you don't really need WDS.

Maybe we have different ideas of what WDS does. In the mac80211/OpenWrt realm, "WDS" means 4addr mode for wifi, so you can bridge stations. It does not client roaming or seamless handover, that is doable with a wired bridged backbone, no need for WDS there.
