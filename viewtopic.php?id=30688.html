I mentioned this in this thread: https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=29828 , but I thought I'd post it separately as a more pointed question.

After flashing any OpenWrt image (including variants like Gargoyle and some offered private builds), my 4 LAN ports come up requiring a cross-over cable to connect to them.  It's pretty odd since they come up in the normal straight-through mode if I flash Netgear images or DD-WRT.  Also, in the router's native recovery mode, the ports are normal.  In OpenWrt failsafe mode, the ports are cross-over.

Any advice on how to get the ports configured back to normal?  (Or even any affirmation that anybody else has seen the same problem? :-) )

I've flashed about a zillion times with various images and consistently see what I described above.
