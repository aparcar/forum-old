Most of the logic would either be 3V3 or 1V8. Get your multimeter and see if you are getting voltage out on the pin you had the 5V connected into (make sure the 5V in is disconnected). Plug in and power up the unit. If 3V3 or 1V8 is present on the pin you should be good. If not I would look to replace a voltage regulator somewhere upstream...

Hot air gun and soldering iron needed. Warranty = VOID. Your milage may vary. Caveat emptor.
