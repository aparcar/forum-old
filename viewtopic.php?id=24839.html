The TP-LINK TL-WR741ND (v1.8) seems to be working fine with 10.03 - it's a pretty much out of the box experience really.

That said, do be warned -- it seems possible to brick the thing if one tries to upgrade openwrt with another/newer
openwrt image release. Perhaps all too late, I may have recalled reading about taking the device back to -standard-
(shipped) firmware, before trying to do such a thing   Whatever... they were on special for $25 a pop, I grabbed
2 of them, and so I'll recover the bricked one just as soon as I soldered a ttl<->rs232 interface together and have a
poke at it to see what's actually broken...
