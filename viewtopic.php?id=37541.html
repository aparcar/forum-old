sayap wrote:

Hi, does anyone know how to get into fail safe mode on TP-Link TL-MR3040? I might have bricked it...

Hello,

there is a fail safe mode on TP-Link TL-MR3040, using the reset buton :
- switch on the router
- wait 5 sec
- press the reset switch

Et voilà, your are in fail safe mode, as you can verify it with a "ping 192.168.1.1"...

Ah, don't worry with the LED, they are never flashing !

Bye
