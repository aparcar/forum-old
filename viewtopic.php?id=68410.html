I was planning on connecting these ones on an older router.
At the moment I did connect in parallel to the dual band ones from the 4300 a set of double biquad omni directional ones, and I must say they work a treat! Best results are with power at 18-19dBm. And the alignment is very important for data speed. I'll try and remove the original two dual band ones from the 2.4GHz line and see if there's any change. The single 5GHz one does the trick very good on the first floor. I get full signal everywhere in the room, maxed at 100mbps.
