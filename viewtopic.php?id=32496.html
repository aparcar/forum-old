These patches were rejected because of several coding issues and are currently waiting for resubmission.

While it is possible to use them with OpenWrt (with a bit of effort), you won't have much fun with it, as ethernet support is missing. Also dependng on the type of flash of your device, you might get further problems (only parallel flash is supported, but neither nand nor serial).
