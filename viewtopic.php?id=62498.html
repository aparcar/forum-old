And because last time I shop for routers WRT54G was the thing (I have 3 of the in fully working order) I was glad to see a familiar face with upgraded looks and internals. WRT1900 , And I managed to get Second version WRT1900ACv2 from what I understand it's the same version as WRT1900ACS with 1.6Ghz Dual Core CPU (how do I test clock speed ??)
