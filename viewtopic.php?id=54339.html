start here: http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/uci/minidlna
There is also a luci-app for minidlna that will let you configure it from the webgui. You'll also need USB & NTFS support and then mount your external HDDs to make them accessible via DLNA. More info in the wiki on those topics also.
