try this way:

connect your router via LAN-Cable to your computer
hold reset button with pencil
plug in power cable

make sure that the power led blinks slowly

atftp
connect 192.168.1.1
mode octet
trace
timeout 1
put openwrt-brcm-2.4-squashfs.trx

wait 6 minutes

power reset
