You need a serial TTY connection.
Be aware : most board use 3.3volts for this
Use a cheap USB to serial(TTL)  cable for this, most have 4 wires.
Don't connect the "red" wire to the board -> 5volts

Use this as example
https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=67192
happy hacking
