slh
23 Dec 2017, 23:35

Basically, yes.

The 2.4 GHz wlan card is based on QCA ath9k, which has good/ free drivers for OpenWrt.

The 5 GHz wlan card however is a lantiq XWAVE300 variant, which is not supported in OpenWrt (you'll find some threads about it in this forum, showing that it's a pain at best).
