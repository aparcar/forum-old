Your PC is already "hidden" by the router in the default configuration. That's what NAT is. Your PC's MAC address is never shown outside the LAN.

I'd be surprised if the captive portal actually picks up your MAC address I think to do that it would need some kind of plugin? I wouldn't recommend changing the MAC address of your router to the same as your PC, then your router wouldn't be able to communicate with it.

Did the router get an IP address from the RJ45 connection? If not, that's where you should start troubleshooting.
