You can't power a the WRT52GS from a USB port reliably. The absolute minimum input voltage for the power regulator is 7v, USB will only provide 5v. Many external 2.5" hard drives are powered from 2 USB ports so you are partly right, but my GS uses (up to) 500mA at 12 V which would be 1.2A at 5v - still too much for 2 ports.
