In that case, you had an ONT, a modem, and a router; in theory, you just need to replace the router. However, you must know how exactly to configure de router, as not all ISPs are the same. Most providers use PPPoE on a VLAN, so you need to know the ID for the VLAN, and the username and password for the PPPoE.

If your logs say "sending PADI to WLAN-1", then there must be something wrong with your configuration, as PPPoE should not be trying to initiate any communication over the wireless network. If you post your /etc/config/network file here, someone will have a look at it.
