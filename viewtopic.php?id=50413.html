If your router just got an random ip address from the other dhcp server. You either look into the other servers lease list or try to look with tools like wireshark for traffic from your router and get its ip address from there.

For recovery mode you need to press the button only shortly. Try to press it more the once during boot, but dont hold it.

There is also a terminal on the serial port.
