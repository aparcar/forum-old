25 Aug 2011, 20:29

ah okay then.
restart the unit and watch the SYS led. Once it starts blinking press and hold the QSS button until the SYS starts flashing really fast.
you will now be able to log into it via telnet at 192.168.1.1 just like when you first flashed it.

that's what's called failsafe mode  http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/tp-link/tl- … lsafe.mode
