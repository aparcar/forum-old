Some systems have built-in hardware monitoring that you can monitor the cpu/system temp with, but not the WRT54GL (AFAIK).  You could always mount a wireless temp probe or a USB temp probe (if you mod your WRT54GL with USB 1.1)... but seems kinda silly to go through all that trouble.  You could also just stick a thermometer in there and check it manually... climb the tree

On another note, you don't have to have internet access on your AP to install packages.  You could download the packages manually from a PC, then transfer them to the AP (with wget from a ftp serve, etc), then install them.
