Two thoughts:

1. It's still possible that you have RX and TX reversed (loopback would still work).  You can verify the router's TX pin with a speaker -- you'll hear it when it's sending characters to the UART.  Or just reverse your connections and try it ...

2. Make sure your comm program is not sending modem initialization strings etc. on startup.  You want "offline mode".
