Whenever access the router with 192.168.1.1, it will be redirect to the 192.168.1.1/cgi-bin/luci by default.
I am wondering how to make the redirection to another url.
Suppose I have a page.html under /www, how can I make the request to 192.168.1.1 default to 192.168.1.1/page.html, but not /cgi-bin/luci?

Setup your www-server to additionally listen to a port different than port 80 and let it serve pages from a directory of your choice.

Post #3

Edit /www/index.html and change the target in the <meta> refresh tag.
Don't forget to clear your browser cache after doing so.
