The WRT54G v5+ only has 2 MB flash, which isn't enough for OpenWrt.  However, DD-WRT has a "micro" version which fits on 2 MB flash... I would go with that.  I have a WRT54G v6 running DD-WRT micro, which works much better than the factory firmware.

Better yet, get a new router (Netgear WNDR3700, D-link DIR-825, or TP-Link TL-WR1043ND) and put OpenWrt on it!
