After reading everything I could find, and thanks to hnyman and others I finally have a pretty good IPv6 Tunnel with HE.  Radvd still has some serious issues since I have to manually restart after any net change.  That however, is not my problem just my way of introducing mysefl as another NOOB to OpenWRT.

Here is my problem, my broaband connect is via a 2wire RG (residential gateway) that is required to act effectively as a dsl modem.  I've placed the WNDR3700v2 in the DMZ so that it can get all of the tunnel traffic, and that works well. But, I get this message logged in the RG almost everytime a DNS request is made:

fw,fwmon src=xx.yyy.aaa.bbb dst=8.8.4.4 ipprot=1
icmp_type=3 icmp_code=3 ICMP Dest Unreachable, session terminated

xx.yyy.aaa.bbb is my obsfuscated ip addr and can be ignored; 8.8.4.4 is the google dns servers that I have the WNDR3700v2 using, but it occurs for the other one as well - 8.8.8.8.  If I specify my ISP dns servers, in the WAN settings then those are logged instead.  It seems as though dnsmasq may be trying to reuse UDP sessions  and the port is rejected?

Any ideas would be appreciated.

BTW, if I just let the clients do their own dns requests I don't see any errors.
