It sounds supportable. https://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/hwdata/zte … xhnf660_23

The first steps are to collect as much detail about it as possible. Open it up take some photos, identify the chips and the serial connector (sounds like you have already). Links to factory images (binaries), GPL source code (if any). Capture a full boot log. You can take a look at what I went through to try and support another device (EVG2000) https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=63950
