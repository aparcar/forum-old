https://dev.openwrt.org/roadmap
Some of those near 400 active tickets should be closed in order to go.
Most of them are duplicate of old ones, some needs input from the author that die in the process... So some tickets can be closed without much knowledge but observation.

Check if your router matches active tickets and contribute:
worksforme You doesn't have the problem described.
obsolete Same as the above but you have a much newer revision than reporter.
fixed You know exactly in witch revision has been solved. Fixed in rXXXXX
duplicate Has been reported before so you feel a dejav√π. Try hard to remember where do you read that before. Dupe of #XXXX
invalid Not related to OpenWrt. People tend to report known bugs from Asterix or whatever here, or a misconfiguration between keyboard and chair like: "OpenWrt allows firewalling myself"

With little work you can add patch to a bug easily solved by yourself but reported by someone else. Apply diff -urN original yours >> patch to all files involved and upload the result patch file.
