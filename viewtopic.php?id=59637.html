Just a heads up if you are having issues with newer Picostations and openwrt. The bootloader was updated with the new airOS and openwrt is bombing when you try to flash it.

-reset pico
-install the latest version of airOS XM 5.6.3
-then downgrade to airOS XM 5.5.11 through the airOS webgui
-reset pico
-tftp your openwrt bin

Hopefully this helps!
