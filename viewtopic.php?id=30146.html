Access FailSafe mode and run,

cd /tmp
wget http://downloads.openwrt.org/backfire/10.03.1-rc4/ar71xx/openwrt-ar71xx-wzr-hp-g300nh-squashfs-sysupgrade.bin
sysupgrade -v -n openwrt-ar71xx-wzr-hp-g300nh-squashfs-sysupgrade.bin

Generic Sysupgrade - OpenWrt Wiki
