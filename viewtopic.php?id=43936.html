I have a NETGEAR WNDR3700 that I just installed Attitude Adjustment 12.09 on and I can't get port forwarding to my Windows 7 box on port 1723 to work for PPTP VPN. It worked fine before with the stock firmware.

I've gone into the firewall and added a port forward for port 1723 to my box, and one for 3389 (RDP). The one for RDP works fine, but the one for the VPN just doesn't seem to want to connect through the WAN. I can connect via wireless or LAN ok.

I've removed all the VLAN config, but otherwise the system is at default. I tried changing the zone settings to allow the WAN to forward to the LAN and vice versa but that didn't seem to help.

How do I get the port forwarding to work for VPN?

Just a guess, add following rules to firewall.user:

# Allow forwarding from/to VPN interface
iptables -A forwarding_rule -i ppp+ -j ACCEPT
iptables -A forwarding_rule -o ppp+ -j ACCEPT
