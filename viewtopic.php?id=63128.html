Browsers tend to remember http redirection status codes (especially 301, moved permanently), which are both used by luci-ssl and many vendor firmwares, basically forever . No amount of reloading the URL, nor restarting the browser is likely to make it re-read the actually requested URL (clearing the cache might be the only option).
