Joe1997 wrote:

I'm not able to flash the openwrt firmware. I always received the 18005 error code.

You should use the forum Search link at the top of these pages to search for "error 18005."  There are many posts and threads with this problem.  Two potential solutions:

https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=63123

https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php … 70#p312170

Just be aware that if there is any error when you flash your router can end up bricked and require TFTP recovery (easy) or serial port connection (hard, crack open the router and solder on serial pins or pad and connect with a special USB-to-TTL cable).  HTH.

Clemmitt
