That's quite a well-written guide - good link to keep.

To the best of my knowledge: no, this is seemingly how it works in OpenWRT.  I have a vague recollection of someone explaining once that this has something to do with the way the Atheros drivers used by OpenWRT work, but I can't back that up with any facts.  If you want to research more it's got something to do with simultaneous client (STA) and access point (AP) mode setups depending on the STA connection being up.
