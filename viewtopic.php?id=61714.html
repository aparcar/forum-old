murasame wrote:
pepe2k wrote:

The mpci-e slot contains USB 2.0 interface only.

Really ? This kinda destroy my dreams but they advertise the compatibility with 3G/4G modems on the pci-e slot, they are just connected to the USB bus on a mini pci-e card ?

3G/4G modems in mpci-e form factor cards use USB bus only, not pci-e bus (plus SIM signals, which are included in mpci-e form factor standard).

MT7621 has 3x pci-e x1 host buses. On WG2626 two of them are taken by Wi-Fi chips and last one by ASM1061 (pci-e to SATA bridge). I don't expect that one pci-e bus is multiplexed between SATA bridge and mpci-e connector.
