Someone posted here about FRITZ!Box a few days ago.  From the references posted by that person, it seems FRITZ!Box does not support WDS.  They do support what they call a "newer, better" repeater mechanism, but sadly don't seem to provide that information to anyone else - despite saying that they can only act as repeater if the other device also supports this "newer, better" protocol.

I'd suggest finding out what this "newer, better" mechanism is before attempting to make an OpenWRT device talk to it.

I'm not German so had to use Google translate, but the following page seems to be quite clear about it: (despite some gross inaccuracies such as claiming WEP is required for WDS)
http://avm.de/nc/service/fritzbox/fritz … Verfahren/
