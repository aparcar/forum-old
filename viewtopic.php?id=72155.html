Thats right, you can use both IPSec and OpenVPN on your router once you've installed LEDE/OpenWrt.

For IPSec, the package you need is called strongswan.
Openvpn is called just that.

Do note that while it might not be as easy as clicking a few buttons on a GUI, their text configuration is easy to understand and once you've grasped the basics you will be able to create complex setups quickly.

And there's a lot of tutorials/info online on how to set either of those up.
