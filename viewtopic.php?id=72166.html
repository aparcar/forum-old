When you boot it up, does one of the LEDs flash fast for a few seconds, then slower, then light up solid?

To enter failsafe mode you should hit (and release) the reset button several times while the LED is flashing fast. If you were successful it will start to flash really fast constantly.   Then you should be able to telnet or ssh (depending on the version) to log in at 192.168.1.1

Do this with the USB device unplugged.  Also make sure your power supply is good.  Cheap phone chargers often aren't good for pocket routers.
