neko259 wrote:

... openwrt-ar71xx-dir-600-a1-squashfs-factory.bin

That is for DIR-600 rev.A, not DIR-615. (Although the Wiki page leads to believe that it may be somewhat suitable for 615 rev.E)

And there are several hardware versions  of DIR-615 itself, with totally different processors etc.
Do you have rev.A, B, C, D or E? They need different Openwrt versions. (It has been a very stupid decision from D-link to maintain DIR-615 brand, although both chassis and the components of the router have changed several times during the past 3-4 years.)

The C revision is the star of that DIR-615 series, and has been supported for a long time. A, B and D were more crippled down versions, and E, F, G might be again something else.
