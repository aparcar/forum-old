miguelcma wrote:

A month ago I've upgraded to Kamikaze 7.09 and apparently wifidog was ok, until a lot of users tried to connect and wasn't redirected to the auth login. This wasn't regular.. sometimes it happens when the fourth client connects, sometimes in the tenth.

I have not used wifidog.  Only chillispot, but someone I know who was using chillispot on DD-Wrt had a similar sounding issue.  The problem was that dnsmasq and chillispot would both try to give the client an IP address.  Sometimes chillispot would win and the client would get redirected to the login page.  Sometimes dnsmasq would win and the client could surf without logging in.

i.e.  make sure that dnsmasq is not handing out leases to your clients on the wifi interface.
