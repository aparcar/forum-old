davidc502 is doing an outstanding job with his custom images for the WRT devices, I'm confident that when the open source-compatible WiFi driver for WRT3200 is released by Qualcomm his build would be among the first to receive it. Just start monitoring his thread on his custom images and be on the lookout for messages like "OMG! WiFi is finally stable!".

In order to go back to stock you need to run sysupgrade -F -n from CLI. If you're familiar with Linux/CLI and are not afraid to google for WRT12xx/19xx/32xx posts on this and LEDE Project forum that should be enough information to get you going.
