You will need a 3.3 volt serial converter.  Most of these connect to a PC by USB, so they can be used with any modern PC.

Once you have the necessary hardware, you will have to open the case of the router and do some soldering to connect it.  The picture of the board in the wiki shows that the serial port connection is near the RAM chip and WPS button.  It is the line of 4 empty holes.  The hole with a square copper pad is pin 1.
