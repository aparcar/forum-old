I found this RT5350F board in a NoName IP camera (it could be the same manufacturer who makes the wansview ip
cameras) .
The camera has the generic chinese design (see pictures)

Its 38pin (19 on each side) and hard soldered on the board.
It has 32MB RAM and 8MB Flash, same specs as many other rt5350 based modules.
Currently i've got the NixCore X1 firmware running on it, it seems quite happy although the board pinout is completly different, but it has the same specs.

I identified pin 37 to be gpio0, i could measure out vcc and gnd pins, but it wouldnt help much i guess.
I did a search on google images btw. Had a quick look at alibaba, etc but couldn't find the exact module.
