Oh, you mean the ones on the PCB... Yes, these Hirose U.FL look about right.
Be careful, I quote: "Female U.FL connectors are not designed with reconnection in mind, and they are only rated for a few reconnects before replacement is needed".

Here you can buy such a pigtail: http://gh.de/?in=&fs=pigtail (they have a UK page as well, just look for it)
