Until now I'm using a Netgear WNDR4300v1 with OpenWrt Chaos Calmer 15.05.1 and I'm quite happy with that. My internet access was 100 MBit/s (bidirectional) and I was able to fully use that bandwidth.

Now my provider made an upgrade to 300 MBit/s. With the router, the provider gives me, I was able to fully utilize that. I tested with speedtest between an ethernet LAN port and the ethernet WAN port. But with the WNDR4300 I get "only" about 160 MBit/s.

For the moment that's more than enough speed. But in the long term I'd like to upgrade my router. Any ideas, what model will give that performance with OpenWRT?
