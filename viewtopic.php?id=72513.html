Look near the yellow RJ45 jack
There is some 4 pin unsoldered header.
This look like some serial line.

Try to figure out which pins are GND, TX, RX and maybe 3,3 Volts.

Use some 4 pin USB to serial adapter.

NOTE/IMPORTANT
Don't attach the red wire from the usb serial adapter.
There are 5 Volts which will damage your board.
