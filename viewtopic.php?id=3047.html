Let you know why I am stuck

As I know there are two ways to rescue a Asus WL500G Deluxe.

1. Reflash the firmware:  Unplug power, press reset, replug power, 1s then release reset.  Then can tftp to 192.168.1.1.

2. Failsafe of openwrt:     Unplug power, Plug again and wait for 3s, then press reset and hold for 10s then release.  Then can telnet to 192.168.1.1 and reach a minimum openwrt

I tried both before, they worked.

But this time, a whole night of 5 hours, retry again and again, all failed.  All I can see is the box bootup until Power and Air are on.  I can see a non-secured wireless network named "openwrt".  Can connect to it.  No dhcp.  No 192.168.1.1 there.  What about LAN?  No, no 192.168.1.1 too.

How was this happen?  I was setting up WDS with another Linksys WRT54G v1 (connecting to ADSL).  It worked fine indeed.  Perfect, I was happy.  So I tried to swap them, connect Asus to ADSL instead.  I simply 'reboot' from ssh.  Then, oops, can't connect back.

People suggested serial console but  I have no idea of how to buy those electronic parts and put things together.

Should I send my box back to Asus.  Will they help me?
