I would not use ANY image that is not on openWRT. Why? Because there is no date stamp, those pages could be months or years out of date (like maddox).
What to flash?
If unknown version:
Try trunk:
http://downloads.openwrt.org/snapshots/ … actory.bin
If you are SURE it is 1.6 then try
http://downloads.openwrt.org/attitude_a … actory.bin
(see: http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/tp-link/tl-wr703n   for explanation)
