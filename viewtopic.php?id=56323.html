The GUI might not have filtered out impossible userpresets in the past, but that doesn't means the device has actually transmitted at those power levels before. Regardless of what you configure, the hardware of your router has its own (hard) limits - which are somewhere between 17-20 dBm for the TL-WDR4300 (likewise are the regulatory domain ranges).
