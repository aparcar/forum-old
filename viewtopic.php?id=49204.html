Sharonbuss
10 Mar 2014, 10:56

The flash is 4MB. Don't flash fw larger the 3.9MB in 4MB flash.
You need the usb2uart cable to access the console.
before you try to flash the new FW, Beware to backup your original FW, especially for the art partition.
If the art data lost,  you can not start the wlan in Openwrt.
Unfortunately have no idea how to backup data use uboot.
