I am a WRTSL54GS owner (and lover) and was about to purchase a second to use for testing upgrades, different releases, etc, when I stopped to consider the WRT350N.  I see that there is recent support for the WRT350N by dd-wrt and soon by openwrt. 

The two models are close: both 8Mb flash, 32Mb ram and usb 2, all features important to me.  I'll admit that the wireless isn't as important to me as the ability to run Linux on the router, but the appeal of new technology, 802.11n, which should work with my Macbook Pro in the near future, is inviting. 

The reviews of the WRT350N aren't very good, but I assume most of those problems are firmware based.  I am a bit worried about the lack of a 802.11n standard, but not overly.

Thoughts?  Opinions?  Rants?  Flames?  Influence me, I'm asking for it.
