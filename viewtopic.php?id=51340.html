http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/howto/generic.failsafe once a buddy erased LAN on a 741 V4 and I sent him this link and he got it in Failsafe mode
How he did it
Connected to WAN
Plugged light adapter to wall and 741 electric switch OFF
Turned switch and first led lighted when second led lighted he used a pin and pressed and hold for about 10 secs. (Like if he was reseting) and got 741 in Failsafe

I had a 741 and the failsafe mode worked like a charm. And I managed to semi-brick  it a lot of times!
Are you sure that you're doing it right?
It's NOT keeping pressed the reset button and powering on the router!
Power as ususal, and look at the leds. When the system led (It looks like a gear) starts lo blink, press repeatedly the reset button, you will see that it starts to blink much faster. Now you're on failsafe mode
