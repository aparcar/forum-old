15.05-15.05.1 includes only chinese version, download ImageBuilder from TRUNK and build your image with profile TLWR941. There will be several images, among them 941nd-v6-CN and 941nd-v6. Second image is compatible with international model. I flashed my international 941nd-v6 few days ago, don't think it's dissapeared from trunk. Regards
