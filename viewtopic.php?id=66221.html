Thanks guys,

you pointed me into the right direction!
My opkg config was faulty.

There where entries pointing to 15.05 in the upper listbox of "GUI / Software / Configuration".
After deleting them, it works like expected.

I had to reload a config-backup a couple of days before.
Perhaps these entries came from there.
