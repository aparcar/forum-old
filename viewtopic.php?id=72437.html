SSL certificates are self-signed (simply because it's not possible to do it otherwise, unless you have a spare domain and either buy a certificate for it from some CA or use letsencrypt/ luci-app-acme, the later only works if your router is directly accessible from the outside), unless you go through the afforementioned trouble, you simply have to accept the self-signed certs (and yes, browsers are not very helpful in this regard).
