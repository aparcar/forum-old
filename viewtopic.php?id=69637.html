I can't speak for OpenWrt, but the LEDE Project (www.lede-project.org) just released its 17.01-rc1 version, aiming for its first stable version sometime in February.

LEDE is a spinoff from OpenWrt - it'll be very familiar to all. It features kernel 4.4.42, fq_codel and cake for latency/lag control, a revamped build system with a commitment to regular updates, and lots more.
