You don't have to ask permission to contribute to the OpenWRT wiki. I contribute there frequently and have both created new pages, made minor edits, and nearly completely overhauled others. Just create a wiki account and make some minor edits here and there (like misspellings, grammar, etc.) until you get comfortable with it. Then start putting in  the new sections that you think would be helpful. Take a look at other wiki pages like the WNDR3700 wiki as reference. I would avoid flat-out deleting sections unless you are very familiar with the hardware and software. Just move them down the page or label them as legacy or something.
