Hi there is bandwidth charts for out going and incoming trafic. You can filter for your hole connection or by client. You can see the data used for 15 mins 1 hour, 1 day or a hole month. You can set quotas so you can't go over your data cap. There is more about this in the Documentation:
https://www.gargoyle-router.com/wiki/doku.php
If your asking how it does it I am not sure. I do know that some of the things are hooked in to the kernel That is why Gargoyle is only built on top of a stable release of OpenWRT.
