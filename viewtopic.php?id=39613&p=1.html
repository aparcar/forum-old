I think this is resulting from the camera not operating at USB2 ("high speed") instead it is only doing 12 Mbps ("full speed").  It is unclear whether the hub you are plugging through is really USB2.  Lack of USB bandwidth will cause the camera device to appear in /dev, but fail to open when you try to use it.

Plugging more than one camera into the same USB bus can also result in bandwidth shortage.

Testing your camera and hub(s) on a Linux desktop may show something.
