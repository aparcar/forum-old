That routers are pretty are old, slow and with small memory.
Are you from Spain? Here there are lots of them, they were used by the ISP Telefonica, and even with the original firmware their performance was just regular.
I don't think the dev team will put their efforts to support such old hardware, being 2012 and with routers twice as powerful as that one for less than $20...
Also it has parallel flash interface, so even upgrading its flash size is a lot harder hard and risky than SPI flash based.
