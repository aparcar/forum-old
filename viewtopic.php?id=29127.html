Network config is:


config 'interface' 'loopback'

        option 'proto' 'static'

config 'interface' 'wan'

Of course vlan 1 stations can reach vlan 3 station, its called routing and is what a router is supposed to do.
You need to prevent vlan 1 <-> 3 traffic crossing either with iptables or policy routing.
