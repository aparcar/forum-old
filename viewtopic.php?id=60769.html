https://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/recipes/relayclient has the instructions

it has to do with the fact that wifi is a broadcast capable interface by default, and therefore has mac addresses and therefore you need either WDS (where the card itself needs to know how to behave as a switch, and the frame format that goes over the air is slightly different).

Or you need relayd to do the nat-like operation with mac and ip addresses.
