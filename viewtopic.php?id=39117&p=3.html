derrij wrote:

can i apply Attitude Adjustment (12.09-beta) sysupgrade.bin or i should squashfs-factory.bin?

I am on OpenWrt Attitude Adjustment r33482 / LuCI Trunk (trunk+svn9309)

The correct version for upgrading an existing Openwrt installation is "sysupgrade". But your current r33482 is already newer than the beta1 from September 4th / r33312. So, an upgrade to beta1 would actually be a downgrade.

Developers said last week that a new beta2 would be published this week. The server troubles may have delayed that by a few days, but that is version for your next upgrade.
