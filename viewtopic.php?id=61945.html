I have an EDIMax 7811U USB Wireless adapter which I am running as a second radio in my travel router. (HooToo TM-02).  I have installed it with kmod-rtl8192cu and it's dependencies.  I have used this in my PCs for quite a while and generally get good wireless performance.  When I connect this to the HooToo it takes the 12Mb performance of the router's single radio down to about 5Mb. 

I do not know if the issue is the wireless adapter, the router, or something else.  I would appreciate hearing from others who use these devices in hopes of finding a better performing unit.  Please include your USB Wireless Device make and Model, the router you are using and the packages you installed to support it.
