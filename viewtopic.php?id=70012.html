Firmware Version:     3.15.1 Build 160616 Rel.44182n

What can I do? How can I install openwrt on the router?

The problem is now solved. If you press in the first seconds after the restart "tpl" + enter and you get to an recovery-menue. Then you can enter the command "tftpboot". After that the router will show you what he need. For example is he looking for an image it called "6F01A8C0.img". And so I have renamed openwrt-15.05.1-ar71xx-generic-archer-c7-v2-squashfs-factory.bin into 6F01A8C0.img. The IP which the router is waiting for is 192.168.1.100 . If I have taken another one like 192.168.0.66 or so on the tftp failed.

Thank you dnkru for your replay that gave me the right direction.
