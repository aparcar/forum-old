wget -O /dev/null http://sonic9:xxxxxx@members.dyndns.org/nic/update?hostname=sonic9.dyndns.org

Even without -O .. 

root@OpenWrt:~/log# wget http://sonic9:xxxxxx@members.dyndns.org/nic/update?hostname=sonic9.dyndns.org

Try to remove wget.

Thanks nguyen, I have research in forum and this post http://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?pid=52046#p52046 has solved my problem.
I did "ipkg -force-overwrite install wget". Now works.
