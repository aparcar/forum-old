The good news is that the  wds0.49153 entries you see in iwconfig and the wds0.4915 (which is really the same wds0.49153, but ifconfig truncates the interface name) prove that your WDS connections are up.

If your "WDS interfaces" are up and things still aren't working, then the problem is probably that they haven't been added to the br0 bridge.

Post the results of the command brctl show

If you dont see the wds0.49153 in your br0 bridge, then the command to fix it is (IIRC):

brctl addif br0 wds0.49153
