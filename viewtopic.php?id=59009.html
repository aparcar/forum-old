I have TP-LINK router TL-WR1043N/ND v1 with OpenWrt Barrier Breaker 14.07 / LuCI Trunk (0.12+svn-r10530) configured as switch connected to ISP router and I created new VLAN DMZ for my Webserver. Router WAN port is disabled.

I can't get Webserver connected to VLAN DMZ to the Internet or ping it from my LAN or WiFi subnet.

LAN and WiFi can ping each other and access to internet.

Also I can ping VLAN WANVLAN 192.168.1.3 and VLAN DMZ 192.168.1.4 and ISP router 192.168.1.1 from WiFi 192.168.6.0\24 and LAN 192.168.0.0\24.

Also for Webserver 192.168.1.5 shows MAC 00:00:00:00:00:00 in Openwrt.

Any ideas are welcome. Thank you.

This is my current more visual setup:
