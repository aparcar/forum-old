a simple 7806 or 7812 voltage regulator with 2 22uf ceramic caps on it should be suitable for this kind of setup (just make sure you heatsink the 78XX

Goes like this:

Left Leg: 12-49V + Input
Middle Leg: Common Ground
Right Leg: Output 6v or 12v

Put one of the 22k's on leg 1 and 2 and another from 2 to 3

Simple, Easy and up to 1.5Amp Regulation

You will NEED the 22k's as they are basically a voltage trimmer for spikes. else a smoking openwrt box when you start your car


Edit: 78XX Comes in many different types
7803
7805
7806
7809
7812
7816
7824

Chose the one thats right for you.. and remember to have at least 1.5v extra on top of the regulation voltage or else you wont get anything from the right leg
