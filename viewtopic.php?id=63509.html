2 years ago I dealed with a d-link based on ralink chipset
after flushing openwrt "iw list" command shows nothing
system does not see wireless devices
found kernel patch that fixes broken device detection
after patching it worked. but wifi still was not stable and slow and I had to sell this router
not sure if its your case but with ralink everything is possible.
its not the best chipset for linux

run "iw list", search dmesg for possible errors from wireless
