cherry31459
17 Feb 2009, 23:39

If you have a Coyote board, then you'll have to select it in the kernel_menuconfig; the resulting openwrt-ixp4xx-zImage should then work; note that the current scripts won't generate an openwrt-coyote-zImage but that is not important. But make sure your HW is in fact a Coyote or clone, otherwise, it won't work.
