I've got OpenVPN running on a WRT54GS, and have found it significantly affects throughput.

With the WRT in client mode, throughput over wireless without any VPN was about 620kbytes/sec.
With an OpenVPN connection terminated on the WRT, and all traffic over wireless going through the VPN, throughput dropped to about 300kbytes/sec.

Looking at the CPU usage during this transfer indicates it's definitely CPU-bound.

I've also tried running a PPTP VPN from a client behind the WRT, and as expected, it had a negligible impact on throughput, as its not adding any overheads on the WRT, as the PPTP VPN was terminated elsewhere.
