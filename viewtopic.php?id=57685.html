for tftp recovery in wdr4300 its not working from all OEM images. Because  none of openwrt factory and tplink firmware images are accepted by bootloader. Its only possible way to recover use serial console.
tftp recovery mode fully works on these tplink factory images: 3.13.33(130617) (english version) and TL-WDR4300_V1_130506_RU ( russian version).
