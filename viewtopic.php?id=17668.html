looks like you didn't installed rootfs. here is how:

mount /dev/mtdblock2 /mnt  
cd /mnt
wget openwrt-ar71xx-rootfs.tgz
gzip -d openwrt-ar71xx-rootfs.tgz
tar xvf openwrt-ar71xx-rootfs.tar
rm openwrt-ar71xx-rootfs.tar
cd /
umount /mnt
reboot
