So, I recently moved and decided top set up my old router as a dumb wireless access point and LAN switch in my living room.My ISP supplies me with a router, and since they dont allow bridge mode, i have the old router as an AP. I used a 1 year old Open-WRT firmware that i had on the router since i last used it.

But now im running out of ideas.

There has been a lot of work on both ath9k and ath10k since Chaos Calmer was released.  Ath9k is the driver for the 2.4 radio and ath10k the 5 GHz.  These newer drivers require a newer kernel version, so they can't be back-ported to CC.

You could also try stock firmware.  Stock firmware is fine for running a dumb AP.  Just go in and turn the DHCP server off.
