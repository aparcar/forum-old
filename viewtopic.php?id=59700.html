Hi,

I found this thread:

https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=57170

Please read post number 8.  You'll need to cross-compile the toolchain to run on your OpenWRT router.  Use the search function to look for forum posts with "native build" or "native toolchain."  There may be better search terms so spend some time and effort on a search.  That could be very fruitful.  This may be too old to help but for completeness:

https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=4411

Unless the router is high-powered, building on it will be slow.  Since you have to cross-compile the toolchain on a workstation to do this, why not just build the firmware or pacakges or complete system on the workstation?  That seems easier than going through an extra, complex, slow step
