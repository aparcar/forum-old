Yesterday I installed bearDropper because the "authpriv.info dropbear" entries on the syslog were starting to get annoying.
I don't worry much about someone actually succeeding on the attack since I use encrypted asymmetric keys for authentication for the SSH service, the same goes for OpenVPN and I don't have any other ports open to the internet.
I woke up and there were ~80 blocked entries on the bearDropper chain... the list keeps growing.

I mainly post this to raise awareness about these issues and to ask: Did this happen to you lately? How are you dealing with this? Thanks for reading.
