Hey guys,           

our company devolo would like to donate some of our devices for experimenting with OpenWRT.

The  dLAN® 200 AV USB extender is based on the Moschip MCS8140 SoC, runs Linux and serves USB devices over the Power Line interface.

For detailed information see http://www.devolo.com/products/Smart/dL … er-com.pdf and  http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/devolo/dlan-usb-extender
