You blocking cookies, scripts or XHR requests with a browser addon?

Authentication sets a cookie that if not included in subsequent requests results in being sent back to the logon page.

If you have multiple instances of LuCI open (e.g. have LuCI open in a tab, and open a new tab and open LuCI in that as well), what you are describing will happen. Make sure you don't have multiple tabs/browsers open connected to LuCI.
