I have a Meraki Mini, with what I think is an incorrect board configuration.
Specifically, 'wlan' is set to gpio-7 and 'reset' to gpio-6. However, the external
LED is connected to gpio-3 and the reset switch to gpio-5. Manual decoding
(ar231x_platform.h:struct ar231x_boarddata) of the 'boardconfig' partition
verifies these incorrect settings.

I can think of two ways to solve this:

Edit the config, update the checksum, flash the partition. Fine, if it's just my
particular unit. But if I screw up?

Tweak (ar2315.c:ar2315_init_gpio_leds). Easy enough for me to patch and build,
but not for something clean enough to submit back. And then there is the issue
of deregistering the gpio%n to free up for other things.

I'm sure these aren't the only options. Suggestions?
