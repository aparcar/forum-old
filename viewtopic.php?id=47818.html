It could be powered on for 12hrs to 48hrs, and suddenly for no reason all the lights shut off on the router while it's plugged into the AC outlet. The power adapter works fine with my old Asus WL-520gu with no issues, so I don't think it's that. It's very intermittent. I have a self powered usb hub plugged into it with an external HDD.

After sitting for a half day, I'll supple power to the router again, and it powers up! 12-24hrs later it powers off again with the power adapter plugged in. It is plugged right into a surge protector that stays on 24/7. No other electronics plugged into the surge protector has issues.

The top should be nicely flat, not round like above.
