So, I've invested a fair amount of hardware into a platform and peripherals that hopefully meet the goals I had in mind for the optimal router.

I've started a github project for the setup and configurations-- I'm asking for help, make it easy for people to help, right? I will happily convert forum suggestions to repo updates for anyone not familiar with git.

It all started with a post on reddit about the best router I could get for $200-- what if you didn't have the constraints that many routers on the market do? Turns out it costs almost double to crazy go nuts. Be that as it may-- let's pimp this thing out in a software kind of way. I'm already trying to make this as public as I can so anyone else with a lapse of good judgement can follow along.

Well, thanks for looking. I hope there are other people curious to see what it can do.
