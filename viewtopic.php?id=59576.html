Basically, you should ssh to your router and then edit /etc/ser2net.conf

Add a port with your desired config (should match your device specs), or use some of the preconfigured ones:

For instance:
6666:raw:600:/dev/ttyUSB0:115200 NONE 1STOPBIT 8DATABITS -XONXOFF -LOCAL -RTSCTS

Like that, you can access to your serial device using your router IP and port 6666.
