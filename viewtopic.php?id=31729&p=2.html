Post #47
maurer
12 Oct 2011, 20:44

faramisimo, download from internet winscp, telnet the 703n and enter the command
passwd new_pass
from winscp connect to 703n to port 22, protocol scp (don't get scared by the initial warnings)
transfer to 703n's /tmp my build image http://ubuntuone.com/6ojc2uAmCOVR43Utw2q5pZ and close scp
now ssh to 703n with root and new_pass and enter the following command
sysupgrade -n /tmp/openwrt-ar71xx-generic-tl-wr703n-v1-squashfs-sysupgrade.bin
wait to finish and for device to reboot and you'll have luci (webinterface)
good luck
