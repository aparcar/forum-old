8Mbytes of memory

As far as I know, 16 MB of RAM is the minimum for any contemporary version of OpenWrt, and from own experience even then it's not much fun to work with. Although it doesn't say anything about flash size, the firmware image reports as 831 kB size. So it may well be one of those unsupportable devices with 2 MB flash and 8 MB RAM.
