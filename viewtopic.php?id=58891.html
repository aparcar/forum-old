All,

I am running CHAOS CALMER (15.05-rc3, r46163) on a TP-Link TL-WDR3600 as  the client AP

I am running a linksys WRT54GL with 10.03 loaded as the AP.

The TP-Link can see the other AP and connect. However cannot pass traffic..

It appears the config on the TP-Link is missing a few options to complete the binding of the
radio0 and eth0 or switch0.

Here's the config from a working 10.03 As a AP.

#################

network.eth0=switch
network.eth0.enable=1
network.eth0_0=switch_vlan
network.eth0_0.device=eth0
network.eth0_0.vlan=0
network.eth0_0.ports=0 1 2 3 5
network.eth0_1=switch_vlan
network.eth0_1.device=eth0
network.eth0_1.vlan=1
network.eth0_1.ports=4 5


network.lan=interface
network.lan.type=bridge
network.lan.ifname=eth0.0
network.lan.proto=static
network.lan.netmask=255.255.255.0
network.lan.defaultroute=0
network.lan.peerdns=0
network.lan.gateway=10.1.2.1
network.lan.ipaddr=10.1.2.5


wireless.wl0=wifi-device
wireless.wl0.type=broadcom
wireless.wl0.disabled=0
wireless.wl0.channel=11

wireless.@wifi-iface[0]=wifi-iface
wireless.@wifi-iface[0].device=wl0
