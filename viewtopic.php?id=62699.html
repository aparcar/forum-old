There's not a whole lot of difference between the 3 routers, and yes 2/ACS are identical in every way except the name.

OpenWrt is stable on all 3 versions, and performance is good. There are various issues, depending on who you talk to, but they are being tracked, and improved all the time with trunk releases.

Look at the wiki, and there are several types to download and try >  https://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/linksys/wrt1900ac

I recommend doing a lot of reading before flashing, and I also recommend you have linux experience.
