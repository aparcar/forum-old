This has been fixed in the svn version of 8.09.  I am not sure what the policy is for rebuilding images on the download site, but here is a post I wrote on how I got it working:

https://dev.openwrt.org/ticket/4952#comment:4

It has been working fine for me for a few hours now, and I have pushed multiple gigabytes of data through it.

Alternatively, without the svn patches, disabling the ability in busybox for ifconfig to report statistics got rid of the kernel bug problem, but then you can't ifconfig -a or ifconfig eth0, and that is a pain in the ass.
