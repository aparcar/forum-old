Hi

First, the "Howtos and Documentation" forum is where you post when you want to tell everybody how to do something, not when you want to ask how to do something.  (Although many people make the same mistake.)

Second, DD-WRT is not OpenWrt.  See http://www.dd-wrt.com/ .  They are more likely to be able to help you with the problem you're having.

Since you're in Cape Town, you might want to talk to these guys: http://www.ctwug.za.net/

By the way, the Telkom Mega 100WR2 is a Texas Instruments AR7, which is also supported by OpenWrt Kamikaze.  The last time I tried the wireless on an AR7 (which was a while ago and not a Mega 100WR2) it hung the box or didn't work.  Other people have had more luck with wireless on Kamikaze on AR7.
