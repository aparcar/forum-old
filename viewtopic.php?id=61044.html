I am using CHAOS CALMER (15.05, r46767) and https is working for me.

I installed luci-ssl, created symbolic links /etc/uhttpd.crt and /etc/uhttpd.key pointing to my cert and key, and restarted uhttpd. The .crt-file contains the complete cert chain (cert + intermediate CA + self signed root CA) in PEM format. (I made symbolic links since I already had my cert in another location used by strongswan.)

Uhttpd seems to be working fine. (Chromium complains about the cipher suit being obsolete, which might be caused by my cert which has a sha1 signature. My cert also doesn't match the local IP address of the firewall I am connecting to.)
