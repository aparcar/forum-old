At  http://wiki.openwrt.org/OpenWrtDocs/Hardware/Compex/ they just say 115200 bps.
Try my WP54AG settings:

Baud rate: 115200
Data: 8 bit
Parity: None
Stop: 1 bit
Flow control: None

or ask Compex support at support@compex.com.sg.
If you gave them your serial number box, they will provide the informations you need.
