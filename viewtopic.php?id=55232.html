Next step might be creating only the STA-interface on your client and no Master and try if it works. Connect a client to an ethernet-interface of your clientrouter, and check it out. Then configure both STA- and AP-Interface again and post your configfiles and logfiles

/etc/config/wifi
/etc/config/firewall
and output of dmesg and logread
