Hello,
I compiled OpenWrt Chaos Calmer r43055 / LuCI Trunk (git-14.294.77948-ecb0c2f) for Archer C7 v2.
I used scripts made by hnyman for WNDR3700 with little changes.
I found following issues:
1. Radios were not detected at the boot. Had to force detecting again by:
    removing /etc/config/wireless file
    giving command "wifi detect > /etc/config/wireless"
    and it worked
2. wlan leds not working.

Does anybody have a solution for wlan leds?

Googoled and found that ticket #18188 by morfik was reporting how to get the wlan5g led working and the lack of triggering for wlan2g.
Waiting for ticket resolution.
