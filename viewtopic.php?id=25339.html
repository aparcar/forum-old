Hi,

why do you want to put the AP into Ad-Hoc mode? You can just bridge the AP too...
Whether you can or cannot spawn multiple interfaces depends on the device and driver.

You can basically use an OpenWrt supported device, hook the DSL to one of the LAN ports and enable wireless.
The you can login to the OpenWrt system and run tcpdump etc. on the br-lan bridge which conntects wifi with the ethernet switch.
