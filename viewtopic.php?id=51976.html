Post #3
dabyd64
3 Aug 2014, 04:43

You can try to fix by replacing the small transformer near the WAN port. See the black part with the code "hst-1025dr"

That part electrically isolates Ethernet an the router, so its very hard to damage the electronics with a voltage spike.
You can buy it on Ebay:
http://www.ebay.com/sch/i.html?_nkw=hst-1025dr

However, sometimes a very big spike can damage the transformer and jump over it , damaging the circuitry.
