Usually, the model of a router is well written on the back of the router. You may wanna consult with the manual that came with the router or send an e-mail to Buffalo to inquire such information. Those are the easiest ways. A more complicated and/or harder way is to open up the router, identify the model from the board. If this fails, connect a USB/Serial console cable to its serial port and let it boot to see what model will be printed out through the boot process. In this case, you will also need to know the PIN configuration of the serial port.
