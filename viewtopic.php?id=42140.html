I downloaded the contents of the mtd5 = art from my WNDR3800 and uploaded here:
http://koti.welho.com/hnyman1/Openwrt/mtd/

I edited the art data for the first few bytes, which contain Mac addresses (3 x 6 bytes), WPS PIN (8 bytes), serial number (13 bytes), 2 empty bytes, then magic (29763654+16+128), router name(WNDR3800), WLAN default Netgear name and password.

I slightly changed Macs, serial and WLAN info.
Otherwise it is original stuff.

I also included the u-boot and u-boot-env mtd partitions, but I guess you have those intact.

root@OpenWrt:~# cat /proc/mtd
dev:    size   erasesize  name
mtd0: 00050000 00010000 "u-boot"
mtd1: 00020000 00010000 "u-boot-env"
mtd2: 00100000 00010000 "kernel"
mtd3: 00e80000 00010000 "rootfs"
mtd4: 00a40000 00010000 "rootfs_data"
mtd5: 00010000 00010000 "art"
mtd6: 00f80000 00010000 "firmware"

Ps. In case you are interested in wndr3800 Openwrt firmwares, you might look in https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=28392
