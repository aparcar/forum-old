Why not first do a make distclean to completely clean, i.e. delete/remove all the object/binary files, configuration files, etc., the root directory. Then, perform svn up and scripts/feeds upgrade to upgrade the source, and proceed to make package/symlinks, make menuconfig,  make kernel_menuconfig, and make to start the compilation process.
