Gargoyle can run on your WRT54GL but with limited FLASH/RAM you will not be able to install many of the cool optional packages available for OpenWRT.   If you run out of memory on your router you will experience reboots, loss of connections and/or the inability to make new connections.  The top end speed of the WRT54GL with Gargoyle is around 8Mbps through the WAN link and not strongly affected by RAM or FLASH size.  Top end speeds are strongly affected by the CPU in your router and the software you load (ie Tomato, DD-WRT, OpenWRT etc).

For you and a couple of roommates and a 5Mbps link I think you have a good chance of success with Gargoyle and the WRT54GL.
