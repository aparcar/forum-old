I do not think they are readily available, but the problem is easy to solve if you are buying a new router.  Just go for 16mb flash or something with a card in it. You can probably get by with 8, but why?

See the new ToH and sort by the flash column.  http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/views/toh_e … _supported  As of CC many 4mb devices are no longer supported off the shelf.  If you roll your own you may be able to get there.  I have an 8mb device and with a few adds (USB, VPN) still have about 30% free.

This sort is Supported and Available items.  Be careful, the table is not fully updated so there may be more Available products than in this list.  Just clear the filters to left.
http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/views/toh_e … ed?dataflt[Availability*~]=Available
