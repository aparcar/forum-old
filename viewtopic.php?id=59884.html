You need to read the WIKI
http://wiki.openwrt.org/start?do=search&id=openvpn
I think this is one of the better articles right now.
http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/howto/openv … rver-setup

You need to install the OpenVPN software.  AA and CC have Luci interfaces, BB does not. 
In CC: openvpn-openssl, luci-app-openvpn (note there are other ways to do this, but this is the basic packages)

You probably want to do some research on the OpenVPN site too
https://openvpn.net/index.php/open-sour … ml#vpntype

Then define your use case: 
Basically do you want to pass internet traffic through the device and/or do you want LAN access.
Will this be the primary router or a stand alone VPN AP?

And one of my favorite tutorials
Frog is Wrong
http://www.frogiswrong.com/blog/article … ore-detail

You may want to consider upgrading to CC 15.05 RC3 or final, though check very carefully on the AR711xx final builds.
