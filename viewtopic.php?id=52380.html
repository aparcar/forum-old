OpenWrt accepts .trx firmwares, you can easily extract .trx our of vendor .bin just skipping few early bytes (hint: dd tool). See http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/techref/header
CFE on the other hand should support .trx without any problem, see http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/techref/bootloader/cfe
