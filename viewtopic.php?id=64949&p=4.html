After uploading, I attempted to install luci-app-statistics.. However, the libpthread could not be found, but yet it is in the "core" group of packages. Unknown reason why it could not be found though the core packages seem to get registered.

Work around was to download the package from here using Firefox >  http://davidc502sis.dynamic-dns.net/sna … _vfpv3.ipk
