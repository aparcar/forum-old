and once again here:

This card has a 10dB tx power offset! Selecting 0 mW in OpenWrt will yield 10 dBm (=10 mW) output power, selecting 17 dBm will yield 27 dBm (500 mW) effective output power.
The power level reporting in RC5 and earlier was wrong, it simply showed regulatory limits and not radio capabilities.
