  • OpenWrt will detect and use only 128MiB of RAM. Not that hard to fix, but low priority

  • Poor BCM4331 performance with b43. Needs a lot of work.

  • So support for 5GHz in b43. Hopefully will be fixed soon (but mind the low performance).

The rest (booting, flash, Ethernet, switch, USB) is supported.
