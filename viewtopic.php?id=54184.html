Hello DAC_2020,

I tried something very similar - check my post at https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=54048
Will not help on the bandwidth logging, but give transparency on the Internet addresses being contacted.

Keep in mind that most WLAN router do NAT - so if you monitor traffic to your router you will only see the router IP, not the devices behind it. And small routers do not offer port-mirroring to capture all traffic.
