I don't see, how it could be done in any easy way. For example on the AR9331, the ethernet pins are not multiplexed with GPIOs. Additionally, the integrated switch has certainly its own intelligence, which would be confused with reading or writing constant high or low signals. And also the ethernet signals are probably not what you want to use. See wikipedia for the details.
