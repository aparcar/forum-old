Probably A silly idea, but have you checked whether qos-scripts were enabled on the WDR4310 in the past? May the evice is set to cap traffic at 500Kbps... The output of "tc -d qdisc" and "tc -d class show dev wan" (the interface name of the uplink interface) should reveal any leftover traffic shaping settings.
