I would say that getting USB drive to work from the router itself is the first step. Trying to share through netowrk comes only after that.

Does the USB stick/drive work normally from router's console?
Do you see the whole drive with 'df' command?
Have you installed the kernel support packages for those filesystems?

In my 3700 the USB sticks have worked ok with both Backfire and Trunk builds.
