Ok. Found something like here: http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/howto/dhcp.dnsmasq

copy/paste (with winscp) in
/etc/dnsmasq.conf

the opendns IPv4 and IPv6 servers:
server=202.67.222.222
server=202.67.220.220
server=202.67.222.220
server=202.67.220.222
server=2620:0:ccc::2
server=2620:0:ccd::2

then in Luci menu
"DHCP and DNS"/"Resolv and Hosts Files"
mark this: "Ignore resolve file".

Saved and Applyed




I have no problem with this lines in Luci's "System Log", i'm satisfied.
