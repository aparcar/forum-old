Could you please confirm or decline my conclusions about the device:
- it has owful stock firmware and to discover all advantages of the hardware it is a good idea to flash it to lede owrt;
- hdd to usb conversion works perfectly;
- hdd over WiFi works slowly at most speed of 1,5mbytes per second and only in open WiFi mode;
- if any of wep/WPA/wpa2 protection used hdd over air speed falls down to 15-40 kbytes per second;
- hdd connected to local wifi home network over WiFi (which has to be a wpa2 for security reason) is not functional as NAS;
So we still have a perfect sata2usb3 box with ability to act as separate unprotected network NAS (like taking it on a trip as photobank with wifi);
- as owrt based system it is very powerful in the range of available options but HDD WiFi limitations make them hardly useful
