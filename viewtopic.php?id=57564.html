28 May 2015, 18:47

I had this exact same problem two days ago. After messing around downgrading the Tp-link firmware on the assumption that they had put some sort of lock on the firmware upgrade, the solution turned out to be ridiculously simple:

It's the filename. Either it's too long, or it uses some forbidden combination of characters or something. Rename openwrt-15.05-rc1-ar71xx-generic-archer-c7-v2-squashfs-factory.bin to openwrt.bin and you're good to go.


Edit: looking again, it's almost certainly the fact that it has a second dot in "15.05" in the filename, confusing it into thinking it's not a .bin file. The barrier breaker images are named differently.
