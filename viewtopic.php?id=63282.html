Hi everyone

We’ve just released the “Reboot WRT” App to easily reboot an OpenWRT router at scheduled times, or with one button press on a mobile device.  We’ve found that rebooting a router can often solve intermittent networking and wifi problems, so we wrote this App to make it simple and automatic to do.  Please give it a try and send us feedback.
 
Currently the App runs on Android mobile devices (play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=n … reboot.wrt).  If there is enough interest we’ll release an iOS version.

How it works:
Step 1. When you first open “Reboot WRT” it will attempt to connect to your router.  All you need is the username and password for your router.
Step 2. Set the time that you want your router to reboot every night (e.g. every night at 2am).

If you need to reboot your router at any other time, just open the App and touch the "Reboot Router Now" button.
