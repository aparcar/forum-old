I actually purchased a BEFSR41 recently with intentions of hacking any of a number of useful functions into it.  I don't need wireless so I figured it would be perfect. 
   What I found, unfortunately, is that 99% (100%?) of the hacking info/tools out there are geared towards the WRT/WAP devices and that while it would technically be possible to hack the BEFSR41 it is sufficiently different from the wireless models that it would take an inordinate amount of time as one would essentially be reinventing the wheel.
   The wireless routers, conversely, have a large and active hacking community and plenty of useful pre-existing tools.  It will be well worth the <$100 you'll spend on a new WRT54GS for the hassle it will save you!
