Your initial problem is that you don't have internet, you could install luci offline from packages.

But the problem is that both your router(modem) from your isp and your openwrt router uses both the subnet 192.168.0
Change one of them to a different subnet and it will work.

Both routers on the same subnet, will simply create a route conflict.
