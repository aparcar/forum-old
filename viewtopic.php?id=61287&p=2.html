seems like here is your problem
heavily recommend you to finally get a serial connection to unbrick first two devices

try to flash breed u-boot, compatible with wt3020 board
https://app.box.com/s/4hecy7shljzuokj1lenf

the link above points to translated breed image (original one is fully chinese and lives here - http://breed.hackpascal.net/)
it provides web interface and dhcp in the recovery mode, also itll be easy to flash your router via breed web instead of serial+tftp mode.
If your third device is still alive, you can test it first on it. To flash breed try to telnet to your stock firmware
username: nexxadmin
password: y1n2inc.com0755

put breed image to the flash drive and insert it, then
# cd /media/sda/sda1
# mtd_write write u-boot-nexx-3020-7620.bin mtd0

u'll see

Unlocking mtd0…
Writing from u-boot-nexx-3020-7620.bin to mtd0… [w]

and after device will reboot

to enter recovery mode just press reset button before powering it up and hold it for ~5 seconds
after entering recovery mode your pc will get ip address from dhcp and web iface will be ready at 192.168.1.1

here what breed is looks like http://img.mysku-st.ru/uploads/images/0 … 575b33.png

BUT
be careful, mistakes made while flashing u-boot can be solved only by unsoldering and reprogramming
