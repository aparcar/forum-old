maybe a mtu issue with your double-nat

try setting (for the openwrt wan-interface)

  • in interface settings > advanced settings > override mtu 1440

  • in firewall settings > enable mss-clamping

if this fixes the problems, find out the applicable mtu for your connection (the buffalo router may now, otherwise ask google)
