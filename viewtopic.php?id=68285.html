The services tab will appear when you add something that creates it, which in your case could be Samba and the supporting Luci app for Samba.  It's not there by default.

You also may want to install the mini-DLNA which will establish the device as a DLNA server.  There is a you-tube video on installing this that's pretty good.  Search the WIKI too.
