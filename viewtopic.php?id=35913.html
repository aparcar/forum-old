It is SquashFS, so you should have failsafe mode.
You are not brick yours, faaaar from it !
You've just blocked the access.

In openwrt, the reset button won't reset settings (by default).
And all buttons has null functions except you define your own.

To obtain failsafe mode, reboot your router (unplug and plug power)
when power LED flashing, press Reset button, power LED will flash very fast,
then TELNET to 192.168.1.1, use mount_root, then change your /etc/config/network
In failsafe mode, there is NO webui.

Openwrt doesn't come with webui by default, (except custom build)
don't you remember ?
