The 2.4 GHz vs 5 GHz thing is interesting. My router has both, both are the same strength when broadcasting.

I wish more of my devices were 5GHz capable because:
*I live in a flat so I don't have range problems
*Data transfer is much faster for 5GHz. I'm lucky enough to have excellent download and upload speeds, so the wireless transfer speed is the bottleneck for me - I get better speed test results on 5GHz on the same device.
*Every other flat has a wireless network, as do some of the shops. There are TEN networks crowding the 2.4GHz space but 5GHz is completely clear.
*Being a flat, with so many kitchens and bathrooms around, the walls are full of water pipes. These are a pain for 2.4GHz but don't seem to troll 5GHz so much.

Clearly, I'm not a network specialist or a developer, but for me choosing which frequency to use IS a big deal because it makes a big difference. A mod disagreeing with me wouldn't change the observable facts in my flat. Everyone should have a voice, the whole point of the forum is for people to help each other. When people read a thread, they don't chose a single post to believe and discount the rest, they use the whole conversion to inform themselves. They shouldn't have deleted your posts, or twisted the conversation to make themselves look better.
