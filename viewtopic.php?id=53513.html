* TP-Link TL-WDR3600
* TP-Link TL-WDR4300
If I understood the wiki correctly they should have almost the same hardware except the "wifi stack".
In particular: the former (which costs less) is 2T2R and the latter is 3T3R.
Of course I would expect the WDR4300 to outperform the WDR3600 when speaking about wifi range/speed.
While I was able to find a lot of people online who are really happy with their WDR4300, I've also found a lot of people unhappy of its wifi performance.
Finally I've found this [http://us.hardware.info/reviews/3423/7/ … -10-metres] benchmark that seems to confirm that the TL-WDR3600 is actually better than TL-WDR4300.

1) Does anyone here own one of those devices?
2) What is your opinion about them in general?
3) What is your opinion about their wifi?
4) Do you think that their wifi will work in different rooms of the same house with robust brick/concrete walls?

I'm also open to other suggestions about different models/brands with GigaBit and 802.11abgn_simultan (1 usb port is a plus but not strictly required).
