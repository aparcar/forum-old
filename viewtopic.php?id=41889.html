I've been very happy with the DIR-825 rev B1 and B2.  I've got about four of them in total, and have used them in both station and AP modes, including simultaneous station on one radio and AP on the other.  However, as far as I can tell, radio 0 can only do 2.4 GHz, and radio 1 can only do 5 GHz.  Apparently in some (but perhaps not all?) units, radio 1 is configurable for either 2.4 or 5 GHz.

I have installed some optional OpenWRT packages on mine, and an getting close to the 4 MB flash limit.  I plan to move to a USB flash, but haven't done it yet.

I'm really hoping to see OpenWRT support for the Asus RT-ACC66U router, as it would be a great upgrade from the DIR-825.
