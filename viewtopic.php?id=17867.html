I've got an 7207Apg... might be a similar design.

The only 8-Pin device near the power section is a PJ34063 made by PanJit

Searching this on google gives: http://www.digchip.com/datasheets/downl … er=PJ34063

But searching AP34063 gives: http://www.digchip.com/datasheets/downl … r=AP34063A

Which seems to be the almost same device, but made by Anachip Corp. instead.

Seems to me that highly likely your toasted part is an AP34063.

Hope this will help you somewhat...
