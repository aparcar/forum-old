I resolved my own issue. 

I had installed the kmod-ipt-nat-pptp package which I had read about from other similar posts, but it didn't seem to make a difference, so I posted here.  However, I realized after posting that I hadn't rebooted the router after installing the package.  Once I rebooted the router, everything now appears to be working and I can successfully connect to the VPN that I couldn't previously.
