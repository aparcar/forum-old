Post #4
robrob
29 Jul 2007, 17:16

The OpenWRT developers haven't been able to get the wireless working very well with the 2.6 kernel, so use the 2.4 unless you change your radio card to something other than Broadcom or don't use the wireless at all. A lot of us are using Atheros a/b/g mini-pci cards in their WL500gP and using the 2.6 kernel.

Rob
