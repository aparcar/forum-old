169.254.0.0/16 are addresses assigned automatically by your computer if it's configured for DHCP, but can't obtain a DHCP lease from a server. Which means when the router is booted normally, it is unreachable from your local network for some reason, so there's probably something wrong with your settings, since failsafe work.

Please configure your router as desired, and if the problem reappears, boot into failsafe and post your /etc/config/network and /etc/config/dhcp, for starters.
