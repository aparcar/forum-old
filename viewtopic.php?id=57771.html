Launch make menuconfig. Then, go to sub-folder LuCI. Under the sub-folder LuCI, go to sub-folder Collection to enable luci and/or luci-ssl. After that, you can enable any other sub-folder, i.e. Modules, Applicationst, etc.

The LuCI files where present under openwrt/feeds, but the LuCI subfolder was not displayed in buildroot.

After updating the feeds with ./scripts/feeds update I could install with ./scripts/feeds install luci.
