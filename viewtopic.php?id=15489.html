It's my understanding that Openwrt does not support any 11n functionality, but the madwifi.org guys are working on it for Atheros based wifi cards. So I would recommend an Atheros based card that may or may not function as a a/b/g card for now, and may get 11n support in the future.

Bottom line, you may want to hold off on an 11n card, and get a cheap $30 Atheros a/b/g card to hold you over if you can't wait.
