Post #48
rviktor
11 Jul 2012, 23:03

If you didn't overwrite the bootloader, try method 2 of this: http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/howto/generic.flashing .
If you succeed in getting back openwrt, DON'T try to flash the 4300 firmware to the 4310, the fw's have differences.

If you overwrote the bootloader you most likely have to burn the flash by desoldering and using a burner that works with the Spansion FL064KIF.

OR you might use your warranty, if you have. (just don't mention what you did)
