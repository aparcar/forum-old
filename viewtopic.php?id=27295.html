Post #7
MarkMentovai
12 Nov 2010, 16:49

You’ve got radio1 configured to use channel 9, but that’s not a channel it supports. radio1 is used for 5GHz, and channel 9 is a 2.4GHz channel. iw phy1 info showed you the channels that this radio supports, they’re the bracketed numbers in the “Frequencies” section.
