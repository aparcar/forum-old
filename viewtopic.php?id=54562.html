Weird, thought I posted a reply to this....

Anywho found a fix to get up and running. You need to take the latest Raspbian and take all the files from the boot folder and put them in your openwrt's boot folder... files to copy over are as follows:

bootcode.bin
fixup.dat
fixup_cd.dat
fixup_x.dat
start.elf
start_cd.elf
start_x.elf
