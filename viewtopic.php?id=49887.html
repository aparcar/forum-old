OpenWrt AA with base asterisk18 package (which consists of chan_sip) should fit in 4 MB Flash, but I can assure you, that it will not fit in a router with 2 MB Flash.

=> at least 4 MB Flash, at least 32 MB RAM and base asterisk18 package is the way to go
