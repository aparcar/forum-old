For ever.

Link goes down, link comes up, start pppd, link goes down.

There's nothing useful in the logs that I can find but I am not at all familiar with pppoe so I don't know what to look for. I posted an image at http://www.seiner.com/cz/pppoelog.jpg

For those of you running PPPoE, can you shed any insight into this?
