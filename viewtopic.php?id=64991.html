There is very little information on this device https://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/trendnet/tew-692gr and I'm guessing that not much if any testing has been performed on it.

The logs seem to show that the relevant hardware is being detected, but as you see there are errors, and there is no reference as to what these error mean, at least not that I have found. I have a device that has errors and I've not found or had any answers.

Unless you are lucky enough to find someone else that has one running successfully then your best chance is to open  a ticket. https://dev.openwrt.org/newticket

I assume that you have made no other settings changes than enabling the wifi?

You may to consider collecting information like you can see on other wiki pages, take photos, links to factory firmware and the source code if it is GPL and available, factory firmware boot logs with a serial cable connected, bootloader type and version, confirm the chipsets, etc etc.
