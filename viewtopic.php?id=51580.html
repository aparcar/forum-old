Hi,

This worked for me in "14.04" to force 100 full duplex (had 100 half duplex on auto negotiation).
Install binary and put:
mii-tool -F 100baseTx-FD eth1
in  /etc/rc.local
-----------------------
mii-tool is deprecated/missing in "15.05" repo, so:
ethtool -s eth1 speed 100 duplex full autoneg off
