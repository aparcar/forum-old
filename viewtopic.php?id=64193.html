Use this command:
tftpboot 0x81000000 code.bin
erase 0x9f020000 +0x3c0000
cp.b 0x81000000 0x9f020000 0x3c0000
bootm 0x9f020000

Firmware without bootloader: ftp://tplink-forum.pl/orgin_bez_boot/wa … 30922).bin
