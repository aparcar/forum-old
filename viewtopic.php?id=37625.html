If your main AP has WDS capability, then your 350n setup a STA(client) WDS to connect to your main AP,
then in 350n, bridge other AP WDS together with the STA WDS ; you can bridge more than one AP(wds) if you needed, and also can use different ssid.
then those AP(should use wds) in your 350n will pass traffics to your main AP, as a result used as wifi bridge(different ssid) and repeater(same ssid).

If your main AP doesn't have WDS capability.
the 350n need to install relayd....... the configuration is a bit complicate.

openwrt wiki have the information.
