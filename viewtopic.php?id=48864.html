What firmware did you use? If it is a v1.x (router version is printed next to model# on the router), Attitude Adjustment 12.09 is what you wanted. v2.x Would be Barrier Breaker.
http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/tp-link/tl- â€¦ 043nd.v1.x

If typing your default gateway into your browser doesn't connect you to the LuCi interface, you should try using an SSH client to connect. PuTTY is a good option since it also supports Telnet (which is needed for booting in failsafe mode). From there, if you aren't familiar with a linux terminal environment, the OpenWRT wiki is your best friend. But if you are familiar with a linux terminal environment, then the OpenWRT wiki is your best friend. Still.

If neither method works, Failsafe mode is probably your last option. https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=38513 may be helpful.
