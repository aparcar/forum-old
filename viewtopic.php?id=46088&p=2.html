The solution is sooooo easy: extroot

- Gives you GB of free space instead of just MB
- No need to search for extra costly routers with 16/32/xxMB flash
- saves you hours of searching
- no need to use *that* special router with the right amount of flash for your packages, but with other features missing
- needs only USB stick + passive USB hub (active only needed if the USB power supplied by the router isnt sufficient)

I have >40MB in my rootfs on a DIR-505.

Get extroot, install *any* package you like, be happy.

P.S.: The power consumption of a passive USB hub + USB Stick isn't that much.
IIRC, a USB stick consumes about 35..75mA; a passive USB hub is around 25...50mA.
If my hubs weren't in use right now, I'd take a measurement for you, if you are really interested.
