Possibly but I have reflash other broadcom CFE based router without using serial at all..
Usually, there is a way to boot into CFE by holding on to a button and then manually configure your computer IP address to 192.168.0.x or 192.168.1.x

And then point your browser to 192.168.0.1 or 192.168.1.1
And just upload the ROM there.

Read the other on Openwrt.org pages on how other use CFE on broadcom based routers.
You will see full instructions there without using serial.

one example is this router I was playing with, read the section on "OEM easy installation"
http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/huawei/hg556a
