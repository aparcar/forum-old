This router is about two things shy of awesome right now.

I believe that I found this router briefly mentioned on the Wiki and that it is running some version of OpenWRT.  I am not clear if there is current support for this router or not, but Here are the issues I have with the device:

1) The only USB support the manufacturer included is some weird software that makes your USB drive act like it is a local USB drive over the wifi, but that limits you to being connected to only one PC at a time.  Pretty useless for the purposes of a networked drive.

2) IPv6, the only options for IPv6 are a tunnel to Freenet6.  Then it is limited to three options in Canada, Australia, or Amsterdam; that is all but useless to me in the states.  Comcast in my area supports IPv6, so I would love to do more with IPv6.  The performance with their options is horrendously bad.

I'd love to turn the radio output up a little too, but I would be happy if I could just fix those two things.  I have tried the manufacturers website support contact form and forums, neither have yielded anything.

Suggestions?
