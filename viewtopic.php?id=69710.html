stangri

If you can enter failsafe -- there's no password required for ssh in failsafe mode. You can reset your password once you ssh into your router in failsafe mode.

PS. Do not press and hold the button -- rapidly press and release it during the bootup until you see one of the LEDs (forgot which one) flash rapidly. You can then recover your router.

PPS. Make sure NOT to flash stock firmware with _boot in the filename!
