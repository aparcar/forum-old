You need to be sure of a few things:
Tx-Rx (router to connector, and vice versa)
voltage is only ever a rough guide to what does what. It does not help with connection issues
RESISTANCE between your connector (away from the soldered bits) to your router (again, away from soldering areas) says lots about how good the connection is. To measure this, you don't need power to anything, just use the multimeter.
