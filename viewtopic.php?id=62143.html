I have been looking for one of these for a while (with battery).  I am not aware of any product that has a battery, can hold an internal SIM card, and run OpenWrt .  The closest thing is the Hame19, also sold as an Aukey.  Some others have been hacking at it, though I do not believe it has any official software.  Unfortunately it's on 4mb of flash makes it challenging space wise in the CC world. 

GLI is working on one, but I would not expect it in the near term.  You can find it here. http://www.gl-inet.com/product/mifi-engineering-board/ You also need a 3g or 4g board and a battery, but it will run from 5V pretty easy.  There is no case for it.  You would need to inquire about the exact support it has today.

More info here: http://www.gl-inet.com/m9331-mifi/
