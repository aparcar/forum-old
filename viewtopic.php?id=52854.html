Routed client (Masquerade) requires no additional software, and will give you a NAT-ed connection. Routed client (relayd) requires that you install relayd, no further packages are needed. I recommend you try relayd if there is room for it. I take it this is for a smart-TV-like device, and as most of them (I believe) can stream video from local video servers (like computers, tablets or smartphones connected to the wireless network) it will work with no changes needed.
