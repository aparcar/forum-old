Hi,

You can reconfigure bridge by adding WAN to br-lan but simplier way is to plug uplink to one of the LAN ports.
After that disable dhcp server on wrt: in /etc/config/dhcp:

config 'dhcp' 'lan'
        option 'interface' 'lan'
        option 'ignore' '1'
