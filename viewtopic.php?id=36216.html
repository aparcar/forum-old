Hi All,

This is my first post here, so please go easy on me I've been running DD-WRT for the longest time on my Asus WL520GU, but I wanted to give openwrt a try. However, I am unable to get DHCP on my WAN connection. Here are the steps I ran through.

1. Unplugged cable modem (Comcast)
2. Flashed kamikaze brcm47xx 8.09.2
3. Plugged cable modem back in
3. Router Reboots, and I'm able to get into openwrt GUI, but no DHCP on WAN connection

Now I flashed back to DD-WRT, and all worked fine. I then tried to flash to openwrt backfire 10.03.1-rc6. Ran into the same problem, no outside connection.

I have a second router that also has DD-WRT on it that isn't really used (a Buffalo WHR-G54S). I flashed backfire 10.03.1-rc6 on that, power cycled everything, and bam I'm able to surf and get out. I'm stumped at this point with the Asus WL520GU. Can anyone think of what could cause this issue with a brand new factory flash of Openwrt?
