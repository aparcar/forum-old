I've had no issues with mine (v1.6), and I upgrade it all the time (I'm on trunk). Have you checked that it doesn't respond to anything (ping, SSH, telnet, HTTP, HTTPS)? Also, when installing your image, are you using the -factory or -sysupgrade image? From OpenWrt to OpenWrt, use sysupgrade. From stock TP-Link to OpenWrt, use -factory. Try not preserving configuration on a sysupgrade.
