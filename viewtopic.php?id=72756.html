slh

g1235813 wrote:

in other words : what am i missing?

The age of that device.

Software isn't getting smaller over time (new features, changing environments, bugfixes, etc.) - and the limits are shifting depending on your use case and the exact configs used for building OpenWrt. Please read https://lede-project.org/meta/infobox/432_warning for details.

The formal cut-off for devices with 16 MB was 2012 (in practice those devices already ceased to work in a reasonable fashion well before).
