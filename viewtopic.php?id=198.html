you know, I have flashed 5 wrtgs's now, and I swear each one of them seemed like I had to different stuff to get them flashed....it seems some sort of combination of unplugging it, and holding on the reset button for 10-15 seconds and, plug the power in(while still holding down on the reset) and hit enter on the tftp xfer. I'v also held the reset button for 20 seconds after its powered up and gotten it to take an image -lol

I think it helped me to have a ping -t open on a seperate dos window. you will see it returning a couple of pings and then time out before resuming after its booted up....those couple of pings are the magic window I think

try the reset thing, after making sure boot_wait=on of course

keep screwing with it, it took me 3 hours to get one of my wrt54gs's flashed, and the next one i did took 5 minutes -lol
