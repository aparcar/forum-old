Hello,

I'have just upgraded my wndr3700v2 to Chaos Calmer Final Release coming from  Trunk BARRIER BREAKER (Bleeding Edge, r39218) ... it worked very good.

I'am using vnstat and some other statistic tools - they save their results in /var/... and /var is linked to /tmp - so every time i reboot the device (dayly) the statistic get lost.

is there an easy way to link /var to an external storage like an usb-stick connected to the wndr so statistical data remains after reboot ?

Thanks

Post #2
tmo26
12 Sep 2015, 12:35

Edit DatabaseDir in /etc/vnstat.conf to point to your flash drive. You may also want to modify SaveInterval to a larger value (the default 30min is still a good value) to minimize writes to flash.
