Yes, there are some problems with latest OpenWrt on Ubiquiti devices. Sometimes it boots OK, and sometimes it hangs somewhere. To know exactly what happens we should try serial console connection and dig into kernel messages.

You can try to unbrick your devices using TFTP and original firmware. Here is the manual that suits for most of their devices: http://dl.ubnt.com/LS-Recovery.txt
