OpenWRT is probably dead. They might do a DD release some day, but you have to ask what the release objectives were in the first place. There wasn't any. They just pooped out releases whenever a couple of the devs were happy with progress or people complained that it had been 1.5 years since the last release and they felt forced to do something.

Most of the development is now going on in LEDE. However, they are not doing a good job of bringing over the end-user community. They don't have a user mailing list, message board, release statements, wiki, etc etc. That's something they need to fix. However, the LEDE builds are unstable enough right now that it's not worth bringing the user base over, so everything is kind of in limbo right now.
