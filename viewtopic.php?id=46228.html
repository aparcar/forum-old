I have configured my TL MR3020 as a wireless client (IP 192.168.0.250). This works fine and I can connect to the device wirelessly if I am also connected to the LAN with a wired connection (IP 192.168.0.254). If the wired connection is not plugged in, I can't connect to the device through wireless. If I disable the wired connection through Luci and then disconnect the LAN cable, wireless connectivity works! I'd like the wired connection to work without having to disable the wired connection manually everytime I reboot the device. Any ideas of how I can get wireless access to the device without having to have a LAN cable plugged in when I reboot?

Problem solved....
Changing the ip address of the LAN interface to a different subnet fixed the issue:

config interface 'lan'
    option proto 'static'
    option netmask '255.255.255.0'
    option ipaddr '192.168.3.254'
