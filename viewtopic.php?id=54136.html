ALL WORKING

Thanks for all the help

Basically, and I am sure the is the easiest thing, but no amount of searching on here or google helped, just trial and error whenever I got ten minutes free at home!

To run the package, I needn't of put "vncrepeater start" from SSH window logged as root, but on its own "vncrepeater" is all that's required then you can also see the output log of the repeater similar to watching a log window in windows.

And although running "vncrepeater start" reported not being able to read vncrepeater.conf (in /etc) which I edited to 5901, running "vncrepeater" reports no such issues.

For noobs when editing vncrepeater.conf ports, use shift: then "x" to write out changes.

Also I tried forwarding the two required ports to 127.0.0.1, no joy, so went back to 192.168.1.254 and all fine, works very well, so a happy man.

I hope this helps someone else searching in this forum.
