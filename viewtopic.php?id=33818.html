NVRAM usage on OpenWrt 'WhiteRussian' - OpenWrt Wiki

All ports lan (vlan0):

vlan0ports="0 1 2 3 4 5*"
vlan0hwname=et0

lan_ifname=br0
lan_ifnames="vlan0 eth1"
lan_proto=static
lan_ipaddr=192.168.1.1
lan_netmask=255.255.255.0
wan_ifname=
wan_proto=none

Untested.
