What you mean by "official" and "pre-compiled"?
Current trunk snapshot? Attitude Adjustment rc version? Or the ancient Backfire?

Compiling it by yourself enables you to include the additional packages you want. When compiled into the firmware, they take less flash space than if they are added later.

As far as I know, the 5 GHz is quite normally available in 3700. (My own build can be found at: https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=28392 )
