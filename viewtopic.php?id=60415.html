Hi.

Just wanted to report that the Belking netcam F7D7601v1 works perfectly with the Wansview NCS601W images (flashed Chaos Calmer and snapshot).
When looking for the USB id of the wemcam I found a picture of a pcb on this site: http://wanda25.de/wansview_ncs601w.html .
It looks very similar to the one in the Belkin, and the ram & flash sizes match.

With the stock firmware you can get root as described over here:
http://sheldor.blogspot.nl/2014/01/open â€¦ shing.html

The only snag is that the webcam does not seem to support VIDIOC_S_FMT.
But I worked around that for now by piping the output of v4l2-ctl through ssh into vlc on my desktop.

Hope this helps.
