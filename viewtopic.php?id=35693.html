I wanted to buy TP-Link TL-WR1043ND because of the price but I needed a vertical positioning. So I bought WNDR3700 but it was too tall and it wouldn't fit into my self made shelf. Finally I bought a used and cheap buffalo WZR-HP-AG300H that fits perfectly and it has external antennas : )

ps you shouldn't expect much from buffalo and wndr3700 models as they are mips 24kc soc routers and they don't even have a floating point unit. They are just fine for routing purposes.
