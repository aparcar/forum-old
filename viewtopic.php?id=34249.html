DWL-2100AP is WiFi access point meaning to add wireless network to your existing one. It can't make/dial a PPPoE connection.
You need a wan router or PC that triggers the PPPoE connection and your DWL attached to it.
That way you will be able to connect wireless clients to it and all have LAN/WAN access.
