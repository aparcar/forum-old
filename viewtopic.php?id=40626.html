Yes, 192.168.1.1 is default. Whether you can connect to this IP or not depends how the router is connected itself.

192.168.1.1 should be accessible if you connect your computer directly to the router's LAN port. Do not connect anything else for this first attempt. This will ensure that openwrt firmware is working fine. If you can connect via browser or telenet the router then you have to look into network configurations for internet part.

//ali
