How do I have to configure the WRT's Smb.conf?

Post #2
forum2006
5 Feb 2007, 17:58

Edit /etc/samba/smb.conf and replace 'writeable = no' with 'writable = yes' and add 'valid users = root' in the [tmp] section. Next, add the 'root' user to /etc/samba/smbpasswd with 'smbpasswd -a root'. Finally reboot.

Btw. there is a typo in /etc/samba/smb.conf (line 17). You may open a ticket and tell the developers about it.
