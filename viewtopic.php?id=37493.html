root@OpenWrt:~# vpnc --debug 3
vpnc was built without openssl: Can't do hybrid or cert mode.

What am I doing wrong?
Is there any alternative to vpnc for Cisco EasyVPN?

Post #2

14 Jun 2012, 11:16

do you need hybrid or cert mode? if not just remove/disable "IKE Authmode hybrid" it in your config-file.
The normal client configuration with username/password and group-id/group-secret works just fine.

if you need hybrid or cert mode look at the Makefile and try to compile it yourself with openssl-support enabled

or check this out
https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=24252
