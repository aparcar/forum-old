Most Ubiquiti stuff has TFTP recovery.  Hold down the reset button while turning the power on.  Keep holding until the signal LEDs begin to blink back and forth.  The router is now a TFTP server at 192.168.1.20.  Use a TFTP client on your PC to "put" a factory firmware to it.

Newer factory firmware may block the later installation of third-party firmware.
