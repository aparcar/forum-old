Giovanni wrote:

Check the wiki for the instructions on how to use the Image Generator here: http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/howto/obtai … .generate. It is very easy to learn (assuming you have some basic *nix knowledge) and gives you complete control of what packages goes into your image.

If you wish I could even give you a list of what packages to exclude if you wish to remove ipv6 completely. I just did that myself to save some very precious space.

Please clarify, what do you wish to do about the reset button? Turn it off entirely? (I would not recommend that). In that case, I suppose the simple & lazy approach would be to simply delete the script that is run when you press the button.
