Hi,

I am using a smart dns service from CactusVPN and wanted to have my IP address validate using CRON, they provide instructions for this using DD-WRT and I was just wondering if it was possible on OPEN WRT? I've included the website instructions below

Thanks :):

"To validate the IP automatically, follow these instructions step-by-step:

Open your DD-WRT router settings.
Go to Administration -> Management, and scroll down till you see the section “CRON”.
Add the following rule:
*/[x_min] * * * * root wget [VALIDATE_IP_LINK]
Important! Replace “[VALIDATE_IP_LINK]” with your Validate IP Link. To find it, sign in to the client area on our website, go to “Packages”, click on your package and find the box “Validate IP Link”. Replace [x_min] with the periodicity of the validate in minutes. For example: 5 – for 5 minutes; 120 – for 2 hours

An example how your rule must look like:
*/5 * * * * root wget http://smartdns.cactusvpn.com/index.php … wt853fd11f
This will validate your IP every 5 minutes."

crontab is identical in practically all Unix/Linux systems.
That should work also in Openwrt.

In Luci GUI the menu item is System --> Scheduled jobs

http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/howto/notuc … ka_crontab

actually that "root" section in you example looks strange. Might be a DDwrt peculiarity.
The line might be just: */5 * * * * wget http://smartdns.cactusvpn.com...
