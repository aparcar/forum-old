Wow, thanks to the amazing support of nebbia88 we were able to bring back to life the poor WR703N

He sent me a backup of his ART partition and we were able to reflash it in UBoot with these commands:


tftpboot 0x81000000 art.bin
erase 0x9f3f0000 +0x10000
cp.b 0x81000000 0x9f3f0000 0x10000
bootm 9f020000

It might not be its specific art partition, but wifi seems to work just fine, i'd attach the file here but i don't this it's possible on this forum...
