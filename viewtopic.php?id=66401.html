Start by listing all the packages you have installed, if any.

You will also need to understand what the drivers are to support your hardware.  Normally I would start here: https://wikidevi.com/wiki/Main_Page but the site appears to be off line.  Search for Linux drivers for your hardware.  In the gui, (system=>software) update your packages and then search kmod for similar drivers.  You should not need any Luci packages for wifi and not sure you will find any for your 3g.  You will probably need to configure your 3g from CLI, but I do not really know.  You may find the devices are not supported in OpenWrt, again do not know.

FYI, if your phone supports a hotspot, it may be easier to just bridge to your phone via wifi for your 3g.
