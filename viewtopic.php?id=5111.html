On Windows:

telnet (router) (ip)

On the Router:

nc -l -p (port) | /dev/tts/0

- helped set this up for a friend of mine who used it to drive a robot with his mind. He had a whole crazy setup with an EEG which would read brain waves of tense muscles or relaxed muscles; which, in MindPong (!) would be "up" or "down", for sentence writing "1" or "0" in a 31-charachter alphabet, or on the mind control platform, "forward" or "right" (no reverse... ).

The big problem we came across was having this run as a startup script. Only way we found in the frantic hours before he had to leave was to edit /etc/init.d/rcS and remove the output pipe to logger.

Enjoy,

- Yasha
