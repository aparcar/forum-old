Sure.  I haven't done it myself, but you should be able to compile on OpenWrt just like any other Linux box (if your platform has the resources needed).  Just make sure you have gcc installed (and any other required packages).  Also remember that you are limited on memory in comparison to a PC...  and if you're compiling on your jffs partition, you are subjecting your flash to a lot of writes.  It would be better to compile on an external device, such as a USB flash drive.
