Puyo:

You can upload one of the brcm63xx images from the CFE. I would suggest you look in the forums for a list of these images. The one corresponding to 6348GW or 6348GW-10 would be the likely image.

However, you will not have support for either the 3341 DSP or the SiLabs SLICs for VoIP. You could run either Asterisk, Freeswitch, or OpenSER but not with the PSTN ports on your box.

marc.
