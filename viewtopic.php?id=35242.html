TOTALLY handled my problem! thank you thank you thank you thank you thank you!

Just simply...
rm -f /etc/config/wireless
wifi detect > /etc/config/wireless

And abracadabra! The thing frakin works all of a sudden.

But it's a bit buggy and doesn't seem to hold the settings properly... I'm going to try putting the zd1211 USB WiFi dongle into AdHoc mode instead of AP mode, see if that works...

Again, THANK YOU!
