With 4mb of flash you do not have a lot of room to do much. 

Your choices are to RESET the device to factory which clears ALL the packages you installed (and config) OR to use the info you provided and Uninstall all the packages you do not want. 

https://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/faq/after.installation?s[]=opkg&s[]=uninstall
https://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/techref/opkg

reboot is not a solution.
