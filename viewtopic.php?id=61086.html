base is the core packages, located in the main Openwrt source repo.
The other directories are the "feeds", from which the other packages are sourced. luci, packages, routing, telephony  etc.
https://github.com/openwrt/luci
https://github.com/openwrt/packages
https://github.com/openwrt/telephony

The feed definitions are in https://dev.openwrt.org/browser/branche … nf.default
