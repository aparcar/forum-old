Post #7
masterdisaster
2 Jan 2012, 20:45

Power up the router and press the button many times (keep toggling) very fast.
The SYS LED (?) on my TP-Link1043nd starts to change the blinking frequency from slow to fast if it worked. I'm not sure if you get the same behavior with your router

The router should be now reachable under 192.168.1.1 and you have to setup  your networking card in your PC to a fixed IP in that range (example 192.168.1.2) because there is no DHCP.
