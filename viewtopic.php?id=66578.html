OpenWrt is technically capable of doing what you want, however your choice of device is not very good.  It has only 4mb flash which will be a struggle to load the packages you need to make this work.  I really would suggest that you look at other devices with 16mb flash and 64mb memory.  A faster processor will also help your VPN processing.

The table of hardware can help you find a more usable device.
https://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/views/toh_ … %5D=travel
(copy the entire link)
Gli devices come with OpenWrt preinstalled.

WISP is pretty transparent and assuming you are using a standard image and LuCi, goto Networking => Wireless and SCAN.
The most important thing is after you select a STAtion to connect to the the first prompt at the top of the page is a check box.  You must UNCHECK this box or it will delete the exsisitng AP (I did not write the code)

Search the wiki for both issues.  Here are the wikis for VPN.  I like the streamlined one...
https://wiki.openwrt.org/start?do=search&id=vpn

The Luci app for OpenVPN is not supported in 14.07 BB
