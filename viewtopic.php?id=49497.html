I don't know if this will and/or will not help. You may wanna contemplate for the EnGenius EAP-9550, instead. Currently, there is an entry in OpenWRT TOH for the EnGenius EAP-9550 which is based on a Ralink RT3052 chipset. Apparently, this RT3052 chipset seemed to be supported by the SVN trunk r32450 and/or newer. If you had the EnGenius EAP-9550 and also a serial/console cable, perhaps you can try to download/flash the RT3052 build from snapshot.

OTOH, you can look for JuhoSG here and/or the #openwrt IRC channel and ask if he can port OpenWRT to the EnGenius EAP300v2 platform. IIRC, his location is somewhere is EU.
