This problem isnt similar.
Can you please tell me whether or not LuCI's default Overview page refreshes every 5-6 seconds or so? And I don't mean "never finishes loading". The page has to successfully render and the browser shouldn't be stuck on "the waiting" phase.

Maybe the confusing portion of "auto-refresh" is being missed completely. The page doesn't itself send a refresh-request as one would do so by hitting F5 (Windows and *Nix). I mean the individual components within the page modularly refresh their contents (similar to using callbacks in desktop apps).

If someone's seen Tomato's interface, they should know what I mean, the page's content refreshes automatically every 3-4 seconds (except in tomato this is literally written at the bottom of each page, LuCI it doesnt say so).

Hope this helps.
