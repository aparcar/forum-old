hi,

do you really need WDS for your neighbour? If he has a different subnet, you could just use routed clientmode (with masquerading or actual routing), there is no advantage in using WDS for this interface. If the neighbour should only have internetaccess and no access to your lan make something like I mentioned here: https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=36689
