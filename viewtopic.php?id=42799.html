Your router moves around ?

If the main problem is how to address your router/server, look into "dynamic DNS" services which allow your router to register its address w/ the service so that you can always access it by DNS name even if their IP address changes.

Messaging servers/systems are already a pretty fleshed out space:  Look into servers using the XMPP protocol (started as the Jabber IM system but it's all IETF type standards now) and possibly the Jingle  extensions for voice and video.

But your message is not clear (to me, anyway) what problem you are trying to solve.
