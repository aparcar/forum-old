Have you tried putting keywords such as "openwrt isolation wireless clients"? You would have come across the solution.
Anyway...
Traffic on one subnet/network doesn't have to pass a router, it will all stay on layer 2 without passing any of the regular firewall rules/policies (iptables). You'll need a layer 2 firewall to fix such things manually -> ebtables.
However, luckily most wireless chips seem to support this isolation natively. See for example the answers on this page I found using Google and the keywords from your topic title.
