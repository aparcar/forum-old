kirschwasser
6 Jun 2013, 18:35

TR-069 is a cool idea. Like pdf is or UEFI or Secure boot.

Sadly the specs of TR-069 contain a lot of stuff, that the master can do. This unnecessary stuff ain't being used, because ISP has better things to do, than bullshit around with their property (which happens to be inside of you home and not just give you Internet-uplink but also manages your WLAN), but ISP could do.

Now thanks to FreeCWMP, you could set up a crippled TR-069 client, one that does not allow for all the unnecessary stuff. And this probably would work, since the ISP is not all interested in the unnecessary part of the specs. Also, they probably dont make their TR-069-master check if the unnecessary part of the specs are well implemented in the client and work as intended (by whom actually?).

However: that is not what the specification sais!

Fazit1: Afaik TR-069 is doable in OpenWrt. Yes, crippled TR-069 is doable as well.
Fazit2: Once you have bullshit specs, no FLOSS software can help.
