8 Jan 2012, 13:14

The EEPROM of the unit is programmed with US regulatory domain, hence the missing channels 12 & 13. Most of the 5 GHz channels require dynamic frequency selection (DFS) and transmit power control (TPC), which AFAIK are not yet supported in OpenWrt for mac80211 drivers. You can go around these restrictions by making your own regulatory.bin file (http://smorgasbord.gavagai.nl/2010/09/w â€¦ to-fix-it/).
