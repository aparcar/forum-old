After looking at the b43 website, this problem maybe due to the fact that with b43 and 2.6.24 kernel you are supposed to use firmware from broadcom-wl-4.80.53.0.  With b43 and 2.6.25 or newer you are supposed to use firmware from broadcom-wl-4.150.10.5.  Possibly this could be the problem?  I think the brcm47xx target is only using 2.6.23 kernel.
