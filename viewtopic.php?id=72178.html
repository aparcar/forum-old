If you have a Xaiomi in your hands now, there are some people building firmware for it (not sure what the difference between 3C and 3g is).
for example https://forum.lede-project.org/t/xiaomi … g/5377/106

Devices which are connected but not moving much data do not require many resources of the router, perhaps a few kB each for their data structures.  The default in hostapd is 150 devices but that can be expanded.  In theory, a wifi AP can connect 2007 clients at once but no one actually does that.
