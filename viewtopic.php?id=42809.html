Just to follow up I discovered the issue this morning. The 5v regulator was fine on the board, the USB port itself had a break inside of itself. Given I didn't have an extra part kicking around I carefully desoldered the USB port from the board, grabbed a piece of lead from a resistor and carefully fixed the part. Works like new and no one would be able to tell unless they pulled the USB apart.
