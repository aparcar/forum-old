I'm in the fortunate position that I have a bunch of public IP addresses at home. However, even at home I'm noticing that more and more devices want to connect to the network and require Internet connectivity. Therefore, I'd like to configure two separate SSIDs on my OpenWRT, one with NAT, and one without NAT.
Now the question: is this possible? And if so, how do I achieve this?

Post #2
FriedZombie
24 Dec 2013, 18:37

Tie one ssid to br-lan and the other to br-wan and you are done
The one tied into br-lan will be natted to the wan interface (as perdefault).
and the second on tied into br-wan will get ip's from the isp's dhcp server.
