If you can access the router at http://192.168.1.1 it is not bricked. I guess you see the original firmware login - not OpenWRT?

My guess is that the "parameter block" (or whatever it is called) of the flash needs to be reset. Search for "factory reset" or "hard reset". Perhaps: https://community.linksys.com/t5/Wirele … d-p/189115

In such case you need to reset the router ...
1. Press and hold the reset button for 30 seconds.
2. Then, unplug the power keep holding down the reset button for
another 30 Seconds.
3. Plug back the power back in, and keep holding down the reset button
for 30 Seconds.
4. Release the reset button.

Good luck.
