WWTK
28 Dec 2017, 16:16

making the assumption that your plex server is connected to the mr200 then, yes its double nat.

Your choices are to move the plex server to the modem, or place the modem in bridge mode so your mr200 gets the public address.

Double NAT is bad , the best solution is bridge mode on the modem.
