1) never connect anything to the 3.3 (VCC) pin of your router, that pin is to POWER something with the router, not really recommended, your usb adapter already powers itself from usb.

https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php … 38#p208738

2) you must connect the router with its ac power adapter! ^^

3) eventually try to invert tx and rx pins
