7 Sep 2016, 23:33

The C7 doesn't support VHT160, so the interface won't come up. If you would have the "old" chip from a V1 device the interface wouldn't work at all.

Did you try other channels? You can also try 149, 153 and 157 to get the same 80MHz channel but with another "master" channel frequency.

What's the distance to your plex?
