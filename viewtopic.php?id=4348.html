Post #2
wtzm
6 Feb 2006, 18:50

You need kmod-usb-uhci instead of kmod-usb-ohci.

EDIT: The usb bus that usb-ohci found, as seen in your dmesg, is the one on the broadcom chip ("pci"-id 14e4:4715), which isn't used on this device.
