Hi,

I have Attitude Adjustment 12.09 on a D-Link DSL-G624T (H/W Ver.: A5 with 32 MB onboard).

It works fine except for being somewhat "fuzzy" when it comes to supplying an ip address to my two tablets.

These tablets are one Mediacom Smartpad 101s2 with Android 4.1.1 and one Start101 with Android 4.0.3, while my OpenWrt router is configured with WPA2-PSK.

Actually they both seems to associate but then they remain stuck at "obtaining an ip address", until I switch off and on again the router and then they are able to connect fine. It doesn't help just turning off and on again the wifi on the tablets.

I have some other device that always connect fine, two iPod Touch, three Samsung Android 4 phones and one Samsung Bada phone, the problem only arises with my two Android tablets.

Could it be an issue with my router configuration or perhaps a problem on my tablets?

Many thanks in advance for your help.
