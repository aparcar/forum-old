So we get to horsepower and driver support. It seems like the 4900 has better driver support, and the PPC processor is a beast, but this is a somewhat hard to find item in this parts of the world.

On the other hand, market is flooded with Archer C7, but driver support seems to be of concern.

I have both (c5 actually which is same hardware)

For WiFi speed and range advantage (even if you only have N clients) then Archer C7/C5.

For processing power for VPN or faster WAN to LAN (NAT) transferw then WDR4900 hands down.

P.S. the ath10k driver is stable at least on the CC trunk for me.
