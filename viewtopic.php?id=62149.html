As far as I know, and as far as google tells me, I'm the first person to get openwrt booted on the Netgear WGR614v10.

Apologies if I'm behind the times. I've never even used Openwrt before, so I have more work to do


Step 1: (optional) Upgrade router to latest official firmware. I'm not sure if you have to, but it's what I did.
Step 2: Download firmware from this site. Filename is "openwrt-brcm47xx-mips74k-netgear-wnr-1000-v3-squashfs.chk"
Step 3: Follow instructions here: http://www.aisenlink.com/2015/05/14/wgr â€¦ part2.html to hack the board id of the firmware you just downloaded
Step 4: Disconnect from all other networks and connect to netgear directly through lan cable. (I have a linksys at 192.168.1.1, so I couldn't be on the internet and tinker at the same time )
Step 5: Use the neatgear web software to flash firmware.
Step 6: Wait a few minutes and try not to sweat too hard.
Step 7: Login through ssh and try not to get too smug

http://imgur.com/lvGcUyb

That's it really. I have no idea what to do past the ascii art and the drink recipe.
