Now I know, how to flash openwrt to dir-320 A1/2 correctly. Information in wiki about this procedure is not fully correct. Tftp-loader in this router can't accept any firmware image bigger than about 3,2-3,4 mb. I spent a lot of time to understand it. So I used old official image OpenWrt AA - it has a size of about 3,2 mb - just what you need.
After flashing AA 12.09 I update openwrt to my private build based on OpenWrt BB 14.07 and now my router works like a charm
P.s. revert router to stock before flashing is not necessary.
