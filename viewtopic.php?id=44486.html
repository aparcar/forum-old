Remote: that means (usually) you will need to set up a ddns. Do you mean remote as in not via the router, or remote as in outside your domain?
You need to clarify in your own mind and mine what it is you want! Sorry to be a pain, but you don't give enough info.
If you wanted to share files across machines at home, then maybe samba or something similar.
If you want access to files from home while you are out on the road somewhere, then you need a ddns solution (involving a web or ftp server).
If you want to watch the squirrels in your vegetable patch while at work, then you need ddns and some sort of streamer.
