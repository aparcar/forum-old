I have been using it for 4 months and I didnt had a problem while I was under win7. I have some computers, tablets and smartphones, and all of them are connected to the same router. So, I configured a QoS to give some prioritys and to have a stable connection. It was working very well, every single device was detected by the router and I could configure them without problems.

A week ago I installed Win8 and since then the router doesnt detect my computer. I have internet, but since the router doesnt detect my computer, the QoS isnt working well with this connection in particular, I have some lags when I play videogames online or the internet is going slower than usual. I have configured everything in Win8 as Win7 but I dont find my dispositive in the router interface.

Here two devices are detected by the router. One if them is a smartphone and the other is a tablet. Both of them are connected to the network by Wifi, but my computer doesnt appear and its connected by wire. I searched in the System log and the mac of the computer doesnt even appear, so is like the computer wasnt connected but I got internet connection.

Can it be a bug with Win8? I haven't seen anything like this.
