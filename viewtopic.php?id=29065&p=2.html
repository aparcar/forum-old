Got it working. Had to do this:

add "option macaddr xx:xx:xx:xx:xx:xx" to the corresponding "config interface" section in /etc/config/network

The macaddr is the Wan HW Addr reported in the Status window of the Stock firmware.

Got the idea from:
https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=14687
https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=27524
