I think files under "/etc/config" should be considered as "start-up config files", as those are the files using during start-up to configure the router. I do not think there are "running config files" in OpenWrt, but you can make changes to "/etc/config" files, and those changes will not be effective until you "commit" them (or reboot the device).

You might want to have a look at "http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/uci" for further clarification.
