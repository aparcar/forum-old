joe -

The point was to set boot_wait before loading on a third party firmware, so if anything happened you'd have an easy mechanism for recovery. Do we require that you use boot_wait and tftp? no - you can load openwrt directly using the webpage on the linksys firmware. I always laugh my ass off when I hear about people who flash with other 3rd party firmwares just to set boot_wait so they can reflash into openwrt.
