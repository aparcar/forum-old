I had situation when http://192.168.1.1/cgi-bin/luci
was not loading

It was due to the fact that I replaced one router with 192.168.1.1 address with another model.
Solution:
1) Clear all cache in Firefox
2) download and install Goolge Chrome, than launch 192.168.1.1 (address of your router) with Chrome.

Hope this helps
