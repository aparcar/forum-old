This is not quite the way to do it.

Presuming your media player is a physical machine you are on the right track... but you do not need to connect the WAN interface of OpenWRT to anything - just make it a LAN member and set it as default gateway on your mediaplayer.

If your media player is just a software application it gets more difficult.

I have not tried exactly the same thing myself... but I think that is the way to do it.
Just
- connect its LAN port to your normal LAN (it will get one LAN IP)
- install VPN client on it
- configure VPN as default gateway on your wr703n
- configure your wr703 as default gateway on your media player
