Just flashed Attitude Adjustment 12.09-beta2 /LuCI 0.11 Branch (0.11+svn9402) on my new v8.0. For wat I need it for it seems to be working great. Simple ppoe config on the WAN, bridged WiFi and LAN. I did have an issue with the bridging initially. VLAN tagging is enabled by default, and even though all ports were set untagged, with dot1q enabled, the wireless and LAN would not bridge. I could not ping the LAN GW from the WiFi.  As soon as I disabled tagging, things worked properly.

Wish this router had USB printing and fileshare capability, but for now I am happy.
Thanks for all the hard work!
