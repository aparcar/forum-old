I finally find them.
Cpu jtag includes 7 pins.
   7       6     5       4    3    2      1
TRST  TDI  TDO  TMS  X  TCK  GND
I use wiggler to connect it.
On the board ,the TRST is pulldown by resister,you should pullup by 3.3V. Pin3 is X .It may be the sys_reset.Ignore it.
