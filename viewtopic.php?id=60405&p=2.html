You can find the index URL to browse the packages manually from the OPKG-Configuration page (from LuCI: system>software>configuration tab)

For me its

http://downloads.openwrt.org/chaos_calmer/15.05/ar71xx/generic/packages/

Change "ar71xx" to match your hardware architecture. The logic behind the sub-folder layout is still a mystery to me, so good luck hunting down what you need!

The OPKG Wiki page also has useful command references.
