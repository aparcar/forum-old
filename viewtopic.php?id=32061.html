dcyli wrote:

You can and you can't. The modified busybox is saved in JFFS2 while the untouched busybox is in the squashfs and can be accessed under FailSafe mode. FailSafe mode can also be activated through serial console.

Linksys WRT54G, WRT54GL and WRT54GS - OpenWrt Wiki - Enter Failsafe Mode
