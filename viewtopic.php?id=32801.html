Hi Enrico,

If you have configured an OpenWrt buildroot you can try building 2.4.11 quite easily:

Just edit ~/package/feeds/packages/gphoto2/Makefile and specify the gphoto version you want to use.

There's no guarantee it'll work of course (any patches that are applied may no longer work, and some new incompatibilities may be introduced), but generally this would be where I'd start.

Hope this helps,

Sam
