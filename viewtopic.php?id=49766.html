Buy one with a wifi, and disable the wifi. TP-Link devices like WDR-4300 and ArcherC7 have a physical switch on the back that turns off wifi. Beyond that, and you don't have to screw in the external antennas.

Or you can do the smarter thing and disable the wifi on your modem, which your service provider is probably using parasitically to steal your power and network bandwidth to share it with your neighbors.
