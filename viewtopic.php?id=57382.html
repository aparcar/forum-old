Most dongles will work.  You can check the kernel wireless website (wireless.wiki.kernel.org) for more specific chip / driver info.  Knowing which chip is going to be in a particular model dongle is kind of a crapshoot.  Wikidevi may be useful.

Realize that the ethernet and wifi must bottleneck through the same USB2 pair of wires in and out of the CPU on a Pi, which isn't going to give you particularly high performance.  Routers may have less CPU power but they are optimized with faster busses to the I/O devices.
