Reverting to Original/Factory Firmware :

Model : tl-mr3420 v1.2 (r33312):

Step 1 : Download TP-link firmware here
Step 2 : Extract it to /tmp of your router (Use Winscp for windows i dont know how in linux )
Step 3 : In Putty  type this without quotes "mtd -r write /tmp/mr3420v1_en_3_12_21_up(120523).bin firmware"

Putty will output rebooting after the flash...Then you are done ( incase u hit 192.168.1.1 and luci/gargoyole/something pops up do this clear your browsers cache)

No need of "dd" step in wiki as the bin file ( factory image ) does not have a  "boot" word .
