If you're a complete noob at this why play around with fire in the first place...


What you can do is..
1. Unplug the SIM and plug your laptop in the LAN port and try and access it that way
2. Access the serial console via the CONSOLE port on the back of the router (most have one..)
3. Reset the router (last option, especially if its been configured to your internet details)
