My system log is spammed every ten minutes with a log entry for each wifi client that's connected to my NAT router running 8.09 RC1 e.g.:

Dec 17 01:07:55 OpenWrt daemon.info hostapd: ath0: STA xx:xx:xx:xx:xx:xx WPA: group key handshake completed (RSN)
Dec 17 01:07:55 OpenWrt daemon.info hostapd: ath0: STA yy:yy:yy:yy:yy:yy WPA: group key handshake completed (RSN)
Dec 17 01:17:55 OpenWrt daemon.info hostapd: ath0: STA xx:xx:xx:xx:xx:xx WPA: group key handshake completed (RSN)
Dec 17 01:17:55 OpenWrt daemon.info hostapd: ath0: STA yy:yy:yy:yy:yy:yy WPA: group key handshake completed (RSN)
Dec 17 01:27:55 OpenWrt daemon.info hostapd: ath0: STA xx:xx:xx:xx:xx:xx WPA: group key handshake completed (RSN)
Dec 17 01:27:55 OpenWrt daemon.info hostapd: ath0: STA yy:yy:yy:yy:yy:yy WPA: group key handshake completed (RSN)

Is there anyway to turn this off?

Here's the running process and args:

hostapd -P /var/run/wifi-ath0.pid -B /var/run//hostapd-ath0.conf

Here's the /var/run/hostapd-ath0.conf:

driver=madwifi
interface=ath0
hw_mode=g
channel=11
bridge=br-lan
ssid=<my ssid>
debug=0
wpa=2
wpa_pairwise=CCMP
wpa_passphrase=<my passphrase>

Thx,

-Eric
