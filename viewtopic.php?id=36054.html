Hi!

I corrupted the redboot loader while a failed recovery process.
Redboot did not work anymore, the serial interface was not dead.

I tried to recovery the redboot using the JTAG interface.
I am using Urjtag and a FT2232H based programmer (http://www.olimex.com/dev/arm-usb-tiny-h.html).

As redboot I tried the redboot offered in the openwrt-atheros section for the Nanostation2 (http://mirror2.openwrt.org/ar231x-redboot/; ubnt_ns2.rom).

As address for redmine I tried 0x1fc00000.

Unfortunately the recovery did not work.

So I can think of two things that went wrong:
- the address is not the correct for redboot,
- the redboot image is not usable for a Nanostation5,
- or both.

I would be grateful for a hint?

BR
Martin
