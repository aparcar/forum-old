I e-mailed you an explanation, but I wanted to take a moment to answer your post.

The short answer is this:

The APs should only carry the same SSID if they share the same ethernet address space, such as by bridge mode (see fyi's link to Dumb AP below).

If your WNDR3700 can do bridge mode, then this should work. If it cannot do bridge mode, you should configure three separate SSIDs for the three APs.

There are a lot of technical reasons for this. I won't bother to explain further unless someone asks me to. By all means, ask if you wish.

Otherwise, if you are satisfied with this answer I'll leave well enough alone.
