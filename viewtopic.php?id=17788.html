You can download the latest USR-5461 firmware from here. Once you have the firmware, you can flash it through a DOS command using TFTP, i.e. tftp -i <IP Address of your USR-5461> -put <firmware file name>. Your computer must be connected to one of the LAN ports of your USR-5461 unit.
