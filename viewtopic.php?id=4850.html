This is supposed to happen, and IIRC, it's in the documentation.

At first, the router accepts telnet from the LAN.
Then, when you change the password for the first time,

1 - Telnet is forbidden, and you have to use ssh to log into the router with your new password
2 - You need the same password to log into webif

Telnet is not secure, and I would use it just to log in that first time if I were you.

-- jp
