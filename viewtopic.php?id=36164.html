"The main menu page of an OpenWRT router looks great."

Google Chrome: 18.0.1025.152 m

"The main menu page of an OpenWRT router is messed up."

More specifically, the menu bar that contains "Overview" "General" "Settings" "Logout" ... "Reboot" options is shown as vertical list of words and all normal page content is printed way below that resulting in most of the page being the vertically stretched out menu bar items.

Known issue?  I found "Ticket #9008 (closed defect: worksforme)", but it's closed

Per the closed bug, clearing the Chrome browser cache DOES fix this, but it begs the question, why is that unusual action even necessary?

Because LuCI performs aggressive caching to lower the amount of hits on the web server. Due to this caching, the stylesheets are not reloaded until you hold shift and click reload or the browser cache is cleared.
