When you "join" it considers the other router your ISP, puts it on the WAN network, so you are routing from it rather than bridged.  This is because the regular AP-STA link is designed to have only one device as the STA.

If both routers were Atheros I would say to use AP-WDS mode instead, as then you could bridge your STA into the LAN on the 4300.  I do not know if that works on an R8000 since it has Broadcom chips.
