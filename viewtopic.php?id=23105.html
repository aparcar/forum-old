Hello Enrico,

I can tell you that that is indeed possible, works well and is not really hard compared to other applications.
Compared with a 'webcam', you'll get brilliant pictures even with a old 3MP powershot.

However, if you never used unix or something similar, you're in for a steep learning curve.
gphoto is the way to go, most powershots work, see their website for compatibility.

Then get the hardware, install openwrt, connect your powershot, and play around with gphoto.

You'll need to write a short script for the automatic upload.

I can answer specific questions on this.

Have fun, and good luck!
Jo
