In theory yes, in practise there needs to be "bug compatibility" between various vendor chipset for stuff to work correctly.
Go to dev.openwrt.org -> New Ticket and put the output of "killall -9 hostapd; hostapd -dd /var/run/hostapd-phy0.conf" there while your webcam tries to associate.
