solved .. can be locked or deleted

... the only thing I had to do was read adam2flash502T.pl again and again and again and finaly use it (with trunk branch).. though funny thing happened
"./adam2flash-502T.pl 5.8.8.8 -setmtd1 ../../bin/ar7/openwrt-ar7-squashfs.bin" couldn't find the image nor ... "../../bin/ar7/openwrt-ar7-squashfs.bin"
but:
cp ../../bin/ar7/openwrt-ar7-squashfs.bin openwrt-ar7-squashfs.bin
./adam2flash-502T.pl 5.8.8.8 -setmtd1 openwrt-ar7-squashfs.bin
was ok
