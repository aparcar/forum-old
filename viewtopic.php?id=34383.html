Hello wr1043nd,

if kmod-gre_2.6.32.27-1_ar71xx.ipk existed in /tmp, Shell (ash) should expand /tmp/kmod-gre*.ipk to /tmp/kmod-gre_2.6.32.27-1_ar71xx.ipk before passing to opkg.
As opkg reports that it could not find /tmp/kmod-gre*.ipk, probability it does not exists.

Double check its location and existence or just use "tab" to complete the path with no chance of error.

I installed local package files multiple times and, trust me, it works.
