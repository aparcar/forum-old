Please check that your card is compatible.

After connecting the card, login to your router with "telnet"

and check that there is folders under "/var/harddisk/"
You should see the folders in your card.

To access your router with "telnet" you need to change your router
saved configuration file telnet="disable" to telnet="enable".

Try also enabling the ftp in your router web page and see if you
can access the ftp.

If you can't see any folders in "/var/harddisk/" or if you can't access the ftp
probally your card isn't compatible.
