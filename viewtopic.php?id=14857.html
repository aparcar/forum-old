jeffster
5 Sep 2008, 23:01
ibrahim wrote:

What is the difference anyway between kamikaze and snapshot ? I see that snapsot is much more recent.

The snapshots are whatever happened to be checked in (last night) when the build was kicked off. Usually they work pretty well, at least to the point where you can get them off your box if they are "broken" in an unacceptable way.

The kamikaze folder is, I believe, built from the "tag" for 7.09, many, many months ago, and is missing a lot of the later changes and improvements.

If you want reliability and stability over specific features, you might be better off with the current version of the vendor's firmware.

If you want to "play around" with your router, then I'd go with the snapshots, and maybe, eventually, learning how to build your own.
