MMCM
28 Feb 2007, 11:29

I'd prefer a router with
- USB ports, without it, it may be you won't have enough storage in the internal flash to install all required software
- an atheros wireless chipset (currently broadcom wireless chipsets require you to use the proprietary broadcom drivers with a 2.4 series kernel)

The WL-500gP has a broadcom wireless chipset, but since it's a mini pci card, you could exchange it with an atheros one.
