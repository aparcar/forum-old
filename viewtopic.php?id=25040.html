I asked nbd, the maintainer of madwifi for OpenWrt and according to him the XR mode never worked properly. I am not sure whether it will ever get fixed since madwifi is slowly faded out in favor to ath5k and ath9k.

Edit:

Oh and btw, after just 20 seconds of google search for "madwifi xr mode" I found the statement below

Currently, madwifi is only supporting XR mode when operating as a STATION and not as an AP.

Source: http://madwifi-project.org/wiki/ChipsetFeatures/XrMode
