In your /etc/config/network
1.find string '0 1 2 3 5t' and change to '0 1 2 5t'
2.find string '4 5t' and change to '3 5t'
This manipulation move port3 of switch into another vlan (which act as wan). Don't forget connect your wan-cable into "LAN1" .
