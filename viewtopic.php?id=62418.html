2-3 years ago I tried DLINK DIR-620 with openwrt.
After compilation and flashing wifi was not working. 'iw' did not see any 'phy'.
Read some forums and found kernel patch. Commented some lines in ralink wifi driver, recompiled, flashed again.
Wifi was operational that time.
But wifi was not stable. Speed is low, often hanging..
I had to sell this router and bought another one on atheros chipset. Atheros is the best, no problem with it.
Since then I avoid ralink routers at all cost.  Ralink drivers in linux are bad.
USB ralink dongles also work very bad with linux, hang kernel sometimes.
