In the guide, 'client' almost always means the box requesting the OpenVPN tunnel, and 'server' means the box listening for that request. 

In the guide, the server is invariably an OpenWrt Router, but the client could really be any box capable of running OpenVPN.

I tried to make the distinction clear in the guide, without being too wordy.
