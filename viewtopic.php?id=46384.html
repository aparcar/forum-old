Don't know what the current status is, but I have bought TOMTOP usb hubs from ebay (and I think 4-port octopus) and they have worked with the 703N.  That is, they report "high speed" in dmesg rather than the "full speed" which won't maintain a reliable serial connection (when wifi is used). 

This was 9 months ago, but they could have regressed.  I don't know of any Canadian retailers who would have these and who would provide certainty that you were getting what you need (and you'd probably pay 5-10 times as much).  Buying usb hubs from Nova Scotia, I purchase on ebay and hope, but find that about half of the ones advertised as "high speed" are in fact "full speed".  I've gotten refunds on the ones erroneously labeled.

Another hub I recently tested (no longterm experience yet) and found OK: DeLock 87445
Cheapest source in Germany: Reichelt, 4,95 Euro

Goodie: This hub can be powered externally (active/self powered hub) and has per-port-power-switching, which means: you can turn off the power for each single port by software (hub-ctrl.py or hub-ctrl.c).
