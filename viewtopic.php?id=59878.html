Hello ds2k5,

I don't own the device by myself but i think you got the wrong firmware file.
I think even the wiki is wrong on that topic..

As seen by your serial console log, you flashed the sysupgrade file.
I used to flash factory firmware files when doing recovery via tftp and serial.

So you should try to do the same. As long as you don't mess with the addresses nothing SHOULD go wrong.

If you success, could you please share it with us so someone / I can update the wiki page.

Good Luck
