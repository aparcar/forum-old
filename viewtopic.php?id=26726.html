There are four of these connectors,I think 2 for each chip and 4-4 internal antennas.In openwrt WNDR3700 wiki there's a picture of the device,I'm now testing the following : soldered a pigtail to the connector closest to usb port,connected an 8 dBi TP-Link ant to the pigtail,applied a patch to mac80211,with that I can control antenna configuration through /proc.Signal is increased a bit,but it's still not what I expected.
Here is the link to the ticket,where I found the patch(999-ath9k-wndr3700-antennas.patch):
https://dev.openwrt.org/ticket/6533
The other patch seems to be applied already.
Ticket is already open,so there's hope the someone who is smarter than me - It's not hard - will solve this.
With echoing different values to antctl2g,signal strength is changing,I'm trying to find the best config.Until now that's all I've found out,no better solution.
I'm sure the best solution would be one or more external antenna,we should find out where to connect these.
