well, you could always go X-10 and add a serial port to the WRT for the x10 serial interface: x-10 firecracker home control kit

x-10 is definitely a bit advertising happy, but the kit I bought years ago (interestingly enough a firecracker starter in the late 90's and it looks like the kit hasn't changed much) worked fine.  and it is only $39.99 before tax/shipping.

the high-end stuff we use at work (from APC and cyclades) is in the expensive camp.  it works well, but it is pricey.

18 Dec 2006, 23:05

They do exist but they are not cheap (20+ euros).
It's cheaper if you buy a simple male to female IEC cable (PC to screen power cable),
chop the female connector off and attach that cable to a power connector (easily and cheap to obtain in a local DIY electro shop).

Or you could buy a cheap power extension cable, chop the female part off and attach the cable to a IEC male (~2euros) connector.
