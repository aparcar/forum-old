Hi Tushar,

if you really need to manually set and control ingress/egress bandwidth of all connected devices in your network, I agree there are no real ready-made out-of-the-box solutions for that (that is gargoyle might come close). (But man tc can be your friend). If you actually have slightly less stringent requirements and are more interested in a reasonably fair shared network, both qos-scripts and sqm-scripts get pretty close to "good enough" for a lot of users.
For example "hard limiting the speed of every device connected to a router" both qos and sqm scripts perform flawlessly (AFAICT) as long as you are happy with limiting the aggregate bandwidth of all devices; both do NOT allow to limit each deice individually. It turns out that for many users flow separation and aggregate bandwidth control work well enough, but unfortunately it just does not work well enough for everybody.

Best Regards
