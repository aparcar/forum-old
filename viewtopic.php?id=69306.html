I installed the sane packages to use the scanner HP3400c. After installing I was able to scan from the router and from my PC. But since the morning after, I only get a device busy error.

On the Arch Wiki:

Device busy

If your USB device is listed with scanimage -L but launching the test scanimage pixma:04A9173E_11DAD1 --format=tiff > test.tiff always return the 'Device busy' error, you might try to add your username to the scanner group usermod -a -G scanner yourusername then blacklist the usblp kernel module by writing blacklist usblp in /etc/modprobe.d/no-usblp.conf (it prevents usblp from loading to support scanning, not needed by either CUPS or xsane and related tools). Reboot to finish.

Since I am scanning as root the only thing to do is remove the usblp. I used 

rmmod usblp

to remove the module, but I still get 'device busy'.

Anyone knows what else I could try?
