I like NuFone (http://www.nufone.net): Pre-paid (via PayPal, no minimum), 2 cents/minute, supports IAX and SIP, and a toll free number to boot (same 2 cents/minute rate applies).  I have also used Broadvoice (http://www.broadvoice.com) who offer an unlimited in-state plan at $10/month and unlimited World plan at $20/month.  I use the NuFone service with my WRT54G running White Russian.  I don't see any reason why Broadvoice wouldn't work with it as well.
