Hi all,

Foneram for la fonera+, aka FON2201 is available here : http://www.moliets-bastide.info/post/FONERAM

It's just an ELF file format you can load from RedBoot, and then run Kamikaze 7.06 (kernel 2.6.19.2) from RAM.
This is NOT a firmware to flash ! Just a way to boot OpenWRT on your device without reflashing. After a reboot your foneraplus is still intact.
Entering RedBoot console is the only way to run foneram.


Features in few lines:
- boot is very fast: 1 to 2 minutes, and safe because no reflashing needed.
- your fonera+ will boot as a classic router wifi-AP with routed internet access if connected to your main router on WAN ethernet port.
  Connect via WEP encrypted WiFi or LAN port, SSH or telnet. Root password and WEP key --> serial number of the foneraplus.
- you can mount squashfs and JFFS FON partitions
- CIFS is supported to mount remote ressources and perform full backups of flash contents.
- test the last release of X-WRT WebifÂ² (script "instwebif")
- customize your version of foneram by script at boot: add your files, modify some paramaters...



The post is very long, sorry !  Lot of warnings !
Perhaps printing the doc may help.
Don't plan to perform all described operations in 10 minutes.
Just take the necesary time you need to understand what you can do with foneram.


Foneram was compiled from OpenWRT SVN with some mods.
Thanks to davidel, OpenWRT, FON for the hardware, and enjoy Foneram.
Just hope this dev will help some guys to unbrick their boxes or have a better look on the system.
