The simplest way to expand storage capacity by using extroot. You need a flash drive (1 GB is more than enough) or other storage media (SD Card, MMC, external HDD, etc.). Con Pivot overlay is recommended.

You can add an ethernet-to-usb converter to use USB on your device.
Check this openwrt wiki article about extroot

Another method is upgrading your NAND hardware. Upgrading from 4 MB flash to 8 MB or 16 MB. A complex workaround can be done manually.

That's it.
