First, you should have a look at the wiki page: http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/hardware/po â€¦ rt.pinouts
Then, you should write in detail what you already did, and the results you encountered.
Something you could do for a start is an echo test. Therefor you make sure that local echo is disabled in your terminal program (don't connect any line of your serial cable and type something in the terminal program - what you typed should not appear on the screen). Then, you connect your serial cables Tx with your serial cables Rx and type something - you should get what you typed printed on the screen.
