It appears to be an AR7 based board. I can read most of the chips, except the flash chip. I see two chips that look like likely candidates. One is the long skinny chip underneath the mini-pci card. There is another similar chip between the two big capacitors towards the back of the board. What numbers are on those chips?

It kinda looks like this is a rebranded Ozenda AR4505GW (http://www.dettingen.co.uk/docs/OzendaSpec_001.pdf)

Finding a serial port would be the next step. Ideally, you want to be able to get access to the boot loader. Then, you compile an image of openwrt with the ramdisk option, and copy that to the ram. You can then boot from that image without writing it to the rom. That allows you to see what works without risking bricking your router.

Let me know what you find,
Jonathan Bennett (oneru)
