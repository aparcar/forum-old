I'd like to get to LuCI remotely on port 8080, without needing to actually have httpd listen on 8080.



Here's what I have so far

iptables -t nat -A prerouting_rule -p tcp --dport 8080 -j DNAT --to 192.168.1.1:80
iptables -A input_rule -p tcp --dport 80 -j ACCEPT

Of course the second rule allows external access to port 80. I'd like to avoid this.



I tried a redirect in /etc/config/firewall but it did not work... connections were still refused.

You are so close.  Change the second line of the iptables rule to this:

iptables -A input_wan -p tcp -d 192.168.1.1 --dport 80 -j ACCEPT

I think that will have the desired effect of keeping port 80 blocked on the wan side.  I do something similar with ssh and it works just fine for me.
