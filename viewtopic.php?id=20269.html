Ok, very simple solution.

Press the Reset Button, Power on the Box. Give your client the ip 192.168.1.100/24 and upload the http://downloads.openwrt.org/kamikaze/8 … uashfs.trx file with the Firmware Update Tool which comes with the CD. It will reboot automatically and you can log in at 192.168.1.1 within minutes. No 30/30/30 Tricks or tftp is needed.

have Fun!
