Oh, hey!  i just noticed that  those "code" boxes scroll.    I thought you had just posted the mtd information.

Aha!   You're supposed to be putting this to "mtd4"  not mtd1, say the instructions.     The values of the two should be the same after you change mtd1 but i'm not clear on whether adam2 just treats these as a variable or whether it actually does something with them when it (Adam2) starts up the way a linux system does.   In the second case the change you're making may not take effect "fully" until the next reboot.  (<-- this is just speculation)

Also after you set the mtd1, do a GETENV to see if it has really changed.   As i understand it that should be persistent across boots so there's something  resetting it.   (e.g. if you flashed the original firmware maybe).
