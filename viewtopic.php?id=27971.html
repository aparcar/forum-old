Post #2
arokh
4 Jan 2011, 11:18

You are trying to compile a package that does not exist, kmod-video-gspca-zc3xx is a kernel module and that means it is provided by the kernel package.

You can do:

$ TOPDIR=$PWD make -C package/kernel clean
$ TOPDIR=$PWD make -C package/kernel

Or:

$ make package/kernel-clean
$ make package/kernel-compile
$ make package/kernel-install
