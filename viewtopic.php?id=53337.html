stangri
1 Dec 2016, 21:15

You can do it with dnsmasq:

uci add_list dhcp.@dnsmasq[0].address='/amzdigitaldownloads.edgesuite.net/127.0.0.1'
uci add_list dhcp.@dnsmasq[0].address='/softwareupdates.amazon.com/127.0.0.1'
uci commit dhcp
/etc/init.d/dnsmasq restart
