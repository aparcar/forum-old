Post #6
chrysx
12 Mar 2009, 12:50

The Ubiquity router-station really rocks,
it has 3 mini-pci, 3 lan, usb, poe, 16MB flash, 64MB ram and a MIPS 24K 680MHz processor. All the additional connectors like GPIO, serial and JTAG have labels on the board to be easily identified :-) . It is the one their web GUI contest is based on (http://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=19118). However the 'political' surroundings might develop it's a nice piece of hardware.
Mine came preloaded with OpenWRT r13658, without web interface; everything works out of the box except the second lan port, I just began to search for a suitable switch-configuration.
