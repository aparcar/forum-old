The Subject line says it all!  I upgraded my Netgear WGT634U to Backfire 10.03.1 and reinstalled mpd, but now...

# mpd
mpd: can't resolve symbol 'g_malloc_n' in lib 'mpd'.

Sadly, I think this may relate to my having many packages installed on external storage - it looks as if "opkg upgrade" doesn't always know that a package needs upgrading! So, I used "--force-reinstall" on most of the libraries listed as dependencies for mpd - I did *not* reinstall glib2 or libpthread, as those are marked essential to OpenWRT. Same results as above.

Where is g_malloc_n? Google searches haven't helped identify which library might be the cause - the most useful result was relating to Ubuntu and Titanium, which doesn't quite apply here. Now what?
