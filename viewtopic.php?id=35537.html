Have you checked your WR1043ND revision?

My WR1043ND is v1.8 which I believe requires backfire 10.3.1-RC6 or newer.

You have installed backfire 10.3 which is relatively old and supports up to v1.6.

Perhaps you should use Backfire 10.03.1 final?

You can download it here:

http://downloads.openwrt.org/backfire/1 … actory.bin
