you have 2 options, i have set up my router to not include the wifi in the bridge (my wlan is eth1)  so my lan_ifname=br0 lan_ifnames=vlan0 (i have openvpn running on tap0 in bridged to br0).  once you have pulled the wifi interface out of the bridge you can do an ifconfig eth1 down and bring the interface down.

the other option is to break the bridge and require an openvpn connection over the wifi to access the wired side of the network.
