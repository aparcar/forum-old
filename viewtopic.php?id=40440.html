DSL-2540U does not have wireless..

It comes in several different versions and simply wrong firmware can very well brick it..

When you choose the official firmware, first you should check the sticker underneath the device, "H/W Ver."  It may be C1, C2, T1 etc..
Choose the firmware that fits accordingly..
