Cracking WPA2-PSK is generally not based on repeatedly attempting to authenticate with the AP.  After capturing the on-air "handshake" of an authorized user, an offline brute-force dictionary attack can be used to test possible passphrases.

There is a ubus call to hostapd which will ban a MAC address from authenticating.  Of course any serious attacker will randomize their MAC address.
