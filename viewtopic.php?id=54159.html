Thread about the V4 here.  https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=50478 There is the v4 "sysupgrade" binary in the download directories.  It appears that you need to serial console and muck around in the u-boot to get it loaded.   In general the fact that it only has a 4 MB flash chip is going to limit you.
