What type of internet service do you have?  The default is to have the WAN port pick up a DHCP address from the modem, which works for most cable systems and many DSL where the modem also acts as a router.

CHAOS CALMER (Bleeding Edge, r45907)

You are actually running the trunk Chaos Calmer, which you really shouldn't.  Suggest installing Barrier Breaker instead.  Since you are off of stock firmware, it should work to just scp an unmodified E4 sysupgrade image to /tmp then flash it with sysupgrade.
