I'm not sure I agree with the decision somebody made to eliminate all traces of the breed firmware from the wt3020 wiki page. It is still the most robust solution for installing openwrt onto the wt3020. I do understand the GPL implications (and I would certainly not support the breed project monetarily or otherwise), but since the hackpascal guy is not making a profit on the uboot image, and nobody else came forward with a similar, but GPL-clean uboot image (actually, no one even bothered to integrate the openwrt failsafe patch in almost a year), the wiki should present all options to the users and let them decide which avenue to pursue.
