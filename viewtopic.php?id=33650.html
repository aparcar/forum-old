transmission shows only the netto-bandwith and doesn't include the protocol-overhead. Downloading with 500kByte/sec utilize at least 30kByte/sec of your upstream bandwith. Maybe this is an explanation for the "missing" uploadbandwith.
   
and probably there are just 2 peer per torrent downloading from you because
"upload-slots-per-torrent" is set to 2?
