Did you try tftp? I pseudo-bricked my WL-500g by setuiding/chowning busybox to 'nobody' (tried to chown/setuid busybox httpd but that chowned/setuided the busybox bin of course). It didn't respond to ping at all. Tried starting it in diag mode (unplug cord, hold reset-button, plug in cord, wait a couple of seconds) and it responded to ping and tftp.

Correct me if I'm wrong, but I believe diagmode/tftp should work in any case of fubared firmware. Fubared nvram not included...
