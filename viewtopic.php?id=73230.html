I use a GL-AR150 with a USB stick modem from the country where I am located using a build of OpenWRT called Rooter from OfModemsAndMen.com .  Haven't tried VPN on this but it seems like it would work.   I have from time to time used a battery pack and I think from all the routers they support, including the A5-V11 (about half the size or the GL-AR150 but half the memory and twice the current drain of the GL-AR150).  From time to time I use a cheap ($8 four cell power bank from eBay which holds four 18650 cells) and it can last a day normally.   I even salvaged the cells from a couple of to-be-discarded laptop battery banks.   I believe the GL-AR150 and the TP703N may be your lowest current draws but the TP link only has 4M of memory where the GL has 8, enough for OpenVPN.

Is that something like you are looking for?
