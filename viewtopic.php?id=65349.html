I have two identical Western Digital MyNet 750 routers.

[edit] It has a 2.4 GHz radio that I use as a master, and a 5 GHz radio I use as a client.  I use the routers as wireless repeaters and the WAN comes from the 5 GHz radio.  [end edit]

I programmed one, BBold, a while ago with Barrier Breaker 14.07
I programmed another one, CCnew, today with Chaos Calmer 15.05



I have both configured as WiFi clients to the same source.  In fact, I copied /etc/config/wireless from BBold to CCnew today.  I used luci to adjust CCnew mode from legacy to N/40 MHz, because that seems to be a new feature for Chaos Calmer 15.05.  But BBold connects at 40 MHz and CCnew connects at 20 MHz.

BBold has WiFi signal of -67 dBm   noise of =-95 dBm
CCnew has WiFi signal of -43 dBm.  noise of -95 dBm

BBold connects at 40 MHz bandwidth, in the 270-300 Mbit/s rate.
CCnew connects at 20 MHz bandwidth, in the 6 - 195 Mbit/s rate.

I thought that -43 dBm was a STRONGER signal than -67 dBm.  The units are about the same distance from the source, but BBold is through flooring and CCnew is through air.

I've got the same wireless config file, but the software version is different.  Did 40MHz get locked out for chaos calmer?

I do not know the details of analysing or debugging wifi.  Tell me what to google and I will.
