But it could also take 3 seconds to sign a host key or something (the router has a 400 MHz Atheros processor), and it would be no good idea to log in every 10 seconds maybe. I just don't want to make my router get slower than my internet connection.

If anyone knows what dropbear does there, that would be good to know.
