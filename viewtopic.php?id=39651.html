Post #2
nott
1 Oct 2012, 17:59

Please check http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/linksys/wrt54g

I suggest you can use Backfire (current stable version is 10.03.1) brcm-2.4
Personally I advice against brcm47xx as 16MB RAM is not enough for the newer kernel.

The upcoming Attitude Adjustment (12.09) will not support WRT54GL, so Backfire is here to stay for ages.
