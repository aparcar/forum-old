Hi,
I have an "Alice Gate 2 PLUS Wi-Fi" (not the business, not voip). It is an "Us Robotics Wireless MAXg ADSL Gateway", re-branded by Pirelli Broadband solutions, re-branded by Alice.

I can install successfully the US Robotics  firmware for  Wireless MAXg ADSL Gateway, and I'm almost sure it is the 9108. The firmware I installed is "USRAGW2-PLUS_fs_kernel_3.04L.01.-100727_2130"
I installed this image using the procedure described here: http://www.ilpuntotecnicoeadsl.com/foru … l#msg40859

You have to short-circuit the two pins marked red, THEN power on the router; if it doesn't work, try fast intermitting the short circuit between the two pins while power on and booting.

If success, the power led becomes red and no other led is active; you can remove the short circuit.
At this point, you connect via ethernet on ETH1, the ip address of the router will be 192.168.1.1, you can connect with your browser at http://192.168.1.1/ and upload a firmware image.
You have to adjust your computer lan settings before doing this, in example a static 192.168.1.2 mask 255.255.255.0 , because when the router is in this "upload firmware" modality, it doesn't serve any ip address to your PC.

In some circustamces, before uploading a new firmware it can be useful to erase the flash, upolading this blank fake firmware: AGW2-PLUS_psi_clear

So flash two times, the first with AGW2-PLUS_psi_clear and the second with the firmware you want to use; repeat the procedure short-circuiting the 2 pins marked in red.

This procedure works for uploading the US robotics firmware, and it unlocks my router for different uses, purposes and providers other than ALICE..
BUT.. I would like to upload openwrt because I need to use this machine as a WI-FI CLIENT and then as a  router between the WI-FI and the Ethernet, simoultaneusly; and maybe to run software on it.
I want to connect to the NETGEAR wi-fi router of my neighbour and then have internet for my PCs on the 4 lan ports of the 9108. How to do this ? Please help. (And I'm ready to contribute immediately as I can to port openwrt on this machine, just tell me how and what to do)
