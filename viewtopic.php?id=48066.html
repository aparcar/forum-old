If you want help you must provide info that will encourage folk to consider your case. At the very least, identify your router and version of OpenWrt.

In particular, you need to clarify, at the very least, of you have (or have not) upgraded to a trunk version or upgraded from one trunk version to a later. Wifi and other systems are definitely affected from time to time within the development branch. In such a case, the answer is to try trunk latest.
