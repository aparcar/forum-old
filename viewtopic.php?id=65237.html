It's just a workaround, I didn't suggest to add this to openwrt or anything, or that this is a fix.  But luup can try if it helps in his case, until the implementation of DFS/radardetection for/in the ath(9k) driver gets better, especially in dealing with false-positives.

My device detects every 2-5 days a radarsignal (?) and switches to a non-DFS channel.
Sadly the tx-power is limited to 15dBm on this channels.
So after 30min I try to switch back to the orginal DFS channel, which should comply with regulatory restrictions (Non-Occupancy Time) in Europe.
