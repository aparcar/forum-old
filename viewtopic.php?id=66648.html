There have been problems with the switch setup, resulting in reboot loops, in the past, I can't quite point to the exact revision numbers anymore, respectively if CC was affected or not, but current HEAD should work.

In general, if you took a snapshot build, luci won't be preinstalled, so try telnet or ssh (recent snapshots no longer ship telnet and only provide ssh) instead.
