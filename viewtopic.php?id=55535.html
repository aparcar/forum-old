The serial cable came! When the OpenWrt wiki says pin 1, does that refer to the pin on the left side, when looking at the router facing the front? Also is it okay not to plug in VCC?

I'm connecting with Putty and mashing Ctrl-C while the router turns on and nothing is showing...

Edit: got tx/Rx mixed up, whoops. It'd be great if someone can give me assistance flashing.

Edit: I got this working...I took the linux image and rootfs from link. I used ymodem, from Extra Putty, to transfer the linux image from my computer to the router (since ethernet seemed to be down) and then did the same for the rootfs. Instructions 9-13 from here helped me a lot--basically, just replace tftpboot with loady and use the proper addresses/file sizes. I put the linux image at 0xbf040000 and the rootfs at 0xbf1b0000. I sort of guessed on the latter address and I wasn't sure if that was correct, so I did a sysupgrade to a clean OpenWrt after rebooting the router.
