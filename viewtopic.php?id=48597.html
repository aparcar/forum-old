With limited hardware, the most tiny solution is allowing only your friends ip addresses (instead of user/pass login) to a 80 port that redirects every connection to the destination site ip address.
And in your friends devices edit the /etc/hosts with your ip adress and the host of the destination site. See the "rinetd" package.
