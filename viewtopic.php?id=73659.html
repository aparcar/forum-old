Routing at full 1 GBit/s line speed needs a correspondingly powerful router, ar71xx (like your Archer C7v2) can not do that. For a 1 GBit/s uplink I'd seriously suggest x86, but mvebu or mt7621/ mt7622/ mt7623 might be able to cope as well (depending on your use case and the additional features you require from your router).
