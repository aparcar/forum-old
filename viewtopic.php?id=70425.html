I recently did a reflash on my router to fix a VPN issue. However, I now have an IPv6 WAN IP everywhere I go. When ever I go to disable my wan adapter, I (obviously) lose access to many websites.

For security purposes, I want my network to rely on IPv4 (my VPN doesn't support secure IPv6 yet).

I don't know what changed, but previously, when ever I would go to whatismyip or I googled "what is my IP" it was always my ipv4 ip. I compared my two configs, old and new, and the only difference was my ipv4 config ran as 'bridge' with my ipv6 interface. I tried to do that again to see if that fixed it, but I lose all connectivity when I do that.
