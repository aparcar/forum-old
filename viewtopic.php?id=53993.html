You did the right thing, supported hardware is most often listed on the TOH.

Yours is not listed, but search reveals that the WNR612v3 has a RealTek RTL8196C SoC with specs similar to the v2 (4MB Flash/ 32 MB RAM) so the v3 is definitely capable of running OpenWRT. That said, it is not (yet?) officially supported.

Found a thread that may be of interest:
https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=46606 (Realtek SoC support in OpenWrt)
