This website was set up by a Chinese guy whose wife is a merchant on Aliexpress. (I'd been complaining to her about how much trouble I was having finding an OpenWrt compatible router there.)

Apparently he had some initial interest, but then no sales & he's more or less given up on it. So I said I'd ask here what people interested in OpenWrt might want from a Chinese webstore specialising in compatible equipment. Done right I think such a website could offer a service to this community - as well as helping the website proprietor earn a living.

So if you have any suggestions*, please fire away. I'll point them at this thread (and help translate a bit if necessary).

(*Mine included:
- less heavy-handed SEO
- more technical info on the devices
- lower prices )

robthebrew wrote:

The tiny amount of china versus openWRT that I experienced is that they find a firmware and the install it even after HW updates that break it.

Yeah - my 'vision' was just a vendor who'd
a) specialise in OpenWrt compatible equipment
b) check the  electronics inside the box was compatible before selling
c) had enough technical knowledge to flash the non-trivial cases &
d) was prepared to cooperate with the community here on gathering info on &/ samples of new devices.

As you say, *most* of the Chinese vendors aim for mass sales. OpenWrt tweaking is certainly a niche, but there are some vendors inside China (on taobao, the ebay-equivalent) who service just such a niche. So maybe it's a big enough niche for an export-oriented vendor too?
