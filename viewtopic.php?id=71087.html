mk24
31 May 2017, 14:40

This is an incomplete install.  /tmp is in RAM, you don't have a permanent overlay.

mount should show a MTD device mounted on /overlay with a jffs2 filesystem.

/dev/mtdblock3 on /overlay type jffs2 (rw,noatime)
