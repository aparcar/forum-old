What are you talking about ? TLS in luci web UI or support of TLS in wget, curl ?
TLS is enabled by default and forcibly redirected from http in dedicated driver snapshot.
Reason why its bad - self signed certificate. Most browsers will refuse go there or at least bark.
TLS in wget/curl - takes significant additional space especially when ca-certificates installed.
