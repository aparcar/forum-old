Possible causes:

a) DHCP server disabled on the LAN-side (or are you using DHCP-relay from the main router?)
b) Missing switch-device configuration in /etc/config/network (if your router model has a built-in switch device)
c) Something else wrong in /etc/config/network, such as extraneous VLAN-configs or something?

Other than that, I think posting the remaining configs here as well may help?
