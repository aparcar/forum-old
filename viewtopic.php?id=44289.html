This is answer to this threat..

1.You probably have MR-3020 FW on your 703N, just like me. Download the firmware from this link:

http://www64.zippyshare.com/v/57841009/file.html

and flash via WEB UI on TP-Link.

2.Download OpenWRT from this link:

http://www16.zippyshare.com/v/25323979/file.html

And flash via TP-Link WEB ui. Dont interuprt the flasing process. That may brick your 703N. !

3. Now probably you have OpenWRT for MR-3020
Now download Putty, and connect via Telnet.

after succesfull login, write:

1.cd /tmp

2.wget http://downloads.openwrt.org/attitude_a … pgrade.bin

3. sysupgrade --force openwrt-ar71xx-generic-tl-wr703n-v1-squashfs-sysupgrade.bin

Router will restart, and you will have OpenWRT for 703N.
