In the kernel are none , but there is one kernel module, the broadcom wireless driver (broadcom-wl) and its authenticator, "nas".

Apart from these two there's also a few firmwares for devices which aren't run on the system itself; these are often accepted as a necessary evil. Many wireless drivers include them, and for the dsl/atm drivers in ifxmips and ar7 its the same.

Everything else is afaik available in source form.
