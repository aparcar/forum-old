Check your Luci packages in telnet via opkg list-installed | grep luci   , or try opkg list_installed | grep luci

You may have to opkg update and opkg install missing luci packages; here's a list of packages required on my router:

luci-admin-core
luci-admin-full
Luci-fastindex
luci-app-firewall (optional but needed for configuration)
luci-app-qos (optional but may need for prioritising WAN traffic)
luci-cbi
luci-core
luci-http
luci-i18n-english
luci-ipkg
luci-lmo
luci-lucid
luci-nixio
luci-px5g
luci-sgi-cgi
luci-sys
luci-theme-base
luci-theme-openwrt
luci-uci
luci-uvl
luci-web
