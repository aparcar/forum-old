Moved from other thread to avoid mess:

I've found all three boot_sel pins, @oxo send me photos of NAND (surroundings) and unpopulated NOR memory.

In F3 to Enable UART CFG 02 mode R20 and R24 needs to be shorted (default is CFG 01)

Default on F3 is only CFG 01 R24 HIGH Vcc 3,3v

R24 shorted (GND) is CFG 00
R20 shorted (VCC) is CFG 03
R15 shorted (VCC)is CFG 05
R20(high)+R24(low) = CFG 02 UART MODE WITH LACK OF EEPROM

UPDATE:
Right config for CFG 04 UART is R15 and R24 shorted.


R149 does something too but for me it is unknown.
