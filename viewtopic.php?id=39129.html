It's not clear whether your question was answered or not. The board does use switching regulators. So it should tolerate a fairly wide range of input voltage without issue. The regulators are spec'd up to 18 V input, but also check the voltage rating of the large-ish capacitor nearest the power input jack. I'd be surprised if it is less than 25 V but it may be 16 V. So don't exceed the lower of 18 V or the rating of that capacitor, and you should be fine. Car battery will be about 13.8 V fully charged, but as high as 14.4 V or so when the engine is running. That should be fine.
