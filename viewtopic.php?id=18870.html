I wanted to do the same, but alas, it's not that simple. You have to reconfigure the built-in switch too, in your configuration it will still send packets tagged for vlan1 or vlan2 to the cpu port, which you only chose to ignore.
And I still don't know how to do that...
My current solution is to include eth0.1 and eth0.2 in the lan-bridge, and let the cpu do the bridging.
