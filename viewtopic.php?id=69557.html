In this context, "filtered" means "closed and safe"; the third column is just the name of the service (usually) associated with each port number, and is a standard.

The default configuration of OpenWrt is to reject everything, no ports open on the internet.  Unless you need to accept incoming connections for some reason, it can be left that way.
