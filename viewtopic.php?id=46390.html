plexie wrote:

Is it dead?

Not necessarily.
Does your router have an emergency web interface at 192.168.0.2?

Another way to resurrect it would be if you can get serial access to the (working) bootloader.
But you would have to solder a pin header (or three wires directly) to the board, and you would need a serial-to-TTL or USB-to-TTL cable.
