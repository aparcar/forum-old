How do I backup the cal_data?

Post #2
alphasparc
29 Jun 2014, 17:21

cat /proc/mtd to show all the available partition.
dd if=/dev/<your calibration data partition> of=/tmp/<name of backup file>
Then use scp to copy it to your computer for safe keeping.
