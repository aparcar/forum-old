I'd like to setup tcpdump to show only the packets that are originating on my router, or are destined for my router.  Barring that, I'd like to get a list of the ports that are being used for NAT clients.

Is there anything like that available?  I tried tcpdump -i vlan1 host <public ip address> but I get all the traffic from clients behind the firewall as well.  I just want to see if anything is targeting my router.

Is there something that will dump a list of the NAT port-forwarding that's going on?  I don't mean the stuff in iptables, I mean the ad-hoc connections that are created when a client on br0 connects to something on vlan1.
