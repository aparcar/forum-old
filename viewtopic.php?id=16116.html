Hello,

Its more an issue with OpenVPN than with OpenWRT per say, but since it involves OpenWRT devices, maybe somebody bumped into this issue:

Here is my setup:

OpenVPN server (Debian) <-- Internet--> ADSL router <------------> OpenVPN client (OpenWRT)
IPs:                  x.x.x.x        y.y.y.y    192.168.1.1   192.168.1.2

To summarize the setup:
- since y.y.y.y is dynamically assigned and x.x.x.x is static, the OpenVPN client is configured to automatically connect to x.x.x.x (port 40000).
- the ADSL router does NAT, so when the client connects to the server, it appears at y.y.y.y .
- OpenVPN is configured to use udp both on the server and client: proto udp

Result:
- all works well, the VPN gets established
