Since the Itus box is no longer available, about two months ago I posted about how we were making one of our appliance available to the developer community. We have just release a second one in a smaller form factor that may be a little more affordable. In case you havent heard of Rhino Labs, here is a little about us:


Rhino Labs(www.rhinolabsinc.com) is a OEM manufacturer of Cavium Octeon III based appliances that run on Linux and OpenWRT. We provide them to a number of the top ten networking and security companies. When one of our customers or a prospective new customer is working with us to develop an application or solution we provide them with a box that is configured for evaluation/development purposes. During this time they will decide on the final configuration (memory, storage, wireless, ports etc.) and the physical form factor of the enclosure of the appliance they want us to provide them.

We want to make these evaluation configured units available to individual developers .Our engineers say it offers six times (6x) the performance of the Itus box.  The box new box I am referring to is our SDNA Shasta: http://www.rhinolabsinc.com/rhino-shast … appliance/

Here are the specs;

Processor- Cavium OCTEON III 7130 4 Cores @1.GHz
Memory- 4GB DDR3
Storage- Onboard 4GB eMMC also includes an option for M.2 Storage module (128-1TB) or 2.5” SATA SSD HD and a uSD Slot
External interfaces- 1GE L2/3 switch with 4x1GE ports, 1GE Management Port, USB Host, Management RS232 via RJ-45 Port
Expansion- Dual mPCIe supporting 801.11ac/n and LTE
SOFTWARE- U-Boot from Rhino Labs, Comes pre-loaded with Linux SDK or OpenWRT
FORM FACTOR- 3.8W x 5.5D x 1.75H (inches)
No enclosure, board with stanbd offs.

We are also offering our SDNA 7130. (http://www.rhinolabsinc.com/rhino-sdna7 … appliance/)
Here are the specs:
Processor- Cavium OCTEON III 7130, quad cores @1.5GHz
Memory- 4GB DDR3
Storage- On board 4GB eMMC/SATA via M.2/uSD slot
External interfaces- 8x1GE + 1GE, one RS232 via RJ45, UDB 3.0
Expansion- Dual mPCIe supporting 801.11ac/n and LTE
Software- U-Boot from Rhino Labs
Comes pre-loaded with Linux or Open WRT
Complete appliance with enclosure pictured on our site.


If you have any technical questions, please post them here so everyone can see them and I will have one of our engineers post answers.

We will offering it at a discount and can discuss pricing offline. If you have an immediate requirement contact me via email terry.hamm@rhinolabsinc.com.

On our side we are interested in what kind of projects you are working on. In order for me to provide a price we are asking for the following information in return:
Will this be for personal use or are you working on a a project for a company/organization?
What type of application or function do you intend to use it for?

Please keep in mind that we manufacturer this configuration in small batches in between our customer's production runs, so when you contact us they may be immediately available or there may be a short wait until are next run.
