or if like me you're using wireless, WWAN. I'm guessing you'll want to select PPPoE, but there is a drop down list with all the options. Take your pick to match your settings.

I believe it'd be under Network->Firewall, then you'll see "Redirections" in the middle of that screen. Add as many as you like. You'll need to select TCP or UDP or both and which port it uses and what internal IP you want it forwarded to. I haven't actually set it up, but from looking at the interface, that is what you're looking for.
