[ar71xx]: add support for the archer c5

Have a look at the Makefile diff, you could probably change the line:

$(eval $(call SingleProfile,TPLINK-LZMA,64kraw,ARCHERC5,archer-c5,ARCHER-C5,ttyS0,115200,0xc5000001,1,16Mlzma))

To 8Mlzma at the end to get a 8 MB image, assuming the wiki is correct that it has 8 MB flash.

The TP-Link TFTP recovery connects to a TFTP server on 192.168.0.66, not 1.66. I'm guessing just a typo though, Wireshark should have shown you that anyway. Don't have any suggestions beyond that aside from serial connection.
