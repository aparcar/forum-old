Did you searched anything?
There are a lot of documentation explaining how to recover and with what files!
Take a look:
http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/tp-link/tl-wr703n
https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=31729

And about loading the mr-3020 firmware onto it, it seems posible, as it's the same hardware (same cpu, same ram, and same flash).
Flashing a different firmware can brick the device, but it will be still recoverable. As long you don't damage the bootloader, you can always flash any firmware over it and repair it.
Learn to recovery in the u-boot bootloader.
