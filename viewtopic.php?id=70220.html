There are numerous solutions to the generic issue of unable to access the AP when the STAtion goes missing.

It's probably worth understanding exactly how you use the ZSun.  Specifically are you connecting to a specific set of STAtions or are you a road warrior and rarely use the same STAtion twice. 

If you are always connecting to different stations, then it may pay to run a job at startup that just removes what ever station is there.

If you are connecting to a specific set of stations then you may want to look at dirks travelmate.
https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=67697
