Hi All,

I have a WNDR3700 and experiencing slow wireless performance on both 2.4ghz and 5ghz radios. I tried every setting/combination for both radios and can't seem to break 130Mbps on my clients (averaging 52-78Mbps). Coming from the stock firmware I was hitting 270-300Mbps constantly.

The signal quality and range are great but poor speed. I went as far as removing/installing latest Intel 6230 drives on my notebook.

Is there something with the particular firmware or router I am using? Can anyone with a WNDR3700 chime in and shed some light with settings that work for them? How does the WNDR3800 stack up?

Current firmware: OpenWrt Firmware Attitude Adjustment (r30486) / LuCI Trunk (trunk+svn8223)

Any direction or advice is much appreciated. Thanks!

Post #2

You have to enable HT40 to go over 130Mbps. Note that it is not allowed if the AP detects other AP's in the area with overlapping channels.
