Just to make sure I understand the process you used:

1) Download a trunk snapshot containing "squashfs-factory" in the name.
-----  ie: openwrt-ar71xx-generic-dir-825-b1-squashfs-factory.bin

2) Follow the loading steps (I used a Win7 machine and the firmware recovery mode) from the wiki.

3) Telnet-passwd-reboot

4) Use the sysupgrade procedure from the wiki.

5) opkg update / opkg install and required for the features/functions you want.

That is the procedure I followed and it worked like champ.  I use a static IP on my devbox to connect rather than DHCP.  Once I get my standard set of packages loaded, I can hop into LUCI and go to town. 

What exact process are you using?
