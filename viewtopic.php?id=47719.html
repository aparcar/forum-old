This has nothing to do with simultaneous dualband. (The AP has 2 radios, but your client don't. So the client can only use 2,4GHz OR 5GHz). So if your client doesn't support 40MHz you are stuck with 216Mbit/s
check http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IEEE_802.11n-2009
the TL-WDR4300 can support 3 spartial streams so your maximum will be MCS 23 = 216Mbit/s@20MHz and 450Mbit/s@40MHz
