I've been rattling my brain for weeks trying to get to the bottom of this problem...

I have 3 TL-WR1043ND routers (1 AP, 2 client) connected to directional antennas to create a ~1200 and ~400 foot link between 3 buildings and most of the time it works perfectly, but some days the units wifi performance gets slower and slower until eventually it's unusable.  Logging into the offending router (usually one of the clients, rarely the AP) and cycling the radio with the 'wifi' command immediately fixes it for an indeterminate amount of time.  The antenna connections are known to work with other units (WRT54GL running in G mode and backfire 10.03.1/kern 2.6), and the issue continues in HT40(+/-), HT20 and G mode.

Since restarting the radio immediately fixes it, I suspect theres an issue with the ath9k driver.  Are there any diagnotics/logs that can help me pinpoint why the radios keep dying?
