I made a mistake changing the LAN protocol to PPP and now I cannot access the router. Wifi was disabled and through the LAN ports I cannot communicate with router.
I cannot get to restore the router by pressing the RESET button. Is this normal with OpenWRT firmware?
Router was flashed with 15.05.1 with default config.

mk24

Try recovery mode.  Boot up, during the 2 seconds or so that the "gear" LED is flashing fast press the reset button-- then it should start flashing really fast and continue to do so.  Now you can telnet to 192.168.1.1 and either run a firstboot to reset to defaults, or run mount_root then fix your configuration.
