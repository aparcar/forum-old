The wiki about flashing/recovering this router is quite clear and and almost fully documented (except JTAG usage).

- The reset button should work.

Openwrt failsafe: Turn on the router, press/release the button Restore Factory Settings, at the bottom of the router, several times until the green power led starts blinking very fast, then you can access via telnet to OpenWrt.

This should be enough to enter failsafe mode. then just erase the configuration partiton

mtd erase -r rootfs_data

If the button doesnt work you can use the serial console to stop OpenWrt before loading the entire system with faulty settings.

- Flashing a new firmware should't be problem. Maybe easier to flash a .chk firmware since you won't need a TFTP server.

- Never erase the flash using CFE, it can destroy data specific for your board, even the CFE bootloader and end with a total brick.

Regards.
