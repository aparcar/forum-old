Revision numbers in wiki articles should usually be read as supported since $revision. It is presumed that device support isn't removed, although this may happen with poorly supported or unmaintained targets. WDR3600/4300 images can be found at http://downloads.openwrt.org/snapshots/ … x/generic/ (latest trunk builds) or http://downloads.openwrt.org/barrier_br … x/generic/ (Barrier Breaker, stable release).

Trunk snapshots don't come with LuCI web interface, but it can be installed as a package, see doc/howto/luci.essentials.
