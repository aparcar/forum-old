What does the firewall do in IPv6 with Masquerading enabled firewall zone?

The firewall only allows forward from LAN to WAN but not WAN to LAN. When Masquerading is enabled in the firewall zone, the outbound connection works. When Masquerading is disabled, forwarding of both directions must be allowed or outbound connection also won't work.

When I enable Masquerading and check IP on webpages, the IP displayed is the computer's IP but not the router's IP. What does the firewall do in IPv6 with Masquerading enabled firewall zone?
