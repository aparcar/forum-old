20 megabit or so is typically good performance for a wireless G lan. Remember wireless is a half duplex medium, so you cut your bandwidth in half transmitting station to station. Ad-hoc could potentially be faster, but has serious disadvantages. If you really want faster wireless to wireless transfers I suggest adding a second access point on another channel.
