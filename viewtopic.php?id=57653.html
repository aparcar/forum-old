Most DDOS can not be stopped. It doesn't matter what you have on your router, because most DDOS is about stuffing your pipe until nothing can get through.

The only answer is to have a enterprise level DDOS protection, where your ISP (or other service provider) dumps the packets before it ever gets to you.

Oh yeah, by the way, most Enterprise level DDOS is many thousands of dollars per month   Thought you should know before you made the call. lol
