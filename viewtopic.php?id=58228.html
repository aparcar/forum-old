You cannot have two 192.168.178.0/24 networks, one on each interface. Either you bridge both interfaces together (and have effectively just one interface), or configure different networks on each interface. Right now, your OpenWrt box knows that it needs to contact the gateway at 192.168.178.101 to reach the internet, but it does not know whether that box is on eth0 or eth1.
