Hi,

Recently I've noticed that my wrt54gl box has problem with throughput. After doing some tests, it showed up that is cannot pass the limit of 2mbps even when links are reported to be at level of 48 or 52mbps. Both the ap and the client are in the same room. The throughput is always 2mbps regardless whether I am copying files between two pcs connected via wireless or when I am downloading stuff from my pc to the router.

As a first thought I've checked for strange nvram variables and found one of them wl0_rate which was set to 4, what would mean that the speed would be 'truncated' to 2mbps. Unfortunately after unsetting this var, comiting and even rebooting I still got 2mbps limit. Thinking that there might be something else in the nvram (i was playing with most of the variables some time ago) i run nbd's nvram-clean script (output included).

Today I've noticed something strange, wl utility reports rates (wl rate) of 24 to 36mps - it changes every few seconds, but the at the pc i get rates of 52 or 48 mbps...

Could this mismatch be the reason of limited throughput? If so, how to get rid of it? ;-)
