Hi slboat,

Nice to see you here, and thank you for these pictures!

Nice work, but I see that you are taking the GND for the LVTTL console on the USB shield, which is NOT connected to GND directly (only through caps). I suggest rather to connect the GND wire to either:
- the large C7 1206 cap (top PCB side) on the terminal facing the SDRAM, or
- the large C114 1206 cap (bottom PCB side) on the terminal close to the SPI Flash chip
- use a color different from black for TP_IN to avoid confusion with GND
- put some hot glue around all solder joints to secure all this!

Squonk
21 Aug 2012, 18:08

Hi slboat,

Yes, the big large pad you used is good GND. It is normally used to connect an external antenna shield, but it is ok to use it for GND as you did!

This was not the case for USB shields, that are not connected directly to GND, only through the specified C7/C114 capacitors. As these capacitors are large 1206, you can take the capacitor terminal away from the USB shield as GND, it is also ok.

The TL-WR703N and the TL-MR3020 are sister routers and share 90% of their schematic, so the MR3020 uses the exact same SDRAM and Flash chips as the WR703N.
