I'm trying to use client-mode OpenVPN on top of MultiWAN.
OpenVPN works fine when MultiWAN is disabled. MultiWAN by itself works well, but when it's on, OpenVPN connects and a few minutes later disconnects, then connects and disconnects again and again. Moreover, even when it's connected, I cannot access/ping anything on the other side of the VPN (192.168.0.0/16).

My MultiWAN's rule dictate the traffic to the OpenVPN server's IP to be routed via a specific WAN (without any kind of balancing)

This is the routing table when MultiWAN is on, and OpenVPN is running:

What am I doing wrong?
