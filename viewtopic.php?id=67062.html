Some of the older models have been patched and are in DD and in LEDE, but I do not think any are in CC, other than the 6416.  I know GLi is submitting patches, but how fast they get incorporated is unclear.

There are "clean" versions, which are GLi versions similar to trunck (no GLI GUI) available for most here.
http://www.gl-inet.com/firmware/
Look for a folder named clean.  The AR-300M is not available yet 'clean'.

For a travel router, the GLi GUI is a great tool for connecting to a station, much easier than Luci.  It is something you will need to do every time you change locations.  It also has USB configured, and the latest versions have VPN installed.  Just load a config to your service.
