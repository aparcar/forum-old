Post #3
mk24
22 Dec 2016, 00:36

That adapter has a Realtek 8187 chip.  Realtek chips have limited support under Linux, including no AP mode in many cases.  I'm pretty sure the 8187 is one of those cases.  So, you will need a different adapter.

Use "iw list' to see which modes are supported in your driver.

I finally founded why It didn't work!
I just add to install required package!

As explained in the Wifi encryption configuration:
WPA-Encryption requires wpa_supplicant (for client mode) or hostapd (for AP and ad-hoc mode) to be installed
