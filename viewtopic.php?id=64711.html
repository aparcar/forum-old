riodoro
9 May 2016, 00:23

Create two "cron" jobs under "system / scheduled tasks" with (eg):

* 22 * * *  ifconfig wlan0 down
* 8 * * * ifconfig wlan0 up

This one will stop wlan0 every day at 22:00 and enable it at 08:00.
