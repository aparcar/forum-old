You can not actually remove packages that are part of the flashed image.  They say that they are unuinstalled, but they are not really uninstalled.

You have not stated your device, but there are a number of 4MB devices like the TP Link MR-3020, one of the grand daddys of the class, but now somewhat frail in it's specifications.  There is something called EX-Root (see the wiki) that lets you use a flash drive to expand the devices capabilities.

There are a number of Travel Routers with more Umph today (16\64 as opposed to 4\32) for short money.  See the wiki TOH.  https://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/start Read the top and go to "Full View" and sort on Travel in the first column.
