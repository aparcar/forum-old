The 1043 is a nice router. As to bricking: anyone can brick any router given enough ineptitude and lack of reading!
If I were in your position, I would narrow down the field first by the most taxing requirements:
5 Gb ports,
then
wireless N
then PoE (though that might limit the list a lot!)
Then see if you can find the router on ebay etc, then do a final check to see if it is in the supported list.
I know nothing about IMS, so your best bet is searching this and voip in the forum/wiki. My bet is that there is something in openWRT that will help, but as I said, I don't know.
