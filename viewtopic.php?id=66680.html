You want to look at "dumb AP" and WDS. 

Dumb AP will do what you want to do as far as allowing the physical connections and setting up an AP.

Seamless roaming may be a bit more difficult depending on the config and control of your "modem".  Read the WDS, BATMAN and other wiki articles on MESH.
