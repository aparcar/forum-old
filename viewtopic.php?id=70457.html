Your ADSL modem can be setup 2 different ways:
1)  Set the modem in "Bridge" mode.  This passes the packets directly to your router and would be what you would use with the WAN settings you listed in your post
2)  My ADSL modem has simple routing capabilities and I entered the ppp0a settings in the modem.  I then used the modem routing capabilities to create a subnet for my SOHO router.
See Customer Comment Netis DL4201.

You did not mention your modem settings and they will impact your OpenWRT set up.  Also, your VC/VPI settings will be different.
