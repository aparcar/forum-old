tapper wrote:

I wood like to show what routers i have bin running in the last lets say 30 days in my sig.

Okay, that is something entirely different from "statistics". That is an individual display of anecdotal data.

Technically speaking it is doable, and it's not even hard, but it's a metric ton of work bringing all the systems together. One would have to create a database with unique, secure and user-maintainable identification, possibly even an interface for the data it contains and how it is output, create a package, create a LuCI interface, and finally extend the forum software. And all of that just to display a string under your posts ... I'd say it doesn't justify the effort.
