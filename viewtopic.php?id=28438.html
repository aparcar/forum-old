to clear things up:

brcm-2.4-buildtarget is for brcm47xx-chipsets with kernel 2.4 and the broadcom binary wifi-driver.

brcm47xx-buildtarget is also for brcm47xx-chipsets, but with kernel 2.6 and if you want wifi you can use the opensource b43-driver (which is mostly stable and useable since backfire)

but I'm curious about your second download link (wrt54gs), are you sure you got it from the brcm-2.4-directory? I don't think so!
