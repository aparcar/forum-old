If you are connecting through a router, the speed drops to 120 - 130Mb / s. People advised to activate the two external ip from your provider, after which the first use as a bridge to the first exit to the LAN and one for Wi-Fi and other ports! Communicated with by the ISP, they said that they will get the router this mode and you must buy a separate switch! I would like to start to try, but I can not find settings to do this! I understand that in the regime of two ip - a set on the PC and the other on the router in the mode of WAN? Help me please, I have been unable to find information!

config 'interface' 'loopback'
option 'ifname' 'lo'
option 'proto' 'static'

config 'interface' 'wan'

option 'reset' '1'

config 'switch_vlan'
option 'device' 'rtl8366rb'
option 'vlan' '1'
option 'ports' '1 2 3 4 5t'
