The integrated 802.11 b/g PHY in this machine's processor, the BCM5354, is of type LP, which appears to mean 'low power'. Now whether it's low power or high power of universal cosmic all-power doesn't matter; what matters is that the LP PHY is an unknown entity and unsupported in current drivers. The reverse engineering effort is on-going; you can check progress at http://bcm-v4.sipsolutions.net/.

Once those specs are complete, then drivers can be written by completely separate parties.
