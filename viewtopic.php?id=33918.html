you solder your D+ wire to R185 and your D- wire to R183 (in both cases to the side facing to the middle of the board. then solder a wire to gnd (there are plenty of points) and connect a 7805 (or better some step-down converter) to the power jack and basically you've got everything you need for usb (like described in the wiki).
