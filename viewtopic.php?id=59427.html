Hi Newtman,

I think we all made the same mistake. To ran transmission on OpenWrt, you do not need uPnP at all. As for why Transmission cannot accept incoming request, it is usually related to your ISP.

For now, I just enable "uTp" protocol in Transmission, and my torrents are working fine.

Hope this helps.
