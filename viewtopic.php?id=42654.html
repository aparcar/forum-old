mp3 encoding is very cpu intensive, you probably can't do it in real time on your router. Just did a quick test; on my WNR3500L encoding a 10s wav file took 150s.

EDIT: To lower the wav file sizes you could record a single channel with lower quality.

For single channel cd quality:

arecord -f S16_LE -r 44100

Lower quality:

arecord -f S16_LE -r 22050

Even lower:

arecord -f S8 -r 22050

Each should be half the size of the previous.
