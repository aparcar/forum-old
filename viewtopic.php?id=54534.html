Hi,
Installed Belkin N600DB firmware (F9K1102 v3) firmware on it and ... it seems to works !
you can find it here

To flash:
Connect Fonera to internet (yellow port to your provider modem)
Connect PC to one of the black network port
Power on Fonera
Open FONERA web interface 192.168.10.1/index.htm
Go to firmware page and upload Belkin firmware.
Wait untill the FONERA restarts (fix green internet led)
Reset Fonera to factory settings (push reset button for more than 5s)
Wait untill the FONERA restarts (fix green internet led)
Open FONERA web interface 192.168.2.1 and enjoy

Tested up to now:
- Led : Ko (wifi and lan led seems to be reallocated to wps authentification, internet led ok)
- Lan : Ok
- Wifi : Ok
- Usb : Ok
- dlna : Ok

For the moment, i haven't tried to flash FON firmware back: I have made a backup of partitions but i feel quite uncomfortable with partition flashing

As usual, i cannot be held responsible for damaged or bricked hardware, you do it at your own risks !
