Post #31
Villeneuve
4 Oct 2016, 20:12

Yes you can flash factory repeatedly. No there is nothing to gain in terms of image content differences, It is the setup of your partitioning i.e transparent to you as an end user. Have done it many times on my c7v2 when fighting an image compilation issue. Supposedly less flash wear using sysupgrade. I would stick with that once you have an image running. Should not be an issue between "family" builds, but I would be inclined to use factory going from gargoyle-owrt-lede, sysupgrade once I have an image in place.
