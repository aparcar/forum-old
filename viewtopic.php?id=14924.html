I could be wrong, but I don't think you can do what you want through the WRT54G boxes.
I think you have to manage your laptop's wireless card, instead.  Check with your distro's repos to see if there's an app that manages your wireless connections automatically ("roaming") so that they pick-up and associate with the different access points as you physically move between them.
