I don't think your problem is due to the config path. The root cause is your system time, everytime it restarts if you don't have NTP configured or your NTP just couldn't connect to internet to get the correct time, then transmission deamon start first, then all your torrent will lost because transmission has time checking logic.


Ensure you have internet connect ready before start your router then ensure ntp is configured.
