You are using the wrong IP address to enter failsafe.
You need to manually assign your computer to use 192.168.1.x
x = any number except 1

And then telnet into the router, and I suggest you execute firstboot and then reboot to fix your problem.
Please read
http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/howto/generic.failsafe
