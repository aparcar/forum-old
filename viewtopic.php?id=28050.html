I am planing to make some wiki pages for the different CPUs (called cores), but this is going to take some time. The different between CPUs can be as huge as the difference between the AMD AthlonXP and Athlon64, or the intel Netburst and the Intel Core2. But it is a bit tedious to collect all the Information.

Also, we are searching for ways to make the Table of Hardware let you choose between multiple features and present you the devices. As of now, you have to work with <Crtl>+<F> to accomplish that. So many routers aren't supported at the present time, it won't take you long.

Also I do not see the point in asking here, because not many people own different devices, so they cannot really compare them.
