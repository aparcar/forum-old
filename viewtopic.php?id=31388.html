Sorry...it is dual-radio, but only one radio operates in the 2.4GHz band.  The other is 5GHz.  I'm not aware of an off-the-shelf router with two 2.4GHz radios.  You'd probably have to build your own based on a RouterStation or RouterBoard plus two wireless cards.
