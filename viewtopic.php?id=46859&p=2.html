Post #36
FriedZombie
30 Nov 2013, 21:06
nebbia88 wrote:

is that me or you wrote 8500 instead of 0850 as hwid ?

You are correct, seems like a typo on the side of tp-link, since there is no board difference between the v1.0 and up.
Also the bootloaders are the same. The issue is only there if your wa850 shipped with the 3.13.27 Build121119 Rel.609994n other 1.0 versions aren't a problem either.

the board id should be, 08500001 but the wa850 mentioned with build121119 expects 85000011 as hwid id.
Also the previous solution I posted I tried it against one of my wa850's that has also v1.0 on the back (and is the one with easy ttl access) and flashed it with the strange tp-link firmware build121119 (with mtd) to see what would work.
Also compared the u-boot part if it was different from other v1.x versions but that wasn't the case either.
