10 Dec 2015, 20:09

OK, I've tried some more. According to http://wiki.darkhaven.org/wiki/Netgear_WN2500RP_v1 the WN2500RPv1 has two WiFi-Chips:
- Broadcom BCM5358UB0 for 2.4 GHz
- Broadcom BCM43236 for 5 GHz

https://wireless.wiki.kernel.org/en/use … /brcm80211 tells us that the BCM43236 ref. 3 (BCM43236B) is supported by the brcmfmac kernel module. Unfortunately, we have ref. 1 or 2.

According to https://www.mail-archive.com/openwrt-de … 24762.html the BCM5358UB0 (N-PHY rev 9) is also not supported by the broadcom-wl driver.

So it seems that OpenWRT is running on this device, but there is no chance to get WiFi working...
