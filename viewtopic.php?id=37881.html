It seems the dongle is your least problem.
For some reason the overlay filesystem is readonly. I suggest to reset it to defaults, then only install ubs-modeswitch and usb-modeswitch-data.

Also your dongle provides three ttys, ttyUSB0, ttyUSB1 and ttyUSB2 - try all of them, often the first one isn't the data port.
