unclean ground.

I have it too. I have to be patient and patient and try again and try again and make sure it has a clean connection and it works.

Evidence of that was when I backed up my factory rom using md command in u-boot. It took a few goes to not have interference on the serial line.

pl2303's are fussy about the grounds. get a CP2102, or try your luck with a max232.
