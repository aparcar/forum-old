It would be a real shame to trash the WR841ND. It's a nice unit, and getting a serial connexion on that thing is easy..

The USB adapter is £1 on eBay. Then it's four screws, two minutes of soldering, a couple of crocodile clips and you're good to go.

If you have never tried connecting to a router over a serial connexion, I definitely recommend you try it. It's way more fun than it should be!

And if you still don't want to do it, at least give the unit to someone who will!

;o) Cor
