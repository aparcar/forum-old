Do you know what chips are in it?  How large is the RAM and the flash?

It has 4 antennas because they are two sets of 2x2 single band antennas instead of dual band.  Performance on each band is still just 2x2 n standard (up to 300 Mbps).

For the same price you can buy a TP-Link WDR3600, which offers gigabit ethernet, extra USB port, detachable antennas, and known good OpenWRT compatibility versus the LB-Link.
