mk24
6 Dec 2016, 20:25

Flash size is coded into the firmware, but RAM size is auto determined at boot, so you don't have to modify it for different RAM.  If you get into the bootloader via serial, you could manually flash a WR741 or generic firmware, it may work.  You probably want to use an older version and custom build to take out as much stuff as you can.
