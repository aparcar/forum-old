It might be just the browser cache. Icons & other things were changed in the last few weeks.
You might try with another browser, and/or with clearing browser cache first.

Additionally, it is a good practice to make the config from scratch every now and then, as the built-in defaults do change and preserving the old configs does not allow new defaults to get applied. I do not remember any specific setting between rc5 and 10.03.1 which would cause that effect, but resetting the router to defaults might be a good idea in any case.
