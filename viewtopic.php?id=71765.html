Ensure you have set the high throughput mode on your wireless configuration (HT40+ or HT40-, depending on your base channel frequency): https://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/uci/wirele … vices_only

Then either ensure that there are no other Wi-Fi stations in your area, or use the 'noscan' option in your wireless config to override the high throughput fallback mode. The fallback mode will make your router use only 20 MHz channels when there's a lot of traffic on the 2.4 GHz band, in order to prevent the radio band from becoming congested.
