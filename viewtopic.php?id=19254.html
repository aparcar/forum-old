Post #2
bbugger
8 Mar 2009, 01:32

Of corse you can:
take a look at /etc/config/network.

change it to somthing like

config 'interface' 'lan'
        option 'type' 'bridge'
        option 'ifname' 'eth0.0'
        option 'proto' 'static'
        option 'ipaddr' '10.0.0.1'
        option 'netmask' '255.0.0.0'

Maybe you also have to change the dhcp options...

Regards
