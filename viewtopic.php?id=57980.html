I have flashed my AG300H several times with the TFTP method.
It is the easiest and most convenient way to do an install/upgrade.

If you are using Windows (and have installed tftp client from Programs & Features -> Turn Windows features on or off), you might have to create inbound and outbound "allow" rules in the Windows Firewall for the tftp client. I have faced tftp transfer failures due to that.

Then you just follow the instructions in the wiki.

PS: In Barrier Breaker final release, the reset button works as expected. I think it works in final Attitude Adjustment too, but I'm not 100% sure (I don't remember).
