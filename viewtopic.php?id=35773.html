Dear Forum!

Yesterday the craziest thing happened to me while I was connected with my laptop through wireless connection to my router, which is a Bytton Topex GPRS aka  Asus WL-500G Deluxe. I was doing a speedtest on Speedtest.net just out of curiosity and when the upload was about to finish, the wireless connection broke and the router disappeared from the radar. Neither my laptop nor any other laptop saw it (I tried two others). So I rebooted the router, the connection came back on, but when I did the Speedtest again, the same thing happened. I was shocked because after 2 years of perfect functioning the router started to act crazy for no reason.

Last evening I reproduced the situation many times, tried to connect and Speedtest from different laptops, tried with OpenWRT Backfire 10.03 and 10.03.1, but in all cases I ended up with a dead wireless connection. Obviously the Speedtest could never finish successfully. The LED for AIR stays turned on, but it's not blinking is at normally does. I am able to connect to the router through cable, Internet works fine, I can do as many Speedtests I want. It's the wireless connection that's dying only.

So I was wondering if you could help me figure out what on earth could suddenly go so wrong!? Because I for one didn't see anything bad in the logs. Please see below. Thank you.
