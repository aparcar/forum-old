Post #6
Zajec
4 Mar 2013, 11:03
siibananda.patro wrote:

Actually the problem is like I am trying to run hostapd in E3200.
So, which version of OpenWRT do supported in E3200 and will it support hostapd or not?

E3200 is a BCM5357. It contains integrated wireless with N-PHY rev 9. That N-PHY is not supported yet by any of the two drivers (b43 or brcmsmac). I've no idea about broadcom-wl.

E3200 has also extra radio connected over some internal USB port (you won't see it after opening the case). That extra USB radio is BCM43236 V2 and Broadcom doesn't plan to support it any time soon (or never at all).
