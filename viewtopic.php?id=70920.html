IMHO it is not that hard to recognize that "Conventions per characteristic" is only the explanation for what is expected as values in the dataentry and the information contained in the conventions is not related to the device in the dataentry above. At least when you read in the conventions "Model DIR-509" (which does not fit to any device) and a column called "Example" you should realize this.
