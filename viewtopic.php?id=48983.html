I am back in business again! Thanks for the hint.

What have I done precisely:

In contrast to the "[HOWTO] Unbrick TP-Link..." I was able to pick up the messages 

 Who has 192.168.0.66? Tell 192.168.0.86

on the LAN port, not on the WAN port. Also note the .0. instead of the .1. described.

As mentioned above, the latest FW TL-WDR3600_V1_130909.zip contains a "boot" file and does not work when sent over by TFTP.
Instead I used TL-WDR3600_V1_130129.zip. This procedure took few mins and was successful.

I then used the webinterface to flash the router to the latest FW, also successful.

Cheers!
