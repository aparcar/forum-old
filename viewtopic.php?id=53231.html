Have you made any progress with this???  Lifted pads are bad.  You may have to scratch the green coating off of the board with a blade to try to find the copper path underneath that the pad connected to.  Then, you'll have to somehow solder to that.  This can be quite difficult & will cause some suffering.  You'll need a bright light & a good magnifying lens or jeweler's loupe.
