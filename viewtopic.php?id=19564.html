You might consider a packet radio device like those made by FreeWave.  I was able to get a 900 MHz 56 kbps symmetric connection setup on WhiteRussian (see http://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=15913) without too much trouble.  You would probably get better bitrates with such a device instead of a walkie-based setup.  Ebay might have some of the older 33 kbaud devices for cheap.
