Hi! I was working with this quite a while ago so forgive me if I don't remember the specifics.

I used the netmask of 255.255.255.255 because that's the mask that was assigned to my ppp0 device. The point of passthru is to have your ethernet device behave as if it's the 3g modem, so I would assign it the same network properties your 3g modem gets.

I would also recommend using iproute2 and the instructions that watts posted: https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php … 640#p96640
