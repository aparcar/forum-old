I have several WRTSL54GS units running OpenWRT v0.9 and the signal is fine for much longer distances than 15 feet.  The SL does have an inferior radio than the GL, but not THAT much worse, it's just missing the onboard amp.  You should get at least 80-90% the range of a GL unit.  You can de-solder the existing antenna and add an RP-TNC connector if you want options of a better antenna, it's not that difficult.

I would suggest you look at the obvious problems, perhaps interference, defective antenna solder joint, or client issues.
