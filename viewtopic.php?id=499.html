Hi all.

I am new to this forum and have only recently started playing with OpenWRT. I've been mostly working with Sveasoft's firmware so far and have built a couple of tools that I thought I would share with you all since they also seem to work with OpenWRT.

I know they aren't great technical marvels but I still believe they might help a person or 2.

The first tool is called Easy Port Forward. It is a simple PHP iptables script generator that allows you to easily forward or redirect ports.

It is available at http://www.nucleosphere.com/EPF/

The second one is a replacement to chillispot's hotspotlogin.cgi script. Its meant as a proof of concept and works quite well... Its intended for people who want to customize their capture portal page.

It is available at http://www.nucleosphere.com/minispot/

Any suggestions/questions/feedback will be greatly appreciated :-)

Best regards to all
