Sorry for late reply, had some connection problems.
Anyway, as I guessed, my router's MAC address was banned! After lots of searching, I came upon the wireless configuration page in the wiki where it had been explained that it's not possible to use the macaddr option under wifi-device in the wireless configuration file to override the MAC address (what I was trying to do at the beginning) but it is possible to do so by adding the same option under wifi-iface and override the MAC, and yeah, it did work too.
Now I'm back on track, connected to uni's AP, and leeching the hell out of the connection; the admin can try banning the MAC address as many times as he can now, I will change it again in the blink of an eye.

Big thanks to everyone for the help.
