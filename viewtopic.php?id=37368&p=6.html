That looks like a bad connection or soldering, check that they D+ and D- aren't touching nearby pins. Or maybe the pins are broken.
With the router powered off, put a multimeter in diode mode. Touch the + on the router's ground and the - on each USB data pin.
They should measure 400-600. If any of them doesn't measure that, it's a bad connection.
