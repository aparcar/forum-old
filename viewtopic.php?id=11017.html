The WGR614 v6 is not supported.  The entry in the table of hardware was added by someone who did not know what they were doing.  The flash/ram values were listed in megabits, but they misinterpreted it as megabytes, which is what they noted on the wiki.  It already has an entry on the Unsupported page with the proper values.  8MB of ram is too little to be a useful device running OpenWrt, and the kernel alone will barely fit on a 1MB flash chip; OpenWrt will not run on it.
