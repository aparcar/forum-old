How much is affordable? 128MB flash is a lot! Most of the better routers has 8MB or 16MB flash. My favorite is TL WR1043nd v2, it has 64MB RAM, 8MB flash, but you can extend the flash with usb flash drive, it is cheap and runs at 720MHz.

The Netgear WNDR3700 v4 or the WNDR4300 might be what you need. Make sure to investigate how well they're supported by OpenWrt though, it looks like 12.09 (Attitude Adjustment) does not support them, there might be some support in trunk ('Barrier Breaker'), and I've read about someone creating images for the latter.
