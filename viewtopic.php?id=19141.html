The current release and trunk of openwrt do not support the switch in the dir-300, therfore you see no wan interface to configure.
To get support for the programmable switch in the dir-300 you have to apply some patches yourself, see:
http://inst.eecs.berkeley.edu/~pathorn/ip175c/phylib/
