I've had the same issue, I have solved by writing the correct timezone in /etc/TZ:
echo UTC-1 > /etc/TZ
(I'm in UTC+1, but in TZ I've to write -1, check for your country)
then I ran the command:
/etc/init.d/ntpclient enable
At reboot it was working
