Well you were right,

I went to Failsafe mode:
"Power up your router. When the 'SYS' light starts to blink you have about 1 seconds to press the WPS/Reset button on the back-left of the router and hold it until the blinking gets faster."

Then i was able to get into the router by telnet with putty, and write "firstboot" to reset all settings to default.

From there i upgraded the OpenWrt and install the LuCi gui.
