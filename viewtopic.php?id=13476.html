bednar.jakub wrote:

Which kernel versions are tested and stable on ADM5120??

Kamikaze tip builds with 2.6.23.1 and runs very nicely for me.  I've had no lock-ups at all, unless it was related to USB (a WIP)..

The instructions here  http://www.student.tue.nl/Q/t.f.a.wilms/adm5120/  are very much out of date, and were more of a proof-of-concept than a fully working solution.  I think you will be much better off using the Kamikaze subversion tip, and raising tickets if you have problems, because at least there is some support.
