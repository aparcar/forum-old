lyndenwld wrote:

do I simply bridge
guest with vlan 6
and private with vlan 10

Yes you need at least one bridge interface for each vlan. You don't have to assign an IP address to the interface if you don't want to. The ethernet bridge will work anyway and forward packets between the physical interfaces of the bridge. (Of course you need at least one IP address for configuring openwrt.)
