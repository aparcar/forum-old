The -n option will make the sysupgrade work just like a fresh install.  It saves no config files, not even those specified in /etc/sysupgrade.conf.

The only options I currently see for preserving specific files are: modifying the tar.gz created by the -b option or making my own tar.gz to provide with the -f option.
