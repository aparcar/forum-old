I just spent about two weeks trying to figure this out so I thought I'd share the solution with you :-)

As far as I can tell RT-N16 support is only in the "bleeding edge" trunk builds known as Barrier Breaker. 

I used the most recently dated firmware at this page. I used the Asus utility, never did get it to work via TFTP (I'm on Windows).
http://openwrt.razvi.ro/
Same brcm47xx sort of naming scheme.

All the steps should be the same with the holding reset button etc. It rebooted pretty much immediately, no waiting multiple minutes (I could tell since I had an infinite ping to 192.168.1.1 going). Your mileage may vary...
