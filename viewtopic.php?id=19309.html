I am trying to configure an Asus WL-500W to bridge the switch, uplink, and wifi together as a single non-smart bridge.  I don't really care about the lan and uplink being bridged and haven't tested it, but don't expect that to be a major issue.  Even if it doesn't work it's not critical at this time.

The wireless however, is odd.  The radio is working, as the SSID is broadcast.  I've even reduced it to operating in clear-mode.  Yet I seem utterly unable to hear any wireless packets.
