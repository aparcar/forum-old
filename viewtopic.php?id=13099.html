Without you giving your router hardware, and the kernel version you are running, we can't tell you for sure your problem, however, I will attempt to read your mind and guess that you are using a broadcom-based device, and decided to try the 2.6 kernel.

Now, at the moment, 2.6 does not support broadcom wireless hardware. You need to either use the 2.4 kernel or work without wireless.

as for an SVN of 7.09, try the tag, which is the official release of 7.09.

You can find that here: https://svn.openwrt.org/openwrt/tags/kamikaze_7.09/

Edit: I see you're using atheros hardware, at least, based on the error messages (i'm blind, i guess).... try the official tag for kami 7.09 and see how that works.
