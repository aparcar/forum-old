I've recently upgraded three routers from 0.9 White Russian to 10.03.1 backfire. Yay! All went well, with nary a forum posting to clarify why QoS wasn't working or how to get firewall logging enabled. I figured it all out on my own power.

Two of my routers are Linksys WRT54G v2 and the other is a Buffalo WBR2-G54S -- not exactly speed demons, but they work fine when just sitting their routing and firewalling.

But, I notice when running the web interface, particularly screens that refresh, the CPU utilization is "high".  I see the highest CPU utilization when running the scrolling realtime graphs. For example, at the moment my main router sometimes shows a load average of 2.8.
