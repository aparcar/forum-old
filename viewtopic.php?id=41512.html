You probably FRIED your router!

TP_IN/TP_OUT serial console signals are 2.7V only, and RS232 is -12V/+12V (well at least -5V/+5V)!!!

This is why you need an adapter, preferably an LVTTL (+3.3V) UART to USB, NOT a simple RS232 cable.

So my guess is that at least the serial console electronic internal buffers are dead, if not worse
