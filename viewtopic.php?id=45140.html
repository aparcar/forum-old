Actually I believe there is a hardware limitation.
According to the data sheets MR3420 is powered by the AR724X SoC.
And that SoC only supports USB 1.1, which is a problem when you are using it with a 4G Modem because USB 1.1 cannot transfer data to and fro the modem at 4G rates.
Which means that even though it may recognise the 4G Modem it will probably never hit 4G rates.
This is just my theory, it will be good if you can test it out and share the results.
