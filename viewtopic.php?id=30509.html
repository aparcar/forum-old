Correction ... I have 7 wzr-hp-g300nh's now.  Well they don't all belong to me but I manage them

The last three I purchased looks like they have the Ethernet switch chip change that several on the forums have been talking about.

I was running the latest Backfire rc5 on all of my other ones but these three new ones would not flash Backfire.  After much head banging I found some forums that talked about the E switch being a different part and that trunk had the latest.  At one point it looks like there were patches for what was internally being called a wzr-hp-g301nh.

I did a svn update of trunk and rebuilt things fresh and I was able to flash the three new ones I just received.  Took a while to figure out what was going on.
