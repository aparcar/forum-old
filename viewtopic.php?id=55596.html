After messing with Chaos Calmer for a little bit and deciding i didn't like it i flashed the Barrier Breaker image for the 6408 on my 6416 (im too lazy to build from scratch) and it seems to work fine;

As you see the full 16Mb is allocated correctly, wifi works fine....everything seems dandy.

Keep in mind that you will get a 'Image check 'platform_check_image' failed.' error when trying to do sysupgrade with this 'wrong' image but just force it;

sysupgrade -F openwrt-ar71xx-generic-gl-inet-v1-squashfs-sysupgrade.bin

You might even be able to force flash 703n images on it like that
