"NAS server, Bittorrent client" are pretty much out of question with normal consumer routers, I'd suggest building or getting an old pc to do the job. I/O writes/reads will crash your modem with  nas and/or bittorrent, as those products weren't meant to do such tasks. With fine tuning you could run bittorrent client with 1 torrent at very limited peer count and speed limit
