johnisaacson
9 May 2013, 10:14

One has to remember the all communication with a router is a two way process.
This is known a " deuplex"  in the business.
Cranking up your router is great but what about the other say your laptop?
What have you done there?
If you have made no changes then sure one may be better but internet is not a one way process and depending one what you want to do you may in fact reduce
your overall performance!
A high gain ohmni directional antenna with a greater db gain will help but using
a directed array will give much better performace without the other inherent
penalties.
Just a suggestion.

~john

If the bandwidth usage is asymmetrical (almost always the case), the end that is sending more data needs more power to maintain a good data rate. In the other direction, it can use a lower bitrate encoding that needs much less power. Keep in mind that your cell phone transmits on the order of a few watts at most while the tower is on the order of 50W or more.
