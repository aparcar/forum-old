The problem: when I try to connect to luci first time, connecting does only work directly after starting the uhttpd (/etc/init.d/uhttpd start). Half a minute later, and uhttpd is running into 98% when try to connect with https.
Even after that moment I'm not able to get the certificate - same problem, uhttpd is running into 98%. I have to restart uhttpd and then download the certificate immediately after starting.
