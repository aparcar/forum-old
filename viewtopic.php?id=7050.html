Which filesystem type are you using? I had problems with jffs2, but I successfully flashed my wl500gp with a squashfs one.

banane wrote:

Original Asus device discovery does not find a device

I had a similar problem, and solved it connecting the PC to the WAN port.
The IP address should be your last LAN IP address (i.e 192.168.1.1).

I had to use Asus Utility but you should not need the Discovery Tool. I never did.

I hope  it's useful.
