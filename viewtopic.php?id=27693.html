Generally speaking, latest trunk may or may not brick your router 50/50.

- The PAPRD patches may render your router permanently deaf. I don't know the actual status of this serious issue. Sometimes is ON, sometimes is OFF.

Go on, is always welcome new blood for the bleeding edge

Thanks for the reply. I'll start from the beginning, shall state its objectives and if I'm wrong, please correct me.
Task:
1. Hardware mod - adding USB
2. Installing the firmware and kernel patch to support USB
3. Connecting Arduino and extra flash media to the router.
That is implemented:
1. Actually the Hardware mod.
2. It is now established Backfire 10.03.1 (r23714)

What do I do next:
1. I collect the firmware (100% works for you r23281):

svn co-r 23281 svn :/ / svn.openwrt.org / openwrt / trank
cd trunk
. / Scripts / feeds update-a
. / Scripts / feeds install-a

make menuconfig (here I choose the desired packages for USB and Arduino)

2. Then produce minor kernel patch:
-------------------------------------------------- ------------
 --- A/files/arch/mips/ar71xx/Kconfig
+ + + B/files/arch/mips/ar71xx/Kconfig

        select AR71XX_DEV_LEDS_GPIO

        default n
 
 config AR71XX_MACH_TL_WR841N_V1
