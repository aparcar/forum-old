28 Feb 2013, 00:29

The reset button was not driving mode tftp, so I was trying to access via serial port.

As I typed the IPCONFIG boot loader displayed the ip 192.168.1.6 as you said, but could not ping or transfer it via tftp firmware. (With lan ip 192.168.1.2/24 on my notebook)

solution:

--- RealTek (RTL8186) at +0800 2007.01.05-16:49 version 1.4a [16bit] (180MHz)
In sys signature at 00010000!
sys checksum error at 00020000!
sys checksum error at 00030000!
\> ipconfig 10.10.10.5 <enter>

In my lan interface: 10.10.10.1/24

After that, I used a tftp client and the firmware was transferred!!

Thank you!
