hi, yes, it is possible. I am using asus wl500g and I shaped p2P in such a way that it does not take too much bandwidth. My rules are not flexible though so it often happens that not all the bandwidth is used, but it was actually my aim since I am sharing it with some other people (I am on a wifi AP).

What you need to do: install openwrt (there is enough information in wiki, you can find some here: www.macsat.com as well),  get all posible upgrades for iptables (use package tracker and search the forums), load all modules you need, read something here: www.netfilter.org and here: www.lartc.org and write your rules. That should be it:-).

Easier way: go here http://wl500g.info/, upload the newes Olegs firmware search for wondershaper - I do not know how effective it is, though.
