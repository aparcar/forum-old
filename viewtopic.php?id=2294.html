JTAG worked for me after I turned my router into 100% brick and nothing else worked, no ping, no tftp, short pint, etc.  JTAG cable allows to write directly to the flash memory.  Since you are getting pings give tftp another try and wait a few minutes after upload has finished before resetting.  Then try to boot into failsafe mode (hold reset button for 2 sec once you see dmz light come on).  Otherwise solder jtag cable, all you need is a db25 connector a few wires and a few resistors - should be easy.
