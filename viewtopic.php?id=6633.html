Hi,

developer Nico has asked me to setup a list of packages which have priority in being ported to buildroot-ng.

So what I'd like you to do is:
1. Have a look at https://dev.openwrt.org/wiki/OpenWrtPackageToDo and go through the packages that haven't been ported yet
2. Decide which of those are the most important to you
3. Post the package names to this thread

Please stick to the available package list and don't use this thread to suggest new packages. That's always a different subject and needs to be discussed elsewhere.

There's no warranty that your package wish is going to be fulfilled first; the goal of this thread is to get a rough idea of what's important to port first.

Thanks for your help
