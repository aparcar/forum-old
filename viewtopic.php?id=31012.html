I followed the advice here http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/howto/virtualbox , and converted a VMDK image by myself.

1) I downloaded http://downloads.openwrt.org/backfire/1 … xt2.img.gz
2) un-gzipped it to get a plain ext2 image.
3) Used the built-in VBoxManage tool in VirtualBox to convert the image:
    "c:\Program Files\Oracle\VirtualBox\VBoxManage.exe" convertfromraw --format VMDK <filename> <outputfile>

Download link to my VMDK image: http://koti.welho.com/hnyman1/Openwrt/o … 1-rc5.vmdk
