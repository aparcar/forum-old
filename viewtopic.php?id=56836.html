Make sure your PC's ethernet port is set for DHCP ("Obtain IP Address Automatically" in windows).  When you connect it should show the router gave your PC an address in the 192.168.1.x range.  A new flash of OpenWRT starts with wifi disabled.  You will have to connect by ethernet.
