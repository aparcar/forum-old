Installing kmod-i2c-core (3.3.8-1) to root...

This dependency will be broken every time you modify the kernel .config (check how .vermagic is generated in include/kernel-defaults.mk). If you used exactly the same config and just enabled a module, you can try to force installing the package, if you enabled anything further it wont work. Why dont you create an image out of what you just compiled for your router?
