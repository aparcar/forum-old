TP-Link Archer C5
TP-Link Archer C7

Price is not a problem.

the pricing o WRT1900AC and other "high end" AC routers like Netgear R8000 is near x86/x86-64 based hardware (depending on features:

- pcengines APU1D with mini PCIe slots for Wireless Cards
- upcoming Soekris net6801
other embedded ITX or IPC ("industrial" PC ) boards
or small server boards like
- Supermicro A1SAi-2750F or other models

Depending on the solution you can achieve better NAS Performance, have USB3 connectivity+performance, better torrent/DHT performance or consolidate "Home Solutions"* (with/without VM) in a single box.
With "VM" (Virtual Machine) because atm there are several different "embedded" / specialized Distributions/Packages that are available. Not all of them are manageable over 1 single web interface.
Like FreeNAS specializes in NAS (w. ZFS) while OpenWrt specializes in Network/Routing

* provide local servers/proxies for Streaming Media, Backup, Collaborative Work, Messaging ...
