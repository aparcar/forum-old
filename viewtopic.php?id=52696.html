Finally I figure this out. If you follow the example config on http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/uci/network … nt.dhcpv6, you just got a global address but failed to get access to the internet. You should add 'option interface wan' below 'config dhcp wan6' and you will get all things to work after restarting odhcpd service.
But here is another problem: you will lose internet connection after a while, so you need to restart this service intervally to make all clients get global ipv6 address and the internet, this maybe a bug or something else, but at least you have ipv6 connections. I will edit the wiki later.
