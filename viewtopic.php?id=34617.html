Unless you want to compile your own modules go with ralink there is support for most of their wireless chipsets, and most of them do ap mode.
I've used rtl8187 rt73 and rt2800usb all worked fine in sta mode.
There are also support for these chipsets in openwrt
zydas / atheros usb
realtek rtl8187
prism54 usb.
