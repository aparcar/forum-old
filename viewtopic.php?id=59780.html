JohnDj wrote:
Borromini wrote:

R7000 runs on OpenWrt but no wireless.

802.11ac and 'rock stable' are not two things you can mention in one sentence so far with FOSS drivers.

Wireless on R7000 seems to run on dd-wrt... so why not on openwrt?

Because DD-Wrt has NDAs in place which allow them to use closed source drivers from Broadcom.

There's also work being done on Mediatek 802.11ac platforms in OpenWrt, don't know how good that support is. It's what I'm keeping an eye on but then again I'm not going 802.11ac anytime soon; it's just in case I do need to replace my router.
