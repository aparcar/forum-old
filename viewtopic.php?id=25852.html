In /etc/config/wireless
# MAC FILTERING
        # Default policy
        option 'macfilter' 'deny'

        # List of authorized mac addresses separated by space
        list 'maclist' 'aa:aa:aa:aa:aa bb:bb:bb:bb:bb'

https://dev.openwrt.org/ticket/2642
http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/uci/wireless

Or with LuCI Administration->Network->WiFi then add additional field "MAC filtering" and of you go.
