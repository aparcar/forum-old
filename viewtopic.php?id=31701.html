There are so many processor/chipset architectures, that there is not "standard version" suitable to all hardware. At least based on the wiki, WNDR3300 is not actively supported by the Openwrt community.

Based on an old Wiki article (http://wiki.openwrt.org/oldwiki/openwrt … r/wndr3300), it is a Broadcom 47xx based device. So, a suitable firmware might be found in http://downloads.openwrt.org/snapshots/trunk/brcm47xx/
The generic one there might be http://downloads.openwrt.org/snapshots/ … uashfs.trx , but more probably trying to flash that might brick your device...

Stick to a firmware that you know should work in the device.
