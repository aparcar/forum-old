I've used ipheth for iPhone 3G/3Gs/4 tethering in OpenWrt. Keep in mind that you also need the userspace software to actually pair and enable tethering. libimobiledevice, usbmuxd, and friends.

I would stay away from any broadcom-based routers. Anything Atheros AR7161 is screaming fast and extremely well supported in OpenWrt.
