Ahm...

1-) Do you want the guest network acessing something on the "private" network?
If not, just create a new wifi network (network, wifi, add), assign it to a "guest" firewall zone (when creating the wifi network, under "interface configuration", "network", checkbox "create" and give it a name... "guest") and configure the firewall (network, firewall) to forward requests from the wifi guest zone to wan (click the "edit" on the guest zone you've just created, under "inter-zone forwarding", "allow forward to destination zones" check "wan")

2-) Running out of addresses?
-what subnet mask you use? 255.255.255.0?
-how many addresses do you need?
