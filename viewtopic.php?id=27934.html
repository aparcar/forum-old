LinkZ
2 Jan 2011, 09:15

I have both and they're both great, powerful routers.  The Gateworks Cambria is better than the Avila.  The Cambria is USB 2.0 ready and has more speed and RAM than the Avila.  However, the RouterStation Pro is the better value in my opinion.  It's more affordable and has gigabit ethernet, whereas the Cambria and Avila don't.  The Cambria is quite expensive.
