According to Wikipedia, difference between wireless "bridge" and "repeater" is that "bridge" connects two different protocol types:

Many wireless routers and wireless access points offer either a "bridge" mode or a "repeater" mode, both of which perform a similar common function, the difference being the bridge mode connects two different protocol types and the repeater mode relays the same protocol type.

What kind of different protocol types? For example IEEE 802.3 and IEEE 802.11?
