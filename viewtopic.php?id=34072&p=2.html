I use a mobile connector cable (USB) for use with Siemens C55 (http://www.preisroboter.de/n/4250465168654.html 5-10Eur). If I'm right it it was aquired from conrad electronic and could be this one: http://www.conrad.de/ce/de/product/7604 … Detail=005. The plug for connect to phone was cut and the 3 lines is that what you want (black: GND, blue: TX, white: RX). The VCC is not nessary because the adapter got its vcc from usb. The chip inside the big usb plug is a oti6858 which works out of the box with modern linux kernels.
