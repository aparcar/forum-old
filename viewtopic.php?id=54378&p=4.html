Sorry for my bad understand

1) Before to upgrade, very important to do restoring Vonets to default, like a hw|soft reset.
2) Make USB-bootable, from downloaded ISO.
3) Power Up router, check what you will see on the WiFi area Vonets_AP.
4) Put RJ-45 to your lan-card and MINI300. (PC<-RJ45->MINI300)
5) Reboot your PC and choice to boot from USB
6) With some patience, will wait for the ending upgrade, make sure what last string will be: Done!
7) Reboot your PC and router.
8) Checking WiFi area. You must see AP MySKU. Pwd is:1234567890, next: admin|admin.
9) Thats all folks! Welcome to latest like a OpenWRT in the russian interface.

Additional, after upgrading, I've making using my HW Programmer a full dump of this is update from chip.MX25L3206E.
