well, first thing, flashing is NOT that simple, there are headers checksums and so on in the image file, and depending on the firmware some parts of the image are NOT copied in flash, some others yes.

so, you always have to use the SUITABLE firmware image with the appropriate flashing method.

from dd-wrt web gui you can ONLY use images that are build for that, so dd-wrt webflash images, or dd-wrt webrevert images. NOT openwrt factory image, NOT sysupgrade, NOT tp-link images (with or w/o uboot....).

i do not know why openwrt factory image was not accepted by tp-link GUI, sometimes you have to rename it to something short, sometimes you have to use Internet Explorer, sometimes try a different O.S, and so on...

now, flashing tp-link firmware over dd-wrt to "revert" to stock could be difficult, try to search the dd-wrt forum for the SPECIFIC webrevert image for your router model AND VERSION.
