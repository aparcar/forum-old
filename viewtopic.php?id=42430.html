5Ghz is much faster and much better suited to bridge use.

While it is theoretically possible to bond a 2.4Ghz and a 5Ghz connection together, the drawbacks often outweigh the benefits in practice, particularly for single-user use.

There is no issue with "overpowering signals" if you used two Locos (I would recommend this option). The transmit power on the units can be adjusted down to 1% in many cases. Even at full power the only way you'll "overload" them is if they were less than 20cm apart, though you will probably see performance degradation at less than 5m unless you turn the power down a little or tilt the units a bit off axis.
