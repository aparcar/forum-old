I am just looking at this unit now. It has 4 GigE ports and a WAN port, in addition to the 2 RJ11 ports for the bonded DSL. It is a BCM6368 unit with a stripped CFE that I was able to use to dump its firmware using cfetool.

One thing that I discovered was that I couldn't login to the unit but it does have 3 basic account types - admin(full access), support (limited access) and locally defined users(read only access to small subset of info).

If you follow the factory reset of the device here:
