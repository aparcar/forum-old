First test that your setup can fully work as a client of an unsecured or PSK AP.  Then you can look at the 802.1x authentication.

Routed client mode is the way to do this.  Replace the normal wired WAN with the wifi client.  Don't have anything else in the WAN network.  Wifi clients will not work in any sort of bridge interface.
