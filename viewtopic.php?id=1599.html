There aren't even any shutdown scripts.. I haven't tried to see if shutdown -h now works.

It should be safe to power down at any point, as long as you don't have an important file open for writing...

As far as stability of experimental, I think you're safe. The devs are currently in the process of thinking of a new name, other than experimental to reflect the true nature of its stability. The older "stable" builds are quickly becoming obselete.

I'm running a v3 and haven't had any problems, though I haven't been using it for any wild stuff, though it looks like you aren't either.
