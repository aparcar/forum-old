This was kind of resolved.  I found a site (http://sr.uz/index.php?p=220&more=1&c=1&tb=1&pb=1 â€” in Russian) with setting suggestions for best performance.

wireless.wifi0=wifi-device
wireless.wifi0.type=atheros
wireless.wifi0.channel=2
wireless.wifi0.disabled=0
wireless.wifi0.diversity=0
wireless.wifi0.txantenna=2
wireless.wifi0.rxantenna=2
wireless.cfg2=wifi-iface
wireless.cfg2.device=wifi0
wireless.cfg2.network=lan
wireless.cfg2.mode=ap
wireless.cfg2.ssid=xxxxxxxxxx
wireless.cfg2.encryption=psk2
wireless.cfg2.key=xxxxxxxxxx
wireless.cfg2.txpower=18
wireless.cfg2.bursting=1
wireless.cfg2.xr=1

These settings seem to work well with 500gP and the Atheros card.  I get much better range and speed now.
