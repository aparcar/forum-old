@fei8th

Read here:

https://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/howto/clientmode

You'll want either fully routed client mode or bridged client with relayd -- either will work with their own pros and cons.  WDS is not implemented on the mwlwifi driver so don't even try to go there.

I have set up both configurations on the WRT1900AC and settled on the routed client due to speed.  Read the wiki closely, if you understand what you're reading and follow step by step it should work -- the page is well written.

You can also search, there are other threads (not related to WRT1900AC) with posted configs for this setup.  Again, ignore anything related to WDS, it will currently not work on this router.  Good luck.
