Hi All,

I have a network running OpenWrt Attitude Adjustment at the router with Routed AP configuration, and at the client with Routed Client configuration. The network worked perfectly fine for about 2 weeks. For the past couple of days, there seems to be a problem with port 80 traffic.

My topology is as follows:

Internet (Public IP) -------- | Openwrt Routed AP (192.168.1.1) | ------WiFi interface---- | OpenWrt Routed Client (192.168.1.6) | ---- LAN interface

The problem:
- Everything works perfectly fine at the Routed AP. I am able to ping www.google.com, wget http://www.google.com just perfectly!
- Problem starts at the OpenWrt Routed Client (192.168.1.6). I can do an nslookup www.google.com . I can ping www.google.com, but wget http://www.google.com is the problem.,.. The page takes too much time to load (order of 10-20 seconds).
- My wireless link seems to be working fine. I ran iperf server on the AP and an iperf client on the STA, and I get good throughputs...

Just to repeat myself - the entire setting was working just fine until a day or two. I am using NAT at the Routed AP to translate from 192.168.1.0/24 to the public IP. Could this cause some problem?

Also, I enabled logging on firewall. It seems like my firewall blocks the first packet during every wget request. Could this be the source of the problem? And if so, how can i fix it? The log for firewall blocking is pasted below.
