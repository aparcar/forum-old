The exact firmware revision is usually printed on the bottom of your router, however in this particular case the firmware images for rev 2 and rev 3 are identical - so you can safely use either:

$ wget -qO- https://downloads.openwrt.org/snapshots/trunk/ar71xx/generic/openwrt-ar71xx-generic-tl-wr941nd-v2-squashfs-factory.bin | sha256sum -
300e7c755147866d220dba00c5a39f959328e34d78631e3385ba9b4a2cfe9eb2  -
$ wget -qO- https://downloads.openwrt.org/snapshots/trunk/ar71xx/generic/openwrt-ar71xx-generic-tl-wr941nd-v3-squashfs-factory.bin | sha256sum -
300e7c755147866d220dba00c5a39f959328e34d78631e3385ba9b4a2cfe9eb2  -
