Most portable/travel routers will do this.  Basically, for a "Router" as opposed to a repeater it's very basic.  The important thing to know is that when you configure the wireless that you have one radio, and that it basically has one config for it's hardware values (1 channel and frequency set in particular)  You may need to change the LAN side to match the WAN side channel in the field.  Search my other posts for more info.  Set the PI IP as something other than your home IP, connect via wireless to your PC (LAN Side) on the same channel as your home router wireless.  Do a wifi scan for your home wireless. 

Depending on your radio it may not support more than 1 SSID (ie not both sides at the same time). 

See these 2 posts and you will probably find what you need to config
https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=59101
https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=53014
