I actually found some cheap suppliers of 110/220 volt adapter for the WRT54G  in the US.
1. JWIN  www..ejwin.com  (look under Accessories/Adapters) has the JAC-82 for sale for $4.88  + shipping. The units come with a variety of interchangeable plugs  (the 2.1mm and 2.5mm ones work fine with the wrt54g) and a number of voltages including the required 12 volts.   The unit is heavy - more than a pound -  and they are unregulated - so don't use it with other hardware which may require regulated supplies.  You will need a Euro plug adapter - but you could find those in Indian or Oriental supermarkets for less than  $1
2. Netgate www.netgate.com  has a switching power supply 12 volts - 1 Amp with a 2.1mm connector and even a Euro plug. Currently (Jan 30, 2008) this is on special sale for $8.00 - marked down from $15.50  They have other regular priced units for $12.50.
