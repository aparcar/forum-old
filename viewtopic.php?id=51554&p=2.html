drawz wrote:

Given that the TFTP recovery is provided by TP-Link, the firmware they put out for download should work for this

please have a look at this:

https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=46371

Since it's an undocumented feature, you may or MAY NOT find a suitable (uboot free!) firmware in download page, given also the fact that download page is often updated, and older firmwares are removed from the list.

long story short, you have to check file size, and if it has uboot, you must trim it by yourself

It's not possible that uboot has been touched by a normal flashing procedure, for sure not with a tftp recovery mode.

I have no idea why the router is not accepting the firmware, but you can recover it for sure if you connect a TTL serial interface (also called UART, make sure it's 3.3V max!) to the board
