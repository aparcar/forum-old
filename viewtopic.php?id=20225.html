Well, on WRT54g, 2.4 or 2.6, there are some dependency.

Wireless speed and reliability, 2.4 > 2.6 on trunk > 2.6 on 8.09 branch.
Qos-scripts is not selectable in 2.4 trunk(as I last check, only for 2.6).
nvram tools now also works for 2.6 on trunk, i am not sure if it has been ported back to 8.09 or not.

So up to now, 2.4 on 8.09 is my first choice. As you can see, things are moving to 2.6, some day when b43 turns out to be reliable enough for daily usage, then that's time to say byebye to 2.4, hopefully.
