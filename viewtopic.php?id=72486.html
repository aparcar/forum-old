The Netgear WNR1000 v3 is not compatible with OpenWrt, it's a Broadcom SOC with 4 MB flash and only 16 MB RAM - the later is a total deal breaker (absolute minimum 32 MB RAM, very much needed >= 64 MB RAM), the wlan card doesn't appear to be supported either.

If you're looking for a well supported device for LEDE/ OpenWrt, please do take these minimum system requirements into account and prefer one of the common SOCs with good wlan support (ar71xx, lantiq (VDSL support, but wlan support varies between devices), mt7621, ipq40xx, ipq806x, mvebu).
