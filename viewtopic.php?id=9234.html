To open TCP ports 22 and 80, add this to the relevant section of your /etc/firewall.user (there's some examples at the bottom too).

# open port 22 (ssh) to wan
iptables -t nat -A prerouting_rule -i $WAN -p tcp --dport 22 -j ACCEPT
iptables        -A input_rule      -i $WAN -p tcp --dport 22 -j ACCEPT

# open port 80 (http) to wan
iptables -t nat -A prerouting_rule -i $WAN -p tcp --dport 80 -j ACCEPT
iptables        -A input_rule      -i $WAN -p tcp --dport 80 -j ACCEPT
