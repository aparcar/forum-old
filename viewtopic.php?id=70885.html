Hi,

first i am located on the Greek part of the Island Cyprus and i am running Chaos Calmer on TP-Link N600 WDR3600 V1.4 behind a Bridged ZXDSL 931VII, naturally my ISP is Cyta.com.cy
My local clients get there IP's via DHCP from openwrt.
I followed the unbound wiki HOWTO and everything seemed to work nice, tests with dnsleak pages showed me that i was using my custom DNS.

Recently my ISP had some cables in my area wired wrong and the ISP modem kept disconnecting and reconnecting. This was quickly fixed by their support.

Now what also happened during that time.
My custom DNS entry's were ignored and i was using my ISP entries, Luci's pppoe-wan Interface shows my custom DNS entry's which are not used.

For now i defaulted back to using manual DNS entries in Windows 10.

What am i doing wrong? Why do i loose my custom DNS entries when the bridged ISP Modem disconnects?

Post #2

Configure your WAN interface to ignore the DNSs from the ISP, and configure DNSMASQ to use your DNSs.
