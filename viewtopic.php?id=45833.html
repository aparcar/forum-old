List of 802.11ad hardware: http://wikidevi.com/wiki/List_of_802.11ad_Hardware

The Qualcomm Atheros QCA9005 contains a 802.11ad adapter. I think you have to ask Qualcomm for an actual sample. It's also used in the Dell Wireless 1601 ($59.00), might be easier to get hold of.

HiperLAN has nothing to do with 802.11ad, though some of it's ideas might be reused in modern WiFi standards. In the end it was more of a research project.
