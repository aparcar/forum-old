I notice that there are 2 "passwords" stored on the system, maybe they got out of sync, due to some problem / crash / whatever? There are passwords stored in /etc/passwd and /etc/httpd.conf. In my case the passwords in these files match exactly. If they don't do in your case, just copy the password-hash from /etc/passwd (used by login) to /etc/httpd.conf (probably used by webif).
