You mainly need to populate R9, R10, R13, R14, D11, C95 and C108 to have the data lines available on the header J5. The rest of the circuits around there is for the 5V power supply (might be controllable by a GPIO).
R9 and R10 should be 22 Ohms
R13 and R14 should be 15 kOhms
On the software side, there are a few changes necessary, similar to what I mentioned on this thread. Just instead of the TP-Link sections and files, it has to be done with the WPN824N's.
