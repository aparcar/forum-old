I have had a similar problem.  My MacBookPro thinks it associated with the AP, but no packets.

Each time this has happened, I see in log read | grep hosted | grep  XX:XX:15

Feb 20 00:01:07 office daemon.info hostapd: wlan0: STA XX:XX:XX:XX:XX:15 IEEE 802.11: disassociated
Feb 20 00:01:08 office daemon.info hostapd: wlan0: STA XX:XX:XX:XX:XX:15 IEEE 802.11: deauthenticated due to inactivity (timer DEAUTH/REMO

Some times shutting down wifi on the laptop fixes the problem.   So right now I have a scheduled task that shuts down wifi on the WNDR3700v2, and then restarts it every hour.

This is a pain, but it keeps my network up. :-(
