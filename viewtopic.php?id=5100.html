Why not buy the ASUS WL-500G Deluxe.

It is priced at appx the same level as the Linksys WRT54GL, and includes the same CPU and 32 MB of RAM instead of 16MB.

Additionally it includes 2xUSB 2.0 interfaces, allowing you to attach external storage, printers and webcams.
