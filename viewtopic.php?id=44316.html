Hi Beeblebrox,

For routers with PCI, Mini-PCI or Mini-PCIe expansion slots you can plug in FXO/FXS cards.
For example...
Alix
Geos
Soekris etc.

Of course I would advise you to use hardware that is fully open source supported (no binary blobs)
