Some abbreviations explained:

  • WISP: Wireless Internet Service Provider

  • WISPr: Wireless Internet Service Provider roaming

  • UAM:   Universal Access Method

WISP is supported by default by OpenWrt, see the wiki article under the section, Routed Client Mode Just your basic masqing an wifi client connection.

WISPr is a protocol used by (most) captive portals and it works with a small xml part within the webpage.
see http://wiki.maemo.org/WISPr_Wireless_ISP_Autologin

UAM is just the backend of the WISPr protocol, for what you are doing you don't need to wory about it.
