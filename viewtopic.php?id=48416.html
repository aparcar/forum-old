I think there's no WR842N. All 842s I run into had detachable antennas, at least in here. The only difference between "n" a "nd" versions across tp link products is the possibility of having the antenna detached, so yeas, the should be the same. But you have to watch the hw version.

You can flash that image as long as you have V2 of your router and you have that international version. All 842s I have seen were V1, because they have been on the market for at least 2 years, but I heard V2 is out too.

edit: I took a look on that product you linked, which is probably sold in China only and it really might have different configuration, so it is possible they are not the same. That chinese 842n have even V4 and is not available in here via official channels.
