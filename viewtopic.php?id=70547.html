The actual net download speed is always considerably slower than the raw radio bit rate.  HT40 in the 2.4 band does not start up unless your client device supports it, and the extra channel is clear of any neighboring wifi signals.  If either of those are not true, it falls back to HT20.  You can check the main status page to see what speed and bandwidth your clients have connected at.
