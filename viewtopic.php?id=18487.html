After getting less than 500 kbps download speeds with 2 different Novatel USB EVDO Rev A modems (USB720 for Verizon and U727 for Sprint) - I found a posting from Novatel that the generic usbserial driver is limited to 500 kbps.
Also found that airprime.c was missing in the 2.6.27 linux distribution (used in the Kamikaze r14035 snapshot of early Jan)   and that the linux developers had left it out and stated that option.c would be a substitute.
With option  (available as a kernel module on downloads.openwrt.org) I am now seeing download speeds of 1 Mbps
