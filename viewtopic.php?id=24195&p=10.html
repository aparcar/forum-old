Since my last post I have moved over to LEDE on my DGN3500.  It was absolutely stunning.  The only reason it ever went down was power utility power outs long enough to drop the UPS.  Ran until 2017 November, at which time I moved house and got fiber - but my trusty LEDE DGN3500 came out when I could not get the Tenda router that was here when I moved in up and going, and it got me up and running on fiber!

Later I got VOX to give me the promised wireless router - turned out to be a Mikrotik, that looks quite powerful.  But they did not immediately hand over admin privileges on the Mikrotik, so back out came the DGN3500!  And I'm working on it right now!

The reason for me posting here, and not on the LEDE forum...  I see LEDE is merged with OpenWRT now.  So, has anyone moved to an OpenWRT system past CC 15.05 yet on the DGN3500?
