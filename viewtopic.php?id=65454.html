Just to help those that may need it:

cat /proc/sys/net/ipv4/tcp_available_congestion_control - the ones that you can use

to see, which control is used,

cat /proc/sys/net/ipv4/tcp_congestion_control

to change you can echo or you can use sysctl
sysctl -w net.ipv4.tcp_congestion_control = {reno,cubic,westwood,yeah}
