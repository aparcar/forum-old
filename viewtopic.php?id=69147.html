I have three routers.  (1) A Netgear wndr3700 (2) a Linsys Wrt54GS and the (3) Asus Rt-N56U.  The wndr3700 is my main router.  It does dhcp, controls two guest networks (Adult and kids), does access restrictions, and runs a VPN, all very successfully.  Within the last week, I made up my mind to stop having ump-teen wireless SSIDs in my house and start meshing them so people could roam through out the house and get the best WiFi signal.  Pretty easy with DD-WRT.  I am having a tough time with OpenWrt, mostly because it is new. update:

I have figured out a couple things:

(1) I upgraded to Chaos Calmer 15.05
(2) My 802.11an radio successfully broadcasts a 5g-adult and 5g-kids wireless signal
(3) my 802.11bgn radio successfully broadcasts an adult and kids wireless signal
(4) I have successfully bridged the 5g kids and kids wireless signal to Br-br1
(5) I have successfully bridged the 5g adult and adult wireless signal to Br-br2
(6) all four access points seem to give the proper IP address (Adult 192.168.3.100-105 and Kids 192.168.2.100-105) but I am not sure the IPs are coming from my main WNDR 3700 and the devices take forever to connect and get an IP.  Wouldn't surprise me if the Asus N56U is assigning the IPs but I don't know how to check.
