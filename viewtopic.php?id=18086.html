Output from ifconfig, iwconfig, and "dmesg | grep b43" are listed below.

I'm out of ideas.  Any suggestions?

The good news is that I did get an AT&T USBConnect Mercury 3G modem (aka Sierra Compass 885) working on it.  That's why I needed to use 8.09rc1 with a 2.6 kernel.  But I wanted to route from 3G to Wifi.  If I can't get the Wifi working in 8.09rc1 with the 2.6 kernel, I may have to use *two* routers, one from 3G to 100baseT, and one running 7.09 from 100baseT to Wifi.  Obviously I'd rather do it with a single router.
