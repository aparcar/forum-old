Bridged networks need  relayd pkg installed to repeat a signal, from another AP or router.
MODE 2: REPEATER, DIFFERENT NETWORK has to be in a dif subnet, if the open AP you are connecting uses Gateway 192.168.1.0 your WDR3500 has to be in 192.168.0.1
Read Repeater configurations here! Both ways, bridged and simple repeater
https://forum.openwrt.org/viewtopic.php?id=39077
