right, talk about jumping in at the deep end.... what you are trying to do is no trivial matter.
and really, i would invest my time in trying to get a VPN w/ TAP to work rather than manually sorting this out (especially since you are new to 'networking and linux')

that being said, I do NOT have solution for you, but this might point you in the right direction...
the problem starts with SSDP, which uses a broadcast (239.255.255.250 for ip4) which isn't/shouldn't be routed so your 'magic' only gets you so far.
you could map this to a mac to an ip which is routable which is remapped to a broadcast whose response are remapped ... really do try to get TAP to work for you.

but if you do solve it, i'm very curious for your solution - please post back here when you do.
