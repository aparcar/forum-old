I just watched this video and I'm still amazed!
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ipdXKPUVOVE

From about this video:
"I built an USB videocard, based on STM32F103VE microcontroller and QVGA LCD module. Then I wrote Linux framebuffer driver for it, recompiled OpenWRT and installed new image on router. Last step was cross-compiling ScummVM and patching Gobliins engine to run without soundcard.
This video shows ScummVM under OpenWRT on TL-MR3020, running Gobliins, classic 1992 quest."

Pure awesomness!
