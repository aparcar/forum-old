Don't be confused.

LAN normally means the wired ethernet ports.
WAN means the wired "Internet" ethernet port.

No Wifi means you cannot use Internet with your wireless devices.
Internet with wired devices connected to LAN should work.

Your Hardware is Broadcom according to wikidevi. Broadcom situation is complicated : many older devices work but newer hardware often does not because Broadcoms management or lawyers are not green lighting release of specifications (to write drivers from scratch or extend the existing free ones) or drivers (they release some drivers but often not for those inside routers).
