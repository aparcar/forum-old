I have finally been successful in setting up my routers as to my needs. I have one gateway router WR512-3GN feeding from a local LAN with ADSL and a connected 3G stick as an fail-over when power/ADSL fails. Inside my own UPS powered subnet I have the 703N with extroot and 32GB of flash storage that is running Samba, Transmission and OpenVPN. When power and/or ADSL fails I have at least 6 hours of uptime/connectivity which is sufficient for the local conditions (suburb Jakarta, Indonesia). Thanks everyone for helping out!
