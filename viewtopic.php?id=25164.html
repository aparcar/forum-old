Hi,
After many years my wrt54g v2 is dying. The switch sometimes locks up making the router non-responsive for a few seconds, and lately the router is resetting itself every now and then. This sucks because I also had some mods (sd card, serial port ->microcontroller->temperature sensors) and lots of hacks in the software too, not to mention it's one of the rare units with 32mb ram instead of 16mb. I'm running it with the case open and i've put a heatsink on the switch, I think it's always been too hot, but the lockups are worse every day anyway.

Anyway, I think the only solution is to buy a new router, and I could use some of your help for that,
what do you suggest taking into account these constraints?
- Serial port accesible
- at least 32mb ram
- a decent cpu (>=200mhz)
- preferably, a chipset that doesn't turn the router into a toaster (most routers are always too hot and eventually break).
- Accesible GPIO lines for SD card mod
- USB would be nice to have but not at all necessary
- Wireless is NOT necessary
- an ethernet switch is necessary (so no single-port devices, i want to use it as a router)
- cheaper=better
