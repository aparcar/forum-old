Hi!

I have a wndr3700 v1 running Backfire 10.03.1-rc5.

Regularly, but after a random amount of time (usually around 1-3 hours), all wifi devices connected to the router lose their connection, at the same time. After a few minutes they are able to reconnect again.

I love OpenWRT, but this is very annoying as there are some things in my home that stop working when the wifi drops out.

How should I go about troubleshooting this? When it happens, hostapd is still running and everything looks normal. I don't see anything fishy in the syslog, and dmesg looks ok.

The router has two configured SSIDs on two different subnets running off the same radio at 2.4GHz in g mode. The 5GHz radio is not used currently. When it happens, all devices get disconnected regardless of which SSID they use.

Wired connections are not affected at all, they are rock stable.

Ideas, anyone?
