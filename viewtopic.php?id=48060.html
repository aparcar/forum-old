Dunno I don't think it is the same bug, but you could hook it up to a ttl-> usb converter and poke around that way.
At least they have the same pinout and procedure for other tp-link devices.
close up of the ttl header (some soldering required).
http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/tp-link/tl- … al.console (use the v8 description where applicable).
Ebay search for some adapters for an idea
Make sure that the output of the adapter is 3.3v on the data pins and don't connect v+/vcc and you are good to go (if you have a ttl device you have 3.3v)
