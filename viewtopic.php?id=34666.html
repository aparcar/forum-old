Your mileage may vary, but I have only been successful in using the TFTP process for installing firmware on the Buffalo WZR-HP-G300NH that I have.  I used the process documented at http://wiki.openwrt.org/toh/buffalo/wzr â€¦ ftp.method with one minor exception.  I had to put a static arp entry in for the MAC address recorded on my device rather than the "02:aa:bb:cc:dd:20" mentioned in the instructions.  On all other counts, the document worked perfectly.
