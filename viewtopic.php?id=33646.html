do you have any special performance needs? I guess nfs will be a litte fast than e.g. samba, but don't expect to much.
you can get about 8-10MByte/sec with any of this cheap router with atheros-cpus (400MHz) tl-wr703n, tl-mr3220 tl-mr3420 but there are many other quite cheap devices.
If you need more you can get a router with gigabit and 680MHz you will get 10-15MByte/sec.

if you need a faster nas you will need the dockstar, sheevaplug, pogoplug or an atom-board.  or actually a nas (with a fast cpu) ... http://www.nas-central.org/wiki/Main_Page there are many nas-boxes you can run debian or other stuff on it.
