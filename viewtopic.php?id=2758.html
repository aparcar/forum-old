Hello blackdir, the actual hardware inside the WRT54G is the same no matter what sort it is (-US -UK -EU -DE). The only differences in hardware are when you change version, like WRT54G v1, v2, v3, v4 etc. The -EU just means it has the european firmware loaded, -DE just means the firmware is in german but the hardware can take any firmware so you could load -UK firmware onto the -DE.

In short yes openWRT should work on a WRT54G-EU as long as the hardware is correct, which it should be.
