So far I have encountered no saboteurs, who would wrongly claim a device is supported, which in fact is not.

The opposite is true: support for new or old devices is achieved but then nobody bothers to document this in the wiki. So, if a device is reported as supported in old OR in nuwiki, then it is most probably so. And if a device/version is not reported in the wiki, the device still could be supported :-(

The reason we have an oldwiki is a port from pmwiki? to dokuwiki. Not all content has been ported yet to nuwiki, because nobody thinks it's worth the bother.
