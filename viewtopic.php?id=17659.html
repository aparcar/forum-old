question is ..... how can i test the watchdog for having sure it will reload the device in case of any problems ?? is this kind of test even possible ?

killall -9 watchdog

yeah .... it works

Nov 12 14:59:49 netflowbox01 user.crit kernel: ar71xx-wdt: device closed unexpectedly, watchdog timer will not stop!

and some few seconds latter, RB450 rebooted.
