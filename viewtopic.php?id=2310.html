17 Aug 2005, 01:02
carlopires wrote:

I found nothing about wrc1000 in toshiba site. Have you a link to this official firmware ?
Also, as I erased nvram, boot_wait is off, how can I reflash it again ?

Goto:
http://www.csd.toshiba.com/cgi-bin/tais … oid=311393

Click on Downloads.. and in the search box, type in "wrc".. 2 firmware versions should show up.  As far as the boot_wait, I believe it's on even if you erased the nvram.. but the  IP you need to TFTP to is 192.168.1.1 (not 192.168.10.1).  If that doesn't work, you can always find the flash chip and short out pins 15 and 16.. the docs are on here somewhere.. again, without the m_lan_ipaddr being set,  the IP of the WRC will default to 192.168.1.1.

Well, I've erased my NVRAM on a few occassions and boot_wait has always been on.  The window to tftp is small.. probably around 2-3 seconds and it's a second or two after the lan ports stop blinking.  I have shorted pins 15 & 16 on the flash chip once, just to see if it would work.. and the WRC waited until I tftp'ed a new firmware.  Make sure you set the IP of the computer you're flashing from to a 192.168.1.* address..

I am trying to flash an openwrt image to a wrc-1000 using the default web interface but I get an Undefined Error after the flash upgrade procedure and after that the unit reboots with the default firmware.  I am trying to use one of these images: http://wifi.ozo.com/airo/asus/firmware/ … uashfs.trx or http://wifi.ozo.com/airo/asus/firmware/ … s2-4MB.trx which actually work quite well on asus wl500g

any hints will be much appreciated!

Edit: Replying to my self,

I was able to flash a custom openwrt image by simply following the directions from the wiki:

tftp 192.168.10.1
tftp> binary
tftp> trace
tftp> rexmt 1
tftp> timeout 60
tftp> put openwrt-xxx-x.x-xxx.trx

You need to use the 192.168.10.1 IP and make sure you DO NOT erase the nvram when you login to openwrt OS- you've been warned :-).  I've test the unit with a senao, atheros and a cisco mpi350 and all three miniPCI wifi cards work quite well !!
