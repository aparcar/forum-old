andy

johnk wrote:

I'm another Niko fan.  The red ball guy is neat!

I'd like to see more submissions from him, actually.  The red ball is quite good, if that's what the team wants to portray with their logo (it's lighthearted and cute).  However, if I were making decisions for my project, I would call for changes on the little ball guy so that he could stand alone.  As it is, the 'O' and the 'P' for his face make sense, but only when next to the text.  Logos have to be able to stand alone apart from any text unless it's very specifically part of the structure of the logo (like SUN's logo, for instance), and including all of that text on -say- a business card, would be a little awkward.

If you put the little ball guy on his own, it just says "OP", which doesn't make a whole lot of sense.  If it was something like "OR" or even just "O", it might.  The shark, on the other hand, while a little cutesy for me personally, stands alone perfectly and would be a combination of a logo AND a mascot.

I need to jump back into this game soon.  These kids are making me look bad...
