eduperez
15 Jul 2016, 10:51

First of all, it acts as a rock-solid router for my network (something I cannot say about other devices I have tried before), with some "premium" features (not available on cheaper devices) such as DDNS, IPv6-on-IPV4 tunnel, QOS and traffic statistics, a guest LAN, or several isolated WLANs.

This device is also a VPN server that lets me reach my network from anywhere, acts as a MQTT server form my IoT experiments, and a VoIP server to make and receive calls using my landline (even from outside my network, using the VPN); I also use it to detect electricity cuts when I am not at home.
